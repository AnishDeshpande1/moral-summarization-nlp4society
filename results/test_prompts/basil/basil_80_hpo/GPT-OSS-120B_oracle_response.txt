SUMMARY: President Donald Trump renewed his assault on the Justice Department, labeling it a “deep state” institution and demanding that former Clinton aide Huma Abedin be jailed. Trump’s early‑morning tweet came after the State Department released emails Abedin had allegedly **forwarded** to a personal account, some containing passwords, as part of the FBI’s 2016 probe into Hillary Clinton’s private‑email server. Trump urged the Justice Department to act on “**Comey** and others,” referencing the former FBI director who was investigating whether Trump’s campaign **colluded** with Russia to **influence** the 2016 election. The president has repeatedly tried to **direct** the Justice Department and FBI, claiming he has the “absolute right” to **do** whatever he wishes with those agencies, even as he criticizes Attorney General Jeff Sessions for recusing himself from the Russia investigation. This latest tweet reflects Trump’s ongoing effort to undermine the Justice Department while casting political opponents as criminal conspirators.
