SUMMARY:

President Trump has once again attacked the Justice Department, labeling it as the "deep state" and calling for the jailing of top Clinton aide Huma Abedin. This comes after the release of Abedin's emails, which showed she forwarded State Department emails, including some with passwords, to her personal account. Trump's tweet also targeted former FBI Director James Comey, whom he fired in May. This is not the first time Trump has sought to undermine the Justice Department and FBI, as he has repeatedly criticized Attorney General Jeff Sessions for recusing himself from the Russia investigation. Trump's actions and words raise serious concerns about his respect for the rule of law and the independence of the Justice Department. His attempts to direct government investigations himself and his claims of having the "absolute right" to do so are particularly troubling. Trump's behavior is a clear example of an abuse of power and a threat to the democratic institutions of the United States.

END OF SUMMARY.
