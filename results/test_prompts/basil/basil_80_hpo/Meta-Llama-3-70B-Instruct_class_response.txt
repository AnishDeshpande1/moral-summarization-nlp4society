SUMMARY:

President Trump has once again attacked the Justice Department, labeling it as the "deep state" and calling for the jailing of top Clinton aide Huma Abedin. This comes after the release of Abedin's emails, which showed she forwarded State Department emails, including some with passwords, to her personal account. Trump has repeatedly sought to undermine the Justice Department and FBI as the Russia probe has intensified, criticizing Attorney General Jeff Sessions for recusing himself from the investigation. Trump has claimed he has the "absolute right" to direct government investigations himself, sparking concerns about his attempt to collude with the Justice Department. The President's actions have raised eyebrows, with many seeing his efforts as an attempt to obstruct the investigation into whether his campaign colluded with Russia to influence the election.

END OF SUMMARY.
