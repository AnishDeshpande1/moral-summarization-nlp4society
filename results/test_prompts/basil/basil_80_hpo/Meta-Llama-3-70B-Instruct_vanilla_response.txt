SUMMARY:

President Donald Trump has renewed his attack on the Justice Department, labeling it as the "deep state". He also called for the jailing of Huma Abedin, a top aide to Hillary Clinton, over emails released by the State Department. The emails, uncovered during the 2016 presidential campaign, showed Abedin forwarding State Department emails, including some with passwords, to a personal account. Trump also referenced former FBI Director James Comey, saying the Justice Department must act on him and others. Trump has repeatedly sought to undermine the Justice Department and FBI as the Russia probe has intensified, criticizing Attorney General Jeff Sessions for recusing himself from the investigation. Trump has claimed he has the right to direct government investigations himself, stating he has the "absolute right to do what I want to do with the Justice Department".

END OF SUMMARY.
