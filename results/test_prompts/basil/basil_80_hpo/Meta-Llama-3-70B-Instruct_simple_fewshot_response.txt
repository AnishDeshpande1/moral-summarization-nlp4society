SUMMARY:

President Donald Trump has launched a fresh attack on the Justice Department, labeling it as the "deep state" and calling for the jailing of top Hillary Clinton aide Huma Abedin. Trump's tweets were sparked by the release of Abedin's emails, which were uncovered during the FBI's probe into Clinton's use of a private email server. Trump also demanded action against former FBI Director James Comey, whom he fired in May. This is not the first time Trump has sought to undermine the Justice Department and FBI, repeatedly criticizing Attorney General Jeff Sessions and claiming he has the right to direct government investigations himself. Trump's actions have raised concerns about the politicization of the Justice Department and the erosion of public trust in institutions. The moral framing of this article suggests that Trump's behavior is an affront to the independence and integrity of the Justice Department, and that his actions are a threat to the rule of law.

END OF SUMMARY.
