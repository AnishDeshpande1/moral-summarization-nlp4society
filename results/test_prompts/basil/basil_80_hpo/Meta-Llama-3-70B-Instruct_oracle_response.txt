SUMMARY:

President Trump launched a fresh attack on the Justice Department, labeling it a "deep state" and calling for the imprisonment of top Hillary Clinton aide Huma Abedin. Trump's early-morning tweet targeted Abedin over emails released by the State Department, which showed she had forwarded sensitive State Department emails, including some with passwords, to her personal account. Trump also demanded action against "Comey and others," referencing former FBI Director James Comey, who was leading the Russia probe until Trump fired him. Trump has repeatedly sought to undermine the Justice Department and FBI as the Russia investigation has intensified, claiming he has the "absolute right" to direct government investigations himself. This is not the first time Trump has attempted to exert influence over the Justice Department, having criticized Attorney General Jeff Sessions for recusing himself from the Russia investigation.

END OF SUMMARY.
