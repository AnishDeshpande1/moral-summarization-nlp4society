SUMMARY:

House Speaker Nancy Pelosi defended Michigan Rep. Rashida Tlaib's vulgar comment about impeaching President Donald Trump, calling it a free speech issue. Tlaib, one of the first Muslim women in Congress, said "we're gonna impeach the motherfucker" during an event hosted by MoveOn. Pelosi, in an interview with MSNBC's Joy Reid, said she doesn't like the language but wouldn't censor her colleagues. She also noted that Trump has made similar comments in the past, such as bragging about grabbing women and referring to certain countries as "shithole countries." Pelosi believes Tlaib's comment might fire up Trump's base but doesn't affect actual House business. Trump responded on Twitter, claiming Democrats only want to impeach him because they know they can't win in 2020.

END OF SUMMARY.
