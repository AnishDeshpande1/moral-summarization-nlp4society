SUMMARY:

House Speaker Nancy Pelosi has defended Michigan Rep. Rashida Tlaib's vulgar comment about impeaching President Donald Trump, saying it's a free speech issue. Pelosi noted that while she doesn't like the language used, she wouldn't censor her colleagues and pointed out that Trump has made similar offensive comments in the past. Tlaib's comment, in which she vowed to "impeach that motherfucker," was made at an event hosted by the progressive group MoveOn. Pelosi downplayed the controversy, saying it wouldn't affect actual House business and was simply a matter of individual freedom of speech. Trump responded to Tlaib's comment on Twitter, claiming that Democrats only want to impeach him because they know they can't win in 2020. The incident highlights the tense and divisive political atmosphere in Washington.

END OF SUMMARY.
