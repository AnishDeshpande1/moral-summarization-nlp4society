SUMMARY: 
Texas Governor Rick Perry is launching a new TV ad, titled "Strong", in which he claims that President Obama is waging a "War on Religion" and attacks the President's stance on social issues. Perry, a self-proclaimed Christian, argues that the country is going wrong, citing examples such as gays serving openly in the military while kids can't openly celebrate Christmas or pray in school. He vows to "fight against liberal attacks on our religious heritage" and promises to "end Obama's war on religion" as President. Perry's campaign has been focusing on gay marriage and social issues, accusing Obama of being out of touch with America's values. The ad ends with Perry stating that "faith made America strong. It can make her strong again." Perry's message is clear: he will defend faith in America and promote traditional American values. 
END OF SUMMARY.
