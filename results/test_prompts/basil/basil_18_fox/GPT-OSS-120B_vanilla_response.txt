SUMMARY: Texas Governor Rick Perry has launched a new TV ad titled “Strong,” accusing President Obama of waging a “War on Religion.” The ad, airing statewide in Iowa, portrays Perry as a proud Christian who will protect religious freedom and traditional values if elected. He criticizes Obama’s support for gay rights, including allowing openly gay service members, the Justice Department’s stance on the Defense of Marriage Act, and a White House memo directing foreign aid to promote LGBT rights. Perry also references Obama’s omission of God from a Thanksgiving speech and frames these issues as attacks on America’s religious heritage. The campaign emphasizes Perry’s Methodist faith and positions him as the GOP candidate who will restore faith‑based strength to the nation. END OF SUMMARY.
