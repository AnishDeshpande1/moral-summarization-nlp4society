SUMMARY:

Texas Governor Rick Perry has released a new TV ad, "Strong," in which he claims that President Obama is waging a "War on Religion" and vows to defend faith in America as president. Perry argues that Obama's administration is promoting gay rights over traditional American values, citing examples such as the presidential memorandum directing agencies to promote and protect the rights of gays and lesbians abroad. Perry frames this as an attack on people of faith, saying that investing tax dollars in promoting a "lifestyle many Americans find so deeply objectionable" is wrong. The ad appeals to social conservatives and evangelical voters, emphasizing Perry's commitment to defending their values. By portraying Obama's actions as a threat to traditional American values and faith, Perry seeks to mobilize his base and attack the president on issues of morality.

END OF SUMMARY.
