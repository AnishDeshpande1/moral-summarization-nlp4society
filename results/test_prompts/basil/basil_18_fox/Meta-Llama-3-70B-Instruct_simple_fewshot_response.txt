SUMMARY:

Texas Governor Rick Perry has released a new TV ad claiming that President Obama is waging a "War on Religion" and vowing to defend faith in America. Perry's ad contrasts Christianity with homosexuality, arguing that it's wrong that gays can serve openly in the military while children can't openly celebrate Christmas or pray in school. The ad is part of Perry's multimillion-dollar television budget, targeting Iowa's Christian evangelicals. Perry's message is a thinly veiled attack on the LGBTQ+ community, implying that their rights are incompatible with religious freedom. This kind of rhetoric is harmful and divisive, perpetuating a false narrative that pits religious beliefs against individual liberties. By using faith as a political tool, Perry is undermining the values of empathy, kindness, and respect that are essential to a functioning democracy.

END OF SUMMARY.
