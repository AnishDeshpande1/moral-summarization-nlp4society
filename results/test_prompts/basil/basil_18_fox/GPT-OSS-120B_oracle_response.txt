SUMMARY: Texas Gov. Rick Perry has launched a new TV ad accusing President Obama of waging a “War on Religion.” Perry, a self‑described “man of faith,” says he will fight Obama’s “attacks” on America’s religious heritage and will end the “war” against traditional values. The ad, titled “Strong,” argues that it is wrong for the administration to allow gays to serve openly while limiting Christmas celebrations and school prayers, and it condemns a presidential memorandum that would promote and protect LGBTQ rights in foreign aid. Perry claims faith made America strong and promises to make the nation strong again by defending that faith against liberal “attacks.” END OF SUMMARY.
