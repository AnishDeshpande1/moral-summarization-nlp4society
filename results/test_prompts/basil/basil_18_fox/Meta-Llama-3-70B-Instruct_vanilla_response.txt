SUMMARY:

Texas Governor Rick Perry has released a new TV ad claiming that President Obama is waging a "War on Religion" in America. In the ad, titled "Strong," Perry asserts that he is the GOP candidate who can defend faith in America. He ties his Christian faith to his conservative stands on social issues, such as gay marriage and prayer in school. Perry's campaign has been focusing on these issues, citing examples of Obama's alleged attacks on traditional American values, including directing the Justice Department to stop defending the Defense of Marriage Act and promoting gay rights through foreign aid. Perry, a Methodist, has never shied away from his faith during the campaign and is appealing to evangelical voters. The ad ends with Perry stating that "faith made America strong. It can make her strong again."

END OF SUMMARY.
