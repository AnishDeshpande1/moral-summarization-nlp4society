**SUMMARY:**  
In a new statewide TV ad titled “Strong,” Texas Gov. Rick Perry attacks President Obama as waging a “war on religion,” positioning himself as the GOP candidate who will defend America’s Christian faith and traditional values. Perry, a Methodist, condemns what he calls liberal attacks on the nation’s religious heritage, citing issues such as gay marriage, the presence of openly gay service members, and the removal of Christmas celebrations and school prayer. He denounces Obama’s foreign‑aid memorandum that promotes gay rights, labeling it another example of an administration “out of touch” with America’s values. Perry promises that, as president, he will end this perceived war on faith and restore the nation’s strength through “faith‑made” America.
