SUMMARY:
Iowa Democratic Rep. Bruce Braley apologized after a video released by the conservative super‑PAC America Rising showed him mocking Sen. Chuck Grassley as “a farmer from Iowa who never went to law school.” Braley said he “respects” Grassley and expressed his “appreciation” for Iowa’s farmers, noting he is “grateful” for their “support.” In the fundraiser remarks, Braley argued that electing him would place a trial lawyer on the Senate Judiciary Committee, whereas Grassley—by Braley’s logic—might become chair despite being “a farmer from Iowa who never went to law school.” Grassley’s office responded that Braley’s logic would also bar a trial lawyer from speaking on agriculture, energy, or health‑care issues, and highlighted Grassley’s “strong record” and his status as one of only two working family farmers in the Senate who “works” on a wide range of policies. Both sides emphasized “respect,” “support,” and “help” in their statements.  
END OF SUMMARY.
