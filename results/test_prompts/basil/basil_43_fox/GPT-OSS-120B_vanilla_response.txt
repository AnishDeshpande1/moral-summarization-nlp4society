SUMMARY: Iowa Democratic Representative Bruce Braley faced criticism after a video released by the conservative super PAC America Rising showed him mocking Senator Chuck Grassley as “a farmer from Iowa who never went to law school.” The remarks were made at a Texas fundraiser while Braley was campaigning for the U.S. Senate seat being vacated by retiring Democrat Tom Harkin. Braley apologized, stating he respects Grassley and supports Iowa farmers. He argued that electing him would place a seasoned trial lawyer on the Senate Judiciary Committee, contrasting this with the idea of a non‑lawyer farmer chairing the committee if Republicans gain control. Grassley’s office responded, saying Braley’s logic would disqualify the senator from speaking on many issues and highlighted Grassley’s record as one of only two working family farmers in the Senate, emphasizing his contributions to agriculture and other policy areas. END OF SUMMARY.
