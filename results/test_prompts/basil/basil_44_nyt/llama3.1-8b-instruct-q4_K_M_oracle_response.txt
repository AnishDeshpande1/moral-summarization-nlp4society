SUMMARY:

Representative Michael G. Grimm, a former F.B.I. agent and Marine, is expected to be indicted on federal fraud charges for allegedly engaging in corruption, obstruction of justice, and lying under oath. The investigation, which began over two years ago, focused on his conduct at Healthalicious, a health food restaurant he owned on the Upper East Side of Manhattan. Grimm's fund-raisers have already been charged with funneling money into his campaign and visa fraud. The congressman "asserts his innocence" and claims the investigation is a "politically driven vendetta." He has developed a reputation for being hot-tempered, which was evident in a bizarre threat he made to a TV reporter earlier this year. The charges against Grimm are expected to include mail fraud and wire fraud, and could potentially impact his re-election bid. If indicted, Grimm would be the latest member of Congress to face serious allegations of misconduct.

END OF SUMMARY.
