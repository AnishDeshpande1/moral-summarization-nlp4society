Representative Michael G. Grimm, a former FBI agent and Marine, is expected to be indicted on federal fraud charges after a lengthy corruption probe. Known for a hot‑tempered demeanor, Grimm recently made a bizarre on‑air threat, telling a reporter he would “break you in half.” The congressman, the sole Republican from New York City, remains in office and seeks re‑election amid the looming legal battle.
