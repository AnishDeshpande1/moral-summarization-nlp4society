SUMMARY:

The NAACP passed a resolution condemning the Tea Party movement's tolerance for bigotry, despite claims from Tea Partiers that it is a political ploy. The resolution calls on Tea Party leaders to repudiate racism and bigotry within their ranks. NAACP President Benjamin Todd Jealous emphasized that the organization takes no issue with the Tea Party movement itself, but rather with its continued tolerance of bigoted statements and actions. This move comes amidst allegations of racism within the Tea Party movement, including an incident where protesters allegedly hurled racial epithets at black lawmakers on Capitol Hill. The resolution highlights the importance of accountability and responsibility in leadership, particularly when it comes to marginalized communities. By condemning bigotry and calling for its rejection, the NAACP is upholding the principles of fairness, care, and respect for all individuals.

END OF SUMMARY.
