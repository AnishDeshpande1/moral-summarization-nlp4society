SUMMARY:

Donald Trump's inauguration marked a new era for the United States, as he vowed to shatter the established order and reverse national decline. In his inaugural address, Trump presented himself as the leader of a populist uprising, painting a dark picture of an America afflicted by economic dislocation and foreign exploitation. He rebuked those who have held power in Washington, saying they have reaped the rewards while the people bore the cost. Trump's view of the United States was strikingly grim, with "American carnage" needing to be stopped. His protectionist agenda and promise to eradicate Islamic terrorism were also highlighted. The transfer of power was marked by protests and disruptions, with over 200 arrests made. Despite this, Trump's inauguration marked a significant shift in American politics, as he cast himself as the ultimate outsider not beholden to the current system.

END OF SUMMARY.
