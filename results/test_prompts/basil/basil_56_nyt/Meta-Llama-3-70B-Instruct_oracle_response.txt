SUMMARY:

Senator Marco Rubio is set to outline his foreign policy approach, which prioritizes preserving America's moral clarity and promoting freedom globally. He vows to "adequately fund" the military to ensure American strength, even in times of peace and stability. Rubio promises to oppose any violations of international waters, airspace, cyberspace, or outer space, including economic disruption caused by invasions. He will reinforce alliances, resist efforts by large powers to subjugate smaller neighbors, and maintain robust foreign assistance programs. Rubio's vision is to advance the rights of vulnerable groups, including women and religious minorities who are often persecuted. He emphasizes that the American people hear the cries, see the suffering, and desire freedom for the afflicted peoples of the world.

END OF SUMMARY.
