SUMMARY:

Senator Marco Rubio outlined his foreign policy approach in a speech at the Council on Foreign Relations, emphasizing three main pillars: ensuring American strength, protecting the economy, and preserving moral clarity. Rubio prioritizes adequately funding the military, even in times of peace, to restore American strength. He vows to use American power to oppose violations of international waters, airspace, and cyberspace, and to support the spread of economic and political freedom. Rubio's approach is marked by a strong emphasis on American exceptionalism and a willingness to take decisive action to maintain global order. His stance is a clear departure from the Obama Doctrine, which is often criticized for being overly cautious. Rubio's speech positions him as a defense hawk, setting him apart from other Republican presidential candidates. Overall, Rubio's foreign policy approach is centered on promoting American values and interests while maintaining a strong military presence.

END OF SUMMARY.
