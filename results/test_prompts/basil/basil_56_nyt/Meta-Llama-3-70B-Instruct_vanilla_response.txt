SUMMARY:

Senator Marco Rubio, a 2016 presidential candidate, will outline his approach to foreign policy in a speech before the Council on Foreign Relations in New York. Rubio's approach has three main pillars: ensuring American strength, protecting the American economy, and preserving moral clarity of America's core values. He prioritizes adequately funding the nation's military, even in times of peace and stability. Rubio promises to use American power to oppose violations of international waters, airspace, cyberspace, and outer space, and to support the spread of economic and political freedom. He also vows to reinforce alliances, resist efforts by large powers to subjugate smaller neighbors, and advance the rights of vulnerable groups, including women and religious minorities. Rubio's speech aims to cast himself as a defense hawk and distinguish himself in a crowded primary field.

END OF SUMMARY.
