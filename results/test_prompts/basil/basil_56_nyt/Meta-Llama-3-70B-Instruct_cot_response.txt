Senator Marco Rubio, a 2016 presidential candidate, is set to outline his approach to foreign policy in a speech before the Council on Foreign Relations in New York. His approach has three main pillars: ensuring American strength, protecting the American economy, and preserving the moral clarity of America's core values. Rubio's first priority is to "adequately fund" the nation's military, emphasizing the need to maintain a robust commitment to transparent and effective foreign assistance programs. He vows to support the spread of economic and political freedom, reinforce alliances, and resist efforts by large powers to subjugate their smaller neighbors. Rubio also promises to advance the rights of the vulnerable, including women and religious minorities who are often persecuted, and to stand up against economic disruption caused by invasions. His vision is to ensure that the afflicted peoples of the world know that the American people hear their cries, see their suffering, and desire their freedom.
