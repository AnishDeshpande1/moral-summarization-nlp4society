In a Council on Foreign Relations speech, Sen. Marco Rubio outlined a “Rubio Doctrine” that stresses American **strength** and **moral** **clarity**. He pledged to **adequately fund** the military even in peace, using U.S. **power** to **oppose** **violations** of international waters, airspace, cyberspace and **outer space**, and to counter **economic** **disruption** caused by one nation **invading** another. Rubio promised to **spread** **freedom**, **reinforce** **alliances**, and **resist** attempts by great powers to **subjugate** smaller neighbors. He called for a **robust**, **transparent**, and **effective** foreign‑aid system that defends the **rights** of the **vulnerable**, including **women**, **religious** **minorities**, and those **persecuted** or **afflicted** by **suffering**. By hearing their **cries**, Rubio says America will demonstrate its commitment to **protecting** the oppressed and promoting liberty worldwide.
