SUMMARY:

Senator Marco Rubio, a 2016 presidential candidate, has outlined his approach to foreign policy, which prioritizes American strength, economic protection, and moral clarity. Rubio's doctrine emphasizes the need for a robust military, promising to "adequately fund" the nation's defense even in times of peace. He vows to use American power to protect international waters, airspace, and cyberspace, and to resist large powers' attempts to subjugate smaller neighbors. Rubio's vision also includes promoting economic and political freedom, supporting vulnerable groups like women and religious minorities, and advancing human rights. His approach is rooted in a strong sense of moral clarity, emphasizing the importance of defending freedom and opposing oppression. By positioning himself as a defense hawk, Rubio aims to distinguish himself in a crowded primary field.

END OF SUMMARY.
