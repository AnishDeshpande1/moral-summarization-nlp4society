SUMMARY:  
Senator Marco Rubio outlined a “Rubio Doctrine” that builds on past U.S. foreign‑policy doctrines by **preserving** American strength, protecting the economy, and keeping the nation’s **moral** clarity. He pledged to **fund** the military robustly even in “times of peace and stability,” arguing that the world is neither. Rubio said the United States must **oppose** any **violations** of international waters, airspace, cyberspace or outer space, and will act against any country that **invades** another and causes **economic disruption**. His broader vision calls for spreading **freedom**, **reinforcing** alliances, and **resisting** great‑power attempts to **subjugate** smaller neighbors. Rubio also promised a transparent foreign‑assistance program that **advances** the rights of the **vulnerable**, including women and religious minorities who are often **persecuted**. He vowed that the American people will **hear** the world’s **cries**, **see** its **suffering**, and **desire** liberty for all.  

END OF SUMMARY.
