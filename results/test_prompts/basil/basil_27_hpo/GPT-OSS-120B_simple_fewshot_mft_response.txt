SUMMARY:
A new $1 million television spot produced by the neoconservative nonprofit Secure America Now features Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu warning about Iran’s nuclear threat. Although Netanyahu has publicly refused to be dragged into U.S. election politics, the ad is strategically placed in Florida’s large Jewish markets to bolster Republican attacks on President Obama’s perceived softness toward Israel and Iran. The spot reinforces the GOP’s narrative that the Obama administration is undermining traditional U.S. leadership and Israel’s security—essentially “throwing Israel under a bus.” By highlighting Netanyahu’s own rebuke of these claims and his respect for Obama’s anti‑Iran stance, the ad attempts to subvert the President’s authority while reaffirming the authority of Israel’s security concerns and the Republican call for a stronger, more decisive U.S. stance. This campaign continues Secure America Now’s pattern of using foreign leaders to question the legitimacy of the current administration’s foreign‑policy authority.

END OF SUMMARY.
