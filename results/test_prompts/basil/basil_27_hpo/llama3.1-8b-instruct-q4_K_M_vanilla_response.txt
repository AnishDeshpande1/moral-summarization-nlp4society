SUMMARY:

A new ad by Secure America Now, a neoconservative nonprofit organization, features Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu discussing Israel's security concerns about Iran's nuclear program. The ad is airing in Florida markets with large Jewish communities and is part of a $1 million campaign. This ad is the latest example of how Netanyahu has become a focal point in the US presidential election, particularly among Republicans who have criticized President Barack Obama for not taking a firm enough stance against Iran.

Netanyahu himself pushed back on these criticisms in interviews over the weekend, saying he won't be drawn into the US election and that there is no truth to claims that Obama has "thrown Israel under a bus." The ad by Secure America Now is the second time this year it has run an attack ad against Obama, despite describing itself as representing all political ideologies.

END OF SUMMARY.
