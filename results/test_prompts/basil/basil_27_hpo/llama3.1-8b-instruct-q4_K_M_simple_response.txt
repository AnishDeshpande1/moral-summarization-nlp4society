SUMMARY:
A new ad by Secure America Now, a neoconservative nonprofit organization, is airing in Florida targeting President Barack Obama's relationship with Israel. The ad features Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu discussing Iran's nuclear program and the security concerns it poses to Israel. This move comes as part of a larger effort by Republicans to paint the Obama administration as dismissive of Israel's concerns. Netanyahu himself pushed back against these criticisms, stating that he is not getting involved in the US election and that there is no truth to claims that Obama has "thrown Israel under a bus". The ad is just one example of Secure America Now's efforts to influence American security and foreign policy debates, and has been criticized for its apparent partisan bias despite claiming to represent all ideologies. 

END OF SUMMARY.
