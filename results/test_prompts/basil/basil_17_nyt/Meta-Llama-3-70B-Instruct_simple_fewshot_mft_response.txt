SUMMARY:

Texas Governor Rick Perry has taken a tough stance on illegal immigration, vowing to detain and deport every undocumented immigrant apprehended by authorities. Perry criticized the Obama administration's "catch-and-release" policy, calling it "horrific" and saying it allows nonviolent illegal immigrants to be released back into the public. Perry's stance is backed by Arizona Sheriff Joe Arpaio, known for his strict immigration policies, who endorsed Perry's presidential campaign. Arpaio praised Perry's efforts to interdict illegal crossings by sending Texas Rangers to the Texas-Mexico border. Perry's stance is seen as a necessary measure to protect America's national security, public safety, and jobs. The endorsement from Arpaio, who has been criticized for his aggressive immigration policies, may help Perry shore up his credentials on immigration. However, it remains to be seen how much the endorsement will help Perry in New Hampshire, where he is polling far behind Mitt Romney.

END OF SUMMARY.
