SUMMARY:

Texas Governor Rick Perry has stated that he would support a federal policy to detain and deport every illegal immigrant apprehended. This policy would involve expedited hearings for those found to be undocumented. Perry criticized the Obama administration's "catch-and-release" policy, which allows some nonviolent illegal immigrants to be released back into the public. Perry's stance on immigration has been a topic of controversy, particularly his support for a Texas plan that grants in-state college tuition to some children of illegal immigrants. He has since apologized for calling opponents of the plan "heartless." Perry has received an endorsement from Sheriff Joe Arpaio of Arizona, known for his tough stance on immigration. However, Arpaio is a polarizing figure, and it remains to be seen how much his endorsement will help Perry in the New Hampshire primary.

END OF SUMMARY.
