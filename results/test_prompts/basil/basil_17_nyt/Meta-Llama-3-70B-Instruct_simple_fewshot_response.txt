SUMMARY:

Texas Governor Rick Perry has vowed to detain and deport every illegal alien apprehended, criticizing the Obama administration's "catch-and-release" policy as "horrific". Perry's stance is seen as a necessary measure to protect America's national security, public safety, and jobs. The candidate has also won an endorsement from Arizona Sheriff Joe Arpaio, known for his tough stance on illegal immigration. Arpaio praised Perry's efforts to interdict illegal crossings by sending Texas Rangers and other state law enforcement to the Texas border with Mexico. Perry's apology for previously calling opponents of his in-state tuition plan "heartless" is seen as an effort to win back Republican primary voters who abandoned him over his stance on illegal immigration. The endorsement from Arpaio, a polarizing figure, may not significantly boost Perry's polling numbers in New Hampshire, where immigration is not a top concern for voters.

END OF SUMMARY.
