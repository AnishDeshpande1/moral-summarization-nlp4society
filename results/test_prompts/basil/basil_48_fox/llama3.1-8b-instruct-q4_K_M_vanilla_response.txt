SUMMARY:
A Republican Congress member's communications director, Elizabeth Lauten, apologized for a Facebook post criticizing the Obama daughters' outfits during President Barack Obama's Thanksgiving address. In the now-deleted post, Lauten told Malia and Sasha to "try showing a little class" and suggested their outfits were more suitable for a bar. She also took a shot at the President and First Lady, implying they set a poor example. The girls had attended the White House turkey pardon ceremony on Wednesday with their mother. After facing backlash, Lauten apologized, stating she realized her words were hurtful and promised to learn from the experience. No public comment was made by the White House or Fincher's office.

END OF SUMMARY.
