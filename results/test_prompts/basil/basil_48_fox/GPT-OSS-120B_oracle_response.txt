SUMMARY:  
A Republican congressional communications director, Elizabeth Lauten, posted a Facebook comment criticizing First Daughters Malia and Sasha Obama’s attire, telling them to “show a little class” and implying they deserved less respect. She also disparaged President Obama and the First Lady, suggesting a lack of respect for their positions. After the post was deleted and widely condemned, Lauten issued an apology, saying she had judged the teenagers unfairly and describing her remarks as hurtful. She said she reflected on the incident through prayer, discussions with her parents, and rereading her words, and pledged to learn and grow from the experience. Neither Rep. Stephen Fincher nor the White House commented publicly.  

END OF SUMMARY.
