SUMMARY: A communications director for Republican Rep. Stephen Fincher, Elizabeth Lauten, posted a disparaging Facebook comment about First Daughters Malia and Sasha Obama after President Barack Obama’s Thanksgiving address. She criticized their attire, suggesting it was more suitable for a bar, and insulted the President and First Lady, questioning their parenting. The post was later deleted, and Lauten issued an apology, acknowledging her judgment was hurtful and promising to learn from the incident. The Washington Post verified the posts as hers, but neither the White House nor Rep. Fincher commented publicly. END OF SUMMARY.
