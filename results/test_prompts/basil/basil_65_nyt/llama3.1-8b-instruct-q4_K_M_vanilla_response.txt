SUMMARY:
According to people who know both of them, Michael R. Bloomberg and Donald J. Trump have had limited interactions despite being billionaires in New York City. They existed in different social circles: Bloomberg is a generous philanthropist who supports the arts, while Trump is known for being crude and flamboyant. The two men met on a few occasions, including playing golf together at a charity event in 2007 and appearing on each other's TV shows. However, their cordiality ended when Trump declared his candidacy, and Bloomberg began exploring an independent presidential bid to block him from being elected. Bloomberg eventually ruled out running due to concerns it would help Trump win. The men have since traded verbal barbs, with Trump questioning Bloomberg's worthiness on CNN.

END OF SUMMARY.
