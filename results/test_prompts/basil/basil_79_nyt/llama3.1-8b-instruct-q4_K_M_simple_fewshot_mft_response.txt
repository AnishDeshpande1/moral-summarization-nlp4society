SUMMARY:

President Trump expressed sympathy for his former national security adviser Michael Flynn, who pleaded guilty to lying to the FBI about conversations with the Russian ambassador. Trump claimed that Flynn's life was "destroyed" by the FBI and contrasted it with Hillary Clinton's treatment after she lied "many times" to the FBI during an investigation into her private email server. However, Trump did not provide specific details about what he considered Clinton's lies. This comment has sparked concerns about Trump's involvement in the Russia investigation and his potential interference with justice. The special counsel's investigation is focusing on Trump's inner circle, and some experts have noted that presidents typically avoid weighing in on federal investigations to avoid influencing the outcome. Trump's comments raise questions about fairness and whether he is trying to deflect attention from Flynn's guilty plea.

END OF SUMMARY.
