SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox blasted President Enrique Peña Nieto for inviting Republican nominee Donald Trump to a private meeting, calling the gesture a **mistake** and warning it would make Peña look like a **traitor**. Fox said “by 130 million people, we don’t like him, we don’t want him,” condemning Trump’s **fearmongering** about “rapists” and drug dealers and accusing him of **using** Mexico to **cheat** voters. He denounced the invitation as **lying** and urged the U.S. public to see Trump as a **false prophet**. Fox recalled his own earlier outburst against the wall, his apology, and his invitation for Trump to visit Mexico “to learn about the real Mexico,” which Trump later mocked. Peña’s approval is already at a record low after multiple **scandals**, and Fox warned that hosting Trump could deepen his political **mistake** and damage Mexico’s dignity. **Stop** the disrespect, Fox demanded. END OF SUMMARY.
