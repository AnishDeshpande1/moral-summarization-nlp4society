He accused Trump of using Mexico and its leaders to boost his sinking poll numbers through fear-mongering about the border wall and immigrants. Fox also condemned Trump for lying about Mexico's willingness to pay for the wall. Peña's decision to host Trump is seen as an opportunistic move that will likely be met with widespread criticism from Mexicans who feel offended by Trump's rhetoric. Fox urged US citizens to see through Trump's "false prophet" image and understand his true intentions.

Fox had previously invited Trump to apologize to Mexicans for his comments, but Trump instead used the opportunity to boast about his own actions. Peña's approval rating is already at a record low due to scandals surrounding his administration. His decision to host Trump may further erode his credibility among Mexicans. END OF SUMMARY.
