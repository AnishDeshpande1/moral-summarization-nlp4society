SUMMARY:
Former Mexican President Vicente Fox blasted President Enrique Peña Nieto for inviting Republican presidential nominee Donald Trump to a private meeting, calling the gesture a blatant subversion of Mexico’s sovereign authority and a betrayal of the Mexican people. Fox warned that Peña would be seen as a “traitor” for enabling Trump, whom he labeled a “false prophet” who cheats both U.S. and Mexican voters with deceptive promises about a border wall, rapists and drug dealers. Fox reminded viewers that he himself once cursed the wall, later apologized, and invited Trump to learn the “real Mexico,” but now condemns Peña’s decision as an opportunistic, unfair move that exploits Mexico’s dignity for political gain. The exchange underscores a moral clash: Fox frames Peña’s invitation as an unlawful abuse of power and a cheating of national interests, while Trump’s retort about Fox’s past apology is portrayed as further disrespect to Mexican authority. END OF SUMMARY.
