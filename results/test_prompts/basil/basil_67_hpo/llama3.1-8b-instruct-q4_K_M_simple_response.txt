SUMMARY:
Former Mexican President Vicente Fox criticized current President Enrique Peña Nieto for inviting Donald Trump to meet in Mexico, calling it a "big mistake" that will harm Peña's reputation. Fox said that by hosting Trump, Peña would be seen as "soft on him" and even a "traitor" among Mexicans. He accused Trump of using Mexico to boost his poll numbers and warned that the meeting could backfire.

Fox also expressed outrage over Trump's previous comments about Mexico, calling him a "false prophet" who is "cheating everybody". He emphasized that Mexico will not pay for a border wall, reiterating his earlier apology for a past outburst. Peña Nieto's approval rating has been low due to corruption scandals, and Fox suggested that the meeting could be seen as an attempt by Peña to boost his image.

The meeting with Trump is seen as a risk to both Peña's and Trump's candidacies, and it remains to be seen how this will play out in public opinion. END OF SUMMARY.
