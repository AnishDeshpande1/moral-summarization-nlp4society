SUMMARY:

The RNC chairman, Michael Steele, made comments that sparked swift reactions from prominent conservatives. Bill Kristol wrote a letter calling for Steele's resignation, saying that the war in Afghanistan was not Obama's choosing but rather a war prosecuted by both Bush and Obama administrations with Republican support. Kristol's letter stated that Steele's comment puts him at odds with nearly 100% of the Republican Party and is an affront to the party's honor. Other conservative figures, such as Dan Senor, also criticized Steele's comments, saying they are utterly irresponsible and inconsistent with his previous statements. Senor noted that the RNC sponsored a conference call praising Obama's Afghan surge last year, but now Steele says Afghanistan is an unwinnable war that has failed to be won by anyone throughout history. The comments have resonated among anti-war Democrats but are seen as embarrassing and damaging to Steele's tenure as RNC chairman.

END OF SUMMARY.
