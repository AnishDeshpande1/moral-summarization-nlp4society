SUMMARY:  
In a confrontational CBS interview, Rick Santorum defended his recent remarks that prenatal **testing**—specifically **amniocentesis**—leads to more **abortions** and should not be **covered** by health‑insurance **procedures** because it “**creates**” a higher risk of **miscarriage** and then **recommends** abortion to **cull** disabled infants. He also argued that federally‑run education is “**anachronistic**,” urging that **control** be returned to local communities and parents, likening schools to a **car** that must meet the **customer’s** needs rather than a **government**‑imposed system. Santorum claimed President Obama’s policies are not **based** on the Bible, calling them a “phony theology” that does not **protect** jobs or quality of life. Obama aide Robert Gibbs rebuked Santorum, saying his rhetoric **disqualify**s civil discourse and pushes politics “to the bottom.”  

END OF SUMMARY.
