SUMMARY:

Rick Santorum, a Republican presidential candidate, made several controversial remarks in an interview on CBS's "Face the Nation". He claimed that President Obama's policies are not based on the Bible and that prenatal testing leads to more abortions. Santorum also stated that government-provided education is "anachronistic" and that health insurance providers should not be forced to cover prenatal testing. When questioned about his comments, Santorum insisted that he was not challenging Obama's Christianity but rather criticizing his worldview. He suggested that Obama's policies are based on a "phony theology" that prioritizes environmentalism over human interests.

Critics, including an aide to the Obama campaign, have called Santorum's remarks divisive and well beyond the bounds of acceptable politics. They argue that such rhetoric is unbecoming of a presidential candidate and that it reflects a disturbing trend in the Republican primary race.

END OF SUMMARY.
