SUMMARY:

Rep. Bob Etheridge (D-N.C.) was filmed being confrontational towards an anonymous reporter who asked him about his support for the Obama agenda. The congressman grabbed the reporter's wrist and knocked over the camera, despite the reporter identifying themselves as a student working on a project. The incident has sparked debate about the behavior of politicians and journalists in today's polarized climate.

The video was released by Big Government, which accused Etheridge of being representative of the "hard, progressive left" that has led to unpopular legislation and economic woes. Mediaite responded by questioning the motivations of the anonymous reporter and suggesting that Etheridge may have been justified in his response.

Etheridge later apologized for his behavior, stating that he regretted his reaction and would work to promote civil public discourse. DNC spokesman Brad Woodhouse also weighed in, accusing the Republican Party of orchestrating the ambush interview as a "hatchet job."

END OF SUMMARY.
