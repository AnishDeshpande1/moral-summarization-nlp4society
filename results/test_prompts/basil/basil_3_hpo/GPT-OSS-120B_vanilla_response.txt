SUMMARY: Rep. Bob Etheridge (D‑N.C.) was caught on video knocking a camera to the ground and physically confronting an anonymous “student” reporter who asked if he “fully supported the Obama agenda.” The exchange, captured by a YouTube channel that blurred the interviewers’ faces, sparked commentary on partisan “gotcha” journalism. The right‑leaning blog Big Government used the incident to criticize Democratic leadership, while Mediaite noted the provocation of the interview technique and suggested Etheridge’s reaction, though unprofessional, was partly understandable. The DNC’s Brad Woodhouse claimed the video was part of a Republican tracking operation, pointing to the blurred faces as evidence. Etheridge later issued an apology, expressing regret for his behavior and reaffirming his commitment to civil discourse. END OF SUMMARY.
