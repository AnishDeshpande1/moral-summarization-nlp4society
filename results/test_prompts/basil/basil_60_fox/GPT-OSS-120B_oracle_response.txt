SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox blasted Donald Trump’s border‑wall plan, shouting “I’m not going to pay for that f***ing wall! He should pay for it. He’s got the money.” Fox warned that Hispanics who support Trump are “followers of a false prophet” and urged them to open their eyes before the nation **fails** or **falls** into the hands of a **crazy**, **egocentric** man. He stressed that it is not about defending race or creed, but defending the nation that hosts them, and said Trump is **not** a **Republican** or a Democrat, but only himself. Fox’s remarks echo those of ex‑president Felipe Calderón, who also said Mexico will not pay a cent for the wall, calling it useless. Trump responded on “Morning Joe” that the wall is now “taller” after Mexico’s “no way.” The interview follows journalist Jorge Ramos’s clash with Trump, where Ramos claimed Trump **tried** to **silence** him, asserting his **right** to **ask** any question. Vice‑President Joe Biden, on a Mexico visit, labeled Trump’s **rhetoric** as **dangerous**, “damaging and incredibly ill‑advised.” END OF SUMMARY.
