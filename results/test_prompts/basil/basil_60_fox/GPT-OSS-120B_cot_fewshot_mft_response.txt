Former Mexican President Vicente Fox told journalist Jorge Ramos that he will not pay for Donald Trump’s “f***ing wall,” insisting the billionaire “should pay for it” because he “has the money.” Fox denounced Trump as a “false prophet” and a “crazy, egocentric” leader who would lead Hispanics “into the desert” and warned they must “open your eyes.” Emphasizing that his stance is not about defending “race” or “creed” but about protecting the nation, Fox warned that the country will fail if it falls into the hands of a “dangerous” man whose rhetoric is “damaging” and “ill‑advised.” He also called Trump “not a Republican, not a Democrat, just himself,” rejecting any claim of party loyalty. Fox’s comments echo those of his successor Felipe Calderón, who also said Mexico will not pay a “stupid, useless” wall. The interview follows recent clashes between Trump and Mexican‑born journalist Jorge Ramos and a thinly‑veiled criticism of Trump by U.S. Vice‑President Joe Biden.
