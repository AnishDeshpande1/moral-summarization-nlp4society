SUMMARY:
Former Mexican President Vicente Fox exploded in a Fusion interview, declaring he will never help fund Donald Trump’s U.S.–Mexico border wall, calling it “that f***ing wall” and insisting Trump himself should pay. Fox warned that Trump is a “false prophet” who is misleading Hispanic voters—citing the Nevada caucus where Trump captured 44 % of the Hispanic vote—and urged U.S. Hispanics to “open your eyes” before they are led into disaster. He framed his opposition not as a defense of race or creed, but as a defense of the nation from a “crazy man” whose ego, not any party principles, drives him. Fox also dismissed Trump’s party allegiance, calling him neither Republican nor Democrat, just egocentric. His remarks echo those of ex‑president Felipe Calderón, who also said Mexico will not pay a cent for the wall. The interview came amid Trump’s reaction to Calderón’s comments, a recent clash between journalist Jorge Ramos and Trump, and Vice President Joe Biden’s criticism of Trump’s “dangerous” rhetoric during a visit to Mexico.  
END OF SUMMARY.
