Glenn Beck, hailed as a hero of the political right, closed the three‑day CPAC with a fiery denunciation of progressivism, calling it a cancer that must be cut from America. He dismissed the simple answer of blaming President Obama, labeling progressivism the disease afflicting the country. Interweaving his personal recovery story, Beck urged personal responsibility, rejecting the notion that anyone should be demonized or penalized for others’ mistakes.
