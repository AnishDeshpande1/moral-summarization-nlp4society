SUMMARY: President Donald Trump took to Twitter on Saturday to condemn social‑media firms for “totally discriminating” against Republican and conservative voices. He warned that “too many voices are being destroyed, some good and some bad, and that cannot be allowed to happen,” urging that everyone be allowed to participate, regardless of quality, so society can work out the consequences. Trump called the platforms’ actions “censorship,” labeling it a “very dangerous thing” that is impossible to police. He also launched his usual attack on “fake news” from CNN and MSNBC, admitting he watches them “with a grain of salt.” While not naming any individual, his rant came as tech companies recently barred conspiracy theorist Alex Jones and Infowars.
