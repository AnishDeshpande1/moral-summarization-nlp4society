SUMMARY: President Donald Trump blasted social media companies on Twitter, accusing them of “totally discriminating against Republican/Conservative voices” and of “destroying” many opinions. He claimed the platforms are “closing down the opinions of many people on the RIGHT” while doing “nothing” for others, warning that such censorship is “a very dangerous thing & impossible to police.” Trump urged that “everybody participate, good & bad, and we will all just have to figure it out,” while also dismissing “fake news” from CNN and MSNBC as something he watches “with a grain of salt.” The rant came amid recent bans of conspiracy theorist Alex Jones and Infowars from the same tech sites. END OF SUMMARY.
