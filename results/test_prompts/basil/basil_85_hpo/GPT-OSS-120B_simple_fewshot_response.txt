SUMMARY:
President Donald Trump blasted social‑media platforms on Twitter, accusing them of “totally discriminating against Republican/Conservative voices” and silencing “too many voices…some good and some bad.” He warned that censorship is “a very dangerous thing & impossible to police,” and urged that “everybody participate, good & bad, and we will all just have to figure it out.” Trump claimed the companies shut down right‑wing opinions while ignoring others, and he again dismissed “fake news” from CNN and MSNBC, saying he watches them “with a grain of salt.” Although he named no individual, the rant came as tech firms recently banned conspiracy theorist Alex Jones and Infowars. Trump’s moral appeal frames the issue as a fight for free speech and fairness, demanding that all viewpoints be allowed to coexist without corporate suppression.  
END OF SUMMARY.
