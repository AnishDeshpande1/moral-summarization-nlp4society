SUMMARY:
President Donald Trump took to Twitter to denounce social‑media platforms for what he portrayed as unfair treatment of Republican and conservative voices. He claimed the companies are “totally discriminating” against the right while allowing other viewpoints to remain unchecked, framing their moderation policies as a form of cheating that silences a segment of the public. Trump warned that “too many voices are being destroyed, some good and some bad,” and called for an open arena where everyone can participate, arguing that censorship is a dangerous, unjust practice that cannot be properly policed. He linked the issue to broader concerns about “fake news” from outlets like CNN and MSNBC, noting he watches them only “with a grain of salt.” The tweet underscores the moral accusation that tech giants are cheating conservatives out of fair representation in the public discourse. END OF SUMMARY.
