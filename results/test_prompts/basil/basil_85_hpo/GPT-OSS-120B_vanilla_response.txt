SUMMARY: President Donald Trump took to Twitter on Saturday to criticize major social media platforms, alleging they discriminate against Republican and conservative viewpoints. He argued that both “good” and “bad” voices are being silenced and called for unrestricted participation, stating that society must figure out the consequences. Trump labeled the companies’ actions as dangerous censorship that is impossible to police, and continued his usual attacks on “fake news” outlets like CNN and MSNBC, noting he watches them “with a grain of salt.” While he did not name any individual, his remarks come amid recent bans of conspiracy theorist Alex Jones and Infowars from several platforms. END OF SUMMARY.
