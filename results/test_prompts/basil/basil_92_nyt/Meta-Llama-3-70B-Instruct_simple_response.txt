SUMMARY:

The special counsel's office disputed a BuzzFeed News report that claimed President Trump directed his lawyer Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about his role in Moscow skyscraper negotiations. The report sparked impeachment talks, but the special counsel's rare public statement challenged the facts, stating that BuzzFeed's description of documents and testimony was "not accurate." The White House and Trump's lawyers also denied the report. The allegations, if true, would be a cornerstone of a case that Trump obstructed justice. Democrats on the House Intelligence Committee vowed to investigate and secure additional witness testimony. The report added to a chaotic week for the White House, which has faced questions about recent revelations, including an FBI counterintelligence investigation into Trump and his interactions with Russian President Vladimir Putin.

END OF SUMMARY.
