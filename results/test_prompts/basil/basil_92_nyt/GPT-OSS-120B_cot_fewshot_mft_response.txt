**SUMMARY:**  
The special counsel’s office publicly **disputed** a BuzzFeed News report that claimed President Trump directed Michael Cohen to commit **perjury** and **subornation** of perjury by pressuring him to lie to Congress about the Trump Tower Moscow deal. In a rare statement, spokesman Peter Carr said BuzzFeed’s description of statements and documents was **inaccurate** and **false**, calling the allegations **categorically false**. Democrats on the House and Senate Intelligence Committees warned that, if true, the conduct would constitute **obstruction** of justice and **cheating** with the legal system, prompting calls for impeachment or resignation. The White House and Trump’s lawyers also denied the story as **false**. BuzzFeed’s editor Ben Smith defended the reporting as accurate, urging the special counsel to clarify its objections. The controversy adds to ongoing investigations into Russian interference and the president’s alleged attempts to **obstruct** justice.
