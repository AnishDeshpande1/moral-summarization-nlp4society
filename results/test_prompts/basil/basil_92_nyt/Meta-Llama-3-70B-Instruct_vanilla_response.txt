SUMMARY:

The special counsel, Robert S. Mueller III, disputed a report by BuzzFeed News that claimed President Trump directed his lawyer, Michael D. Cohen, to lie to Congress about a Moscow skyscraper project. The report led to calls for impeachment proceedings, but Mueller's office said the description of specific statements and documents was "not accurate." The White House and Trump's lawyers also denied the report. BuzzFeed News stood by its reporting, urging Mueller's office to clarify what it was disputing. The House Intelligence Committee plans to investigate the report, and if true, it could constitute obstruction of justice and subornation of perjury. The report added to a chaotic week for the White House, which has faced questions about the FBI's investigation into Trump's interactions with Russian President Vladimir Putin.

END OF SUMMARY.
