SUMMARY:

Special Counsel Robert S. Mueller III disputed a report by BuzzFeed News that President Trump directed his lawyer Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about the Moscow skyscraper deal. The special counsel's spokesman, Peter Carr, said the facts of the article were not accurate. However, BuzzFeed News stood by its reporting and sources. If true, the allegations could be grounds for impeachment proceedings against Trump.

Representatives Adam B. Schiff and Joaquin Castro called for investigation into the matter, while Senator Mark Warner expected Cohen to testify privately in early February. The White House and Trump's lawyers also denied the report. Mueller's office previously described how Cohen lied to Congress about the deal and discussed it with Trump more frequently than he claimed.

The dispute added another jolt to a chaotic week for the White House, which has faced questions about F.B.I. investigations into Trump and his interactions with Russian President Vladimir Putin.

END OF SUMMARY.
