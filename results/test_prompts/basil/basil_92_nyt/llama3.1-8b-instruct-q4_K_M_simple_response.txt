SUMMARY:

Special counsel Robert S. Mueller III disputed a report by BuzzFeed News that President Trump directed Michael D. Cohen, his lawyer and fixer, to lie to Congress about the Trump Tower Moscow deal. The special counsel's spokesman, Peter Carr, said the report was not accurate, but BuzzFeed News stood by its reporting. If true, the allegations could be grounds for impeachment proceedings against Mr. Trump. Senior members of Congress, including Representative Adam B. Schiff and Senator Mark Warner, reacted to the report with urgency, calling it a serious matter that requires investigation.

The special counsel's office has accused Mr. Cohen of lying to Congress about his role in the negotiations, which continued as late as June 2016. Mr. Trump has denied any wrongdoing, but his former lawyer, who pleaded guilty to campaign finance violations and financial crimes, said he acted out of blind loyalty to the president.

END OF SUMMARY.
