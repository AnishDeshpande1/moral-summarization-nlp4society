**SUMMARY:**
The special counsel's office disputed a report by BuzzFeed News that President Trump had directed his lawyer, Michael Cohen, to commit perjury about the Trump Tower Moscow deal. The BuzzFeed report alleged that Cohen told prosecutors he was pressured by Trump to lie to Congress. If true, this would be a damning revelation in the investigation into Russia's attempts to sabotage the 2016 election and could lead to obstruction charges against the president. The White House and Trump's lawyers denied the allegations, calling them "categorically false." However, Cohen had previously lied to Congress about his interactions with Russian officials and the fact that he asked Trump to travel to Russia to promote the deal. His truthfulness in subsequent interviews was a departure from his pattern of deception throughout his professional life, driven by greed.
