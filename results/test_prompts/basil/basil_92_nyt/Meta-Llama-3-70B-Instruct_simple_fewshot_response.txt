SUMMARY:

The special counsel investigating Russian interference in the 2016 election disputed a report that President Trump directed Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about his role in negotiations to build a skyscraper in Moscow. The rare public statement by the special counsel's office challenged the facts of the article, stating that BuzzFeed's description of specific statements and characterization of documents and testimony were "not accurate". The report led to a flurry of reactions by senior members of Congress, who said that the allegations, if true, could be grounds for initiating impeachment proceedings against Mr. Trump. The president and his team vigorously denied the report, while BuzzFeed stood by its reporting. The dispute adds to a chaotic week for the White House, which has faced questions about recent revelations that FBI counterintelligence agents began investigating Mr. Trump in 2017 and that Mr. Trump has tried to conceal the details from senior administration officials about his interactions with President Vladimir V. Putin of Russia.

END OF SUMMARY.
