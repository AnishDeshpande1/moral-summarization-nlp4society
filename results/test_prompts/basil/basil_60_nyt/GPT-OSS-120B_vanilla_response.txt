SUMMARY: Former Mexican presidents Vicente Fox and Felipe Calderón publicly rejected Donald Trump’s demand that Mexico pay for his proposed border wall. Fox, using profanity, insisted “I’m not going to pay” and criticized Trump as egocentric, while Calderón called the wall “stupid,” useless, and warned it would harm the U.S. economy and that Mexico would not fund any part of it. Both leaders emphasized that Mexico will not bear any cost. Trump, meanwhile, maintains he could force Mexico to cover the estimated $8 billion price tag, but expressed irritation at Fox’s language and demanded an apology. END OF SUMMARY.
