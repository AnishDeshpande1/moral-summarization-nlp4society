**SUMMARY:**  
At his rallies Donald Trump repeatedly asks, “Who’s gonna pay?” and receives the chant “Mexico!” Former Mexican presidents Vicente Fox and Felipe Calderón both rebuke the demand. Fox calls Trump “egocentric,” a “false prophet” and says he will not “pay” for the wall, demanding that Trump himself fund the $8 billion project. Calderón adds that the wall is a “stupid,” “useless” idea, labeling Trump’s claims about Mexican immigration “misinformed” and warning that closing the border would harm America’s economy. Both leaders stress that Mexico will not bear the cost, framing the proposal as unfair and degrading. Trump, irritated by Fox’s profanity, tells the former president to apologize for his “foul” language. The exchange highlights a clash over authority, fairness, and moral degradation surrounding the border‑wall plan.
