**SUMMARY:**  
At his rallies, Donald J. Trump repeatedly asks, “Who’s gonna pay?” and receives the chant “Mexico!” while promoting a giant border wall. Former Mexican presidents reject the demand. Vicente Fox, calling Trump “*egocentric*” and “a *false prophet*,” swore he would not pay, insisting Trump “has the money.” Felipe Calderón echoed the rebuke, labeling the wall “*stupid*” and “*useless*,” and warned that it would be ineffective and harmful to the U.S. economy. Calderón also said Trump is *misinformed* about immigration flows. Trump, who claims he can force Mexico to foot the $8 billion price tag, was angered by Fox’s *profane* remarks and demanded an apology for the *foul* language. The Mexican leaders’ united stance underscores a moral rejection of the wall and the burden‑shifting rhetoric.
