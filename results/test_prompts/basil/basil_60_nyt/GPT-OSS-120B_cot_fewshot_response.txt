**SUMMARY:**  
At his rallies, Donald Trump repeatedly asks, “Who’s gonna pay?” and receives the chant “Mexico!” from supporters. Former Mexican presidents Vicente Fox and Felipe Calderón both rebuffed the claim, declaring “I’m not going to pay” and calling the wall “stupid,” “useless,” and a product of a “false” narrative. Fox labeled Trump “egocentric,” expressed disbelief at the candidate’s success with Hispanic voters, and warned they are “followers of a false prophet.” Calderón added that the wall is “useless,” that Trump is “misinformed” about immigration flows, and that it would damage the U.S. economy. Trump, angered by Fox’s profanity, demanded an apology for the language used. The two former leaders made clear Mexico will not fund the wall.
