SUMMARY:
Former Mexican presidents Vicente Fox and Felipe Calderón bluntly rejected Donald Trump’s demand that Mexico pay for his proposed border wall, calling the idea “stupid,” “useless,” and “a false prophet’s promise.” Fox, swearing that he will not fund the wall, slammed Trump as “egocentric” and warned Hispanic voters who supported the candidate to “open their eyes.” Calderón added that the wall would damage the U.S. economy and that Mexico will not contribute a single cent. Trump, meanwhile, insisted he could force Mexico to foot the $8 billion price tag and demanded an apology from Fox for his profanity. The former leaders’ united stance frames the wall as a misguided, morally bankrupt project that would harm both nations, urging voters to see through Trump’s rhetoric.  
END OF SUMMARY.
