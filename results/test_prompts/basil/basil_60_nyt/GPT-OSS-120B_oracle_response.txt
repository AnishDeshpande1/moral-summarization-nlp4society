SUMMARY:  
At his rallies, Donald J. Trump repeatedly asks, “Who’s gonna pay?” and his supporters answer, “Mexico!” Former Mexican presidents Vicente Fox and Felipe Calderón both **called** out the wall proposal, insisting Mexico will not pay a single cent. Fox, using **profanity**, said the president‑elect should finance the $8 billion project himself and labeled Trump “egocentric” and a “false prophet” for courting Hispanic voters. Calderón added the wall would be “completely useless” and warned it would **harm** the U.S. economy by misreading immigration flows. Trump retorted, demanding an apology from Fox for his **profanity** and claiming he could **go** to Mexico and force payment. Both ex‑leaders warned that the wall would be a costly, ineffective venture.  

END OF SUMMARY.
