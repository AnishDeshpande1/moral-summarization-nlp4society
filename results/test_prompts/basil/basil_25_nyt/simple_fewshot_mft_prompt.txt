“You may have heard that Governor Romney just chose his running mate, Congressman Ryan.
He's a good man. He's a family man.
He's the ideological leader of the Republicans in Congress,” Obama said.
“And he's an articulate spokesperson for Governor Romney's views.
The problem is, those views are wrong.”
