SUMMARY:

All Republicans on the House Intelligence Committee have demanded Rep. Adam Schiff step down as chairman, citing his repeated claims of Trump-Russia collusion despite Mueller's findings. In a letter and during a hearing, they accused Schiff of "knowingly and unlawfully lying" and leaking information. President Trump also called for Schiff's resignation, tweeting that he should be forced to leave Congress. Top Republican Sen. Lindsey Graham compared Schiff to Oliver Stone, saying his comments were "not helpful." Schiff has doubled down on his claim of collusion, pointing to evidence of Russian hacking and meetings between Trump's campaign and family members with Russians. House Speaker Nancy Pelosi dismissed the calls for Schiff's removal as "absolutely ridiculous," saying he was being attacked because he's doing an outstanding job.

END OF SUMMARY.
