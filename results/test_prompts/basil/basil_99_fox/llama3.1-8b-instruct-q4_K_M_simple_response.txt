SUMMARY:

House Republicans on the Intelligence Committee have demanded Rep. Adam Schiff step down as chairman, citing his repeated claims of Trump-Russia collusion despite Special Counsel Robert Mueller's findings. The lawmakers slammed Schiff for "knowingly and unlawfully lying and leaking" in a letter obtained by Fox News. President Trump also called for Schiff's resignation, labeling him a liar. Sen. Lindsey Graham compared Schiff to Oliver Stone, saying he was acting like a conspiracy theorist.

Schiff has doubled down on his claims of collusion, despite Mueller's report finding no evidence. He cited Russian hacking and operatives offering dirt on Hillary Clinton as examples of foreign interference in the election. House Speaker Nancy Pelosi defended Schiff, calling the calls for his removal "absolutely ridiculous." A Democratic aide said Schiff was being attacked because he had done an outstanding job.

The controversy comes as the White House and Congressional Republicans seek to undermine Democrats' efforts to secure the full Mueller report and underlying evidence. END OF SUMMARY.
