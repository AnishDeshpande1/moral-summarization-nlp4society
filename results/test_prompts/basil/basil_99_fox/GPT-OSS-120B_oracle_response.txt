SUMMARY:  
All Republican members of the House Intelligence Committee demanded that Rep. Adam Schiff resign as chairman, accusing him of repeatedly **lying**, **leaking** and pushing a **false**, **outrageous** **conspiracy** narrative that Trump **colluded** with Russia. In a letter obtained by Fox News, the GOP lawmakers said Schiff’s actions are **incompatible** with his **constitutional** duty and that they have **no faith** in his ability to act **consistent** with his responsibilities. They cited the Mueller **investigation**—which found no **collusion** evidence—and warned that Schiff’s claims about Russian **hacking**, **dirt**, and **meetings** with the Trump campaign only **undermine** public trust. President Trump and Sen. Lindsey Graham echoed the criticism, labeling Schiff’s statements as **conspiracy**‑laden. Democrats, led by Speaker Nancy Pelosi, defended Schiff, calling the attacks “**ridiculous**” and insisting on full **oversight** and release of the complete Mueller **probe**.  
END OF SUMMARY.
