SUMMARY:

The Republican members of the House Intelligence Committee have demanded that Rep. Adam Schiff step down as chairman, citing his repeated claims of collusion between the Trump campaign and Russia. They argue that his actions are incompatible with his duty as Chairman and that he has lied about evidence of collusion in the media. The letter to Schiff follows the conclusion of Special Counsel Robert Mueller's investigation, which found no evidence of collusion. President Trump also weighed in, accusing Schiff of knowingly and unlawfully lying and leaking information. Schiff has doubled down on his claim that there is collusion, despite Mueller's findings. Republicans have accused him of driving a false narrative and undermining the faith of the American people. Schiff defended himself by pointing to evidence of Russian hacking and meetings between Trump's campaign members and Russians.

END OF SUMMARY.
