**SUMMARY:**  
All Republican members of the House Intelligence Committee signed a letter demanding Rep. Adam Schiff resign as chairman, slamming his repeated claims of “more than circumstantial evidence” of Trump‑Russia collusion. They called his actions “unlawful,” “knowingly” misleading and “false,” saying they undermine the public’s faith in the committee. President Trump echoed the demand, tweeting that Schiff has been “knowingly and unlawfully lying and leaking.” Sen. Lindsey Graham labeled Schiff’s statements “outrageous” and likened him to a “conspiracy‑land” Oliver Stone, though he stopped short of demanding resignation. Schiff defended his stance, citing Russian hacking, offers of dirt on Hillary Clinton, and meetings between Trump associates and Russian operatives, insisting “undoubtedly, there is collusion.” House Speaker Nancy Pelosi called the calls to remove Schiff “absolutely ridiculous,” asserting Democrats will not be intimidated or distracted from securing the full Mueller report.
