SUMMARY:
At the Democratic National Convention, former Republican mayor Michael Bloomberg urged Americans to vote for Hillary Clinton, framing the choice as one between responsible authority and subversive chaos. He portrayed Donald Trump as a “dangerous demagogue” whose record of bankruptcies, lawsuits and deceptive business practices threatens the nation’s well‑being, warning that his presidency would harm the economy, retirement savings, public safety and America’s global standing. Bloomberg highlighted the unfairness of Trump’s hypocrisy—boasting wealth while hiding his net worth and tax returns—and contrasted it with Clinton’s competence and steadiness. By emphasizing the need for a sane, competent leader who respects the public good, Bloomberg positioned Clinton as the legitimate authority who will protect citizens from the harmful and cheating behavior he attributes to Trump. END OF SUMMARY.
