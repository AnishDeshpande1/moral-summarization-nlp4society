SUMMARY:

A tense hearing took place in the House Intelligence Committee as Republicans demanded Chairman Adam Schiff's resignation, accusing him of promoting false information and damaging the committee's integrity. Rep. Kevin McCarthy compared Schiff to Senator Joseph R. McCarthy, who was known for his accusations of Communist infiltration. Schiff defended himself, stating that he would not absolve President Trump or his campaign from potential wrongdoing without reviewing Mueller's report.

The hearing turned into a partisan battle, with Democrats accusing Republicans of turning a blind eye to Russian interference and trying to obstruct the investigation. Rep. Jim Himes said that history will clarify what happened, while Speaker Nancy Pelosi praised Schiff as "calm, professional, patriotic." The party has shown confidence in Schiff, naming him a key fund-raiser for vulnerable members.

The hearing was a stark contrast to the Senate Intelligence Committee's approach, which continues its investigation with a no-leaks team. The episode highlighted the deep divide between Democrats and Republicans on the issue of Russian interference and potential wrongdoing by Trump's campaign.

END OF SUMMARY.
