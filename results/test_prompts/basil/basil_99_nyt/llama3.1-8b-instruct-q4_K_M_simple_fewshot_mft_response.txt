SUMMARY:

The House Intelligence Committee's hearing on Russia's intervention in the 2016 presidential election descended into chaos as Republicans demanded Chairman Adam Schiff's resignation, accusing him of abusing his position and promoting false information. Representative Kevin McCarthy compared Schiff to Joseph R. McCarthy, who fueled fears of Communist spies in the 1950s. The Republicans' attacks were seen as an attempt to deflect attention from the committee's investigation into Trump's campaign and its potential involvement with Russia. Chairman Schiff pushed back against the accusations, stating that the committee would continue to investigate evidence of foreign compromise, despite the Republicans' attempts to discredit him. Democrats praised Schiff for his professionalism and patriotism, while Trump accused him of "knowingly and unlawfully lying and leaking." The episode highlighted the bitter divide in Congress over the Russia investigation and the Trump administration's actions.

END OF SUMMARY.
