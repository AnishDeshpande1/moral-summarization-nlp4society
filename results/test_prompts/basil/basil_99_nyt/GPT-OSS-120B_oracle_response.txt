SUMMARY:  
During the first House Intelligence Committee hearing after Mueller’s report, Republicans **demanded** Chairman Adam Schiff’s **resignation**, accusing him of **promoting** false narratives and **damaging** the committee’s integrity. Schiff retorted that the Trump campaign’s **nefarious conduct**—including the **establishment** of a secret back‑channel, **contact** with the GRU, and **conferred** talks with the Russian ambassador to **undermine** sanctions—was **immoral**, **unethical**, **unpatriotic** and **corrupt**. He warned of a **pattern** of **wrongdoing** and said the president’s son‑in‑law **lied** to the FBI. Republicans labeled Schiff’s actions **incompatible** with his duties, **abused** his position and **forced** him to **leak** information, while President Trump called for his removal. Democrats defended Schiff as **professional**, **patriotic** and **responsible**, contrasting him with former chair Devin Nunes, whom they called **irresponsible** and **criminal**. Expert witnesses warned that Putin’s Russia is **spreading** conspiracies and **efforts** to **destabilize** Western democracies, a threat the Senate panel continues to **investigate**.  

END OF SUMMARY.
