**SUMMARY:**  
During the first House Intelligence Committee hearing after Mueller’s report, Republicans moved en masse to demand Chairman Adam Schiff’s resignation, sparking acrimony and a bitter partisan clash. Rep. Kevin McCarthy likened Schiff to Senator Joseph McCarthy, accusing him of subverting the committee and chasing “Russian collusion” like a McCarthyist witch‑hunt. Schiff retorted that the president’s son‑in‑law’s secret back‑channel with Russia was immoral, unethical, unpatriotic and corrupt, calling the conduct a betrayal of the nation. Republicans hailed Mueller’s “absolution” of Trump, branding Democrats as enraged liars who leak false information and undermine faith in institutions. Democrats defended Schiff as calm, professional and patriotic, condemning the Republican demand as abusive, irresponsible and criminal. The showdown highlighted deep divisions over authority, fairness, loyalty and the perceived degradation of democratic norms. END OF SUMMARY.
