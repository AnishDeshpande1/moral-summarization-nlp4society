SUMMARY:

Former Mexican President Vicente Fox strongly criticized current President Enrique Peña Nieto for inviting Donald Trump to meet with him in Mexico, stating that Trump is "not welcome" in the country. Fox accused Trump of demonizing Mexicans and vowed that Mexico would never pay for Trump's proposed border wall. He also called Trump a "false prophet" who was misleading his followers. Fox urged Americans not to vote for Trump, questioning his ability to run a nation or even a business. Trump responded on Twitter, referencing a past incident where Fox invited him to visit Mexico, but Fox fired back, accusing Trump of lying and disrespecting Mexico. The exchange highlights the deep-seated tensions and mistrust between Trump and Mexican leaders, with Fox framing Trump's actions as a subversion of authority, unfair, and degrading to Mexico's dignity. Fox's moral outrage emphasizes the importance of respecting Mexico's sovereignty and rejecting Trump's divisive rhetoric.

END OF SUMMARY.
