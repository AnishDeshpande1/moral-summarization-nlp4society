SUMMARY:

Former Mexican President Vicente Fox strongly condemned Donald Trump's visit to Mexico, calling it a "mistake" and stating that Trump is "not welcome" in the country. Fox criticized Trump's demonization of Mexicans and his inconsistent policies, saying "You cannot trust a guy like this." He also expressed disbelief that current President Enrique Peña Nieto would invite Trump for a private meeting, suggesting it's a desperate attempt to boost Trump's sagging poll numbers. Fox urged Americans not to vote for Trump, saying he doesn't know how to run a nation or even a business. In a Twitter exchange, Fox reminded Trump that he previously invited him to apologize to all Mexicans, not to play politics. Fox's strong words convey a sense of moral outrage and disrespect towards Trump's divisive rhetoric and actions.

END OF SUMMARY.
