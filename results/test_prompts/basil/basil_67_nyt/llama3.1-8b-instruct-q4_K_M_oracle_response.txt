SUMMARY: 
Former Mexican President Vicente Fox strongly opposes Donald Trump's visit, calling it a mistake and stating that Mexico does not want him. Fox has been critical of Trump's plan to build a wall on the US-Mexico border and his treatment of undocumented immigrants. He believes Trump's words are misleading and that he disregards Mexicans with his demonizing rhetoric. According to Fox, Trump is not welcome in Mexico because he cannot be trusted due to his ever-changing policies. 

Fox also expressed disbelief that current Mexican President Enrique Peña Nieto invited Trump for a private meeting, suggesting it was a desperate attempt by Trump to boost his poll numbers. Fox urged Americans not to vote for Trump, emphasizing that he lacks the leadership qualities needed to run a nation. The former president even accused Trump of lying about Mexico paying for the border wall and playfully mocked him on Twitter.

Fox's message is clear: Trump should be disregarded and not taken seriously. He believes Trump's words are false prophets meant to mislead his followers, rather than genuine attempts at leadership. END OF SUMMARY.
