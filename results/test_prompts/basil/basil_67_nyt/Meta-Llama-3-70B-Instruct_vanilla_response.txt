SUMMARY:

Former Mexican President Vicente Fox has strongly criticized Donald Trump's visit to Mexico, stating that Trump is "not welcome" in the country. Fox condemned Trump's plans to build a wall on the US-Mexico border and deport 11 million undocumented immigrants, calling him untrustworthy and a "false prophet". Fox also expressed disbelief that current President Enrique Peña Nieto would invite Trump for a private meeting, suggesting that Trump is only trying to improve his sagging poll numbers. Fox urged Americans not to vote for Trump, saying he doesn't know how to run a nation or a business. Trump responded to Fox's attacks on Twitter, but Fox fired back, reminding Trump that he had invited him to apologize to all Mexicans.

END OF SUMMARY.
