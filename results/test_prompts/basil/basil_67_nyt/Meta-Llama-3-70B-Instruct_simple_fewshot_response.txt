Trump responded on Twitter, referencing a past incident where Fox invited him to visit Mexico, but Fox fired back, accusing Trump of lying and disrespecting Mexico. The moral framing of this exchange suggests that Trump's actions are offensive and disrespectful, and that Mexican leaders have a moral obligation to stand up against his divisive rhetoric. Fox's strong criticism of Trump's visit highlights the deep-seated tensions and mistrust between Trump and Mexican leaders.
