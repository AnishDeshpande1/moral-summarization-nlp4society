SUMMARY:

Former Mexican President Vicente Fox has strongly condemned Donald Trump's upcoming visit to Mexico, calling the invitation a "mistake" and stating that Trump is not welcome in the country. Fox criticized Trump for "demonizing" Mexicans and his inconsistent stance on deporting "undocumented" immigrants. Fox questioned how anyone could trust Trump, who "disregards" his own people and Mexicans, and whose ideas "change" every day. Fox also slammed Trump's plan to build a border wall, which he vowed Mexico would never pay for. Fox urged Americans not to vote for Trump, saying he doesn't know how to "run a nation" or even a business. In a Twitter exchange, Fox accused Trump of "lying" and told him to "show some respect" for Mexico. Fox's strong words reflect his outrage at Trump's actions and rhetoric, which he believes are harmful to Mexico and its people.

END OF SUMMARY.
