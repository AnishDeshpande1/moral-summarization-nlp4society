Former Mexican President Vicente Fox strongly condemned Donald Trump's visit to Mexico, stating that he is "not welcome" in the country. Fox accused Trump of demonizing Mexicans and disrespecting their dignity, calling his plan to build a border wall a "desperate" strategy to boost his poll numbers. He also urged Americans not to vote for Trump, saying that he doesn't know how to run a nation or a business. Fox's criticism was rooted in his belief that Trump is a threat to Mexican sovereignty and a violator of human rights. The former president's stance has sparked a heated exchange with Trump on Twitter, with both sides accusing each other of lying and disrespecting their opponents. Fox's moral framing highlights the importance of authority and respect for nations and peoples, while also emphasizing the need to hold leaders accountable for their actions and rhetoric.
