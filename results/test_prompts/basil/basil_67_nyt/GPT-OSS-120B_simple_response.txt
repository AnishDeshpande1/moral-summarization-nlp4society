SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox publicly rebuked Donald J. Trump’s planned visit to Mexico, declaring that Trump is “not welcome” and warning that his rhetoric demonizes Mexicans. Fox condemned Trump’s wall proposal, his shifting immigration stance, and labeled him a “false prophet” who cannot be trusted to respect either Americans or Mexicans. He accused Trump of using the trip as a desperate political stunt to boost sagging poll numbers and urged U.S. voters to reject him, asserting that Trump lacks the competence to lead a nation or even run a business. In response, Trump pointed to Fox’s earlier invitation, while Fox retorted on Twitter that Trump must apologize to Mexicans and respect Mexico’s sovereignty. The exchange underscores a moral judgment that Trump’s behavior is disrespectful and dangerous, and that Mexico should not tolerate his presence. END OF SUMMARY.
