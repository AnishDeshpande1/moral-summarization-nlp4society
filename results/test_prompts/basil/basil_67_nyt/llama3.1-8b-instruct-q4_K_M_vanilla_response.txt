SUMMARY:
Vicente Fox, former President of Mexico (2000-2006), has expressed strong opposition to Donald Trump's visit to Mexico. Fox stated that the invitation from current President Enrique Peña Nieto was a mistake and that Trump is "not welcome" in Mexico due to his demonization of Mexican people. Fox criticized Trump's plan to build a border wall, saying it would never happen, and called him a "false prophet" who misleads his followers. He also expressed disbelief that Peña Nieto invited Trump for a private meeting, suggesting it was a desperate attempt to improve Trump's sagging poll numbers.

Fox urged Americans not to vote for Trump, stating that he doesn't know how to run a nation or a business. The exchange between Fox and Trump escalated on Twitter, with both leaders trading insults. Trump noted that Fox had initially invited him to visit Mexico, but Fox fired back, reminding Trump of his previous invitation and asking him to show respect.

END OF SUMMARY.
