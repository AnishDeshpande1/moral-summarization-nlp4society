SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox publicly rebuked Donald J. Trump’s upcoming trip to Mexico to meet President Enrique Peña Nieto, declaring that Trump is “not welcome” and should never be trusted. Fox criticized Trump’s border‑wall proposal, his rhetoric against Mexicans, and his shifting immigration policies, labeling him a “false prophet” and questioning his competence to lead. He suggested Peña Nieto’s invitation was a mistake and framed Trump’s visit as a desperate move to boost his poll numbers, urging Americans not to vote for him. Trump responded on Twitter, pointing to Fox’s earlier invitation for a visit, while Fox countered with a tweet demanding Trump apologize to Mexicans and respect the country. END OF SUMMARY.
