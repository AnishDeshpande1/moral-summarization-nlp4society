SUMMARY:
Former Mexican President Vicente Fox strongly rejected Donald Trump's visit to Mexico, calling him "not welcome" in the country. Fox criticized Trump for demonizing Mexicans and his proposals to build a wall on the border and deport undocumented immigrants. He expressed disbelief that current President Enrique Peña Nieto invited Trump for a private meeting, suggesting it was a desperate attempt to improve Trump's poll numbers. Fox urged Americans not to vote for Trump, stating he doesn't know how to run a nation or a business. Fox also accused Trump of being dishonest and disrespecting Mexico.

Fox has been critical of Trump since the beginning of his presidential campaign, calling him a "false prophet" who was misleading his followers. He vowed that Mexico would never pay for the border wall and urged Trump to stop lying about his intentions. The exchange between the two leaders highlights the tense relationship between Trump and many Latin American leaders.

END OF SUMMARY.
