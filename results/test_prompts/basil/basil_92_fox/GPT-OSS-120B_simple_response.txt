SUMMARY: President Trump praised Special Counsel Robert Mueller for issuing a rare statement that contradicted a BuzzFeed report alleging Trump ordered Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about a Moscow‑tower deal. Trump called the BuzzFeed story “a total phony,” a disgrace to the country, journalism, and the mainstream media, warning that the press would take a long time to regain credibility. He said the episode was “a very sad day for journalism, but a great day for our country.” Mueller’s office, unusually, said BuzzFeed’s description of Cohen’s testimony was “not accurate,” without giving details. BuzzFeed stood by its reporting, while Trump’s allies, including Rudy Giuliani, hailed Mueller for “correcting the false story” and accused the media of a hysterical campaign to destroy the President. The clash underscores a moral narrative that frames the press as dishonest and untrustworthy, while casting Trump and the special counsel as defenders of truth. END OF SUMMARY.
