SUMMARY: President Trump praised Special Counsel Robert Mueller’s statement that it was appropriate to dispute the BuzzFeed piece, calling the coverage a disgrace to journalism and saying it has destroyed mainstream media credibility. He said the BuzzFeed story was a phony article and a very sad day for journalism but a great day for the country. The BuzzFeed article claimed that Michael Cohen told Congress to lie about the timing of Trump Tower Moscow dealings and that Trump backed a plan to meet Vladimir Putin. Mueller’s office released a statement saying the description of statements and documents in the article were not accurate. Trump and his lawyer Rudy Giuliani praised the correction, urging the press to stop its hysterical desire to destroy the President. BuzzFeed’s spokesman reconfirmed the reporting, standing by the story and saying they will share more when able. END OF SUMMARY.
