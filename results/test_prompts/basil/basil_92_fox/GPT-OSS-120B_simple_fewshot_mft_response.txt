SUMMARY:
President Trump praised Special Counsel Robert Mueller for issuing a rare statement that contradicted a BuzzFeed report alleging Trump ordered Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about the Trump Tower Moscow talks. Trump denounced the BuzzFeed story and the mainstream media’s coverage as a “disgrace” that has destroyed their credibility, framing the episode as an unjust attack on legitimate authority. The Mueller office, invoking its own authority, said BuzzFeed’s description of Cohen’s testimony was “not accurate,” though it gave no details, positioning the agency as a guardian against false accusations. BuzzFeed stood by its reporting, asserting it had no evidence of inaccuracy, while Trump’s allies framed the dispute as a matter of fairness—defending the President from cheating accusations and portraying the media’s claims as a subversive, dishonest campaign. The clash highlights competing claims to authority and questions of fairness versus cheating in the battle over the truth of the alleged Moscow‑deal lie.

END OF SUMMARY.
