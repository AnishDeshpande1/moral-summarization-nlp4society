SUMMARY:
President Trump defended the FBI's special counsel Robert Mueller, saying that Mueller's statement disputing parts of a BuzzFeed report was "appropriate" and that the BuzzFeed piece was a "disgrace to our country" and "journalism". The report claimed that Trump directed his former lawyer Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about discussions over a proposed Trump Tower project in Moscow. Mueller's office disputed the accuracy of specific statements and characterization of documents in the article, but did not offer further details. Trump also attacked the mainstream media for their coverage of the story, saying it was "disgraceful" and had lost credibility. Meanwhile, BuzzFeed stood by its reporting, citing sources that informed the story, and called on Mueller's office to clarify what they are disputing. The controversy has sparked debate over the role of journalism in investigating presidential scandals.

END OF SUMMARY.
