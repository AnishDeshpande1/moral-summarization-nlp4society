SUMMARY:

President Trump responded to a BuzzFeed news report claiming he directed Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about the Moscow Trump Tower project, calling it a "phony story" and a "disgrace to our country". He appreciated FBI special counsel Robert Mueller's statement disputing parts of the report. Mueller's office said that BuzzFeed's description of specific statements and characterization of documents were not accurate, but did not provide further details. Trump's team had previously denied the claims in the article. The controversy led to calls for investigations and impeachment proceedings from Democrats.

BuzzFeed editor-in-chief Ben Smith stood by the reporting, citing sources who informed it. Mueller's office rarely issues statements disputing press reports, making this a notable move. Trump lawyer Rudy Giuliani praised Mueller's office for "correcting" BuzzFeed's report. The dispute highlights the tension between the media and the Trump administration over the accuracy of news reports.

END OF SUMMARY.
