SUMMARY: President Trump praised Special Counsel Robert Mueller’s statement that disputed key claims in a BuzzFeed report alleging Trump told Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about a Moscow Trump Tower deal. Trump called the BuzzFeed story a “phony” and a disgrace to journalism, saying it damaged mainstream media credibility. The BuzzFeed article, based on two law‑enforcement sources, claimed Cohen told Mueller he was instructed to mislead Congress and that Trump had planned a Russia trip to meet Putin. Mueller’s office unusually issued a statement saying BuzzFeed’s description of statements and documents was “not accurate,” without detailing the errors. Trump and his lawyer Rudy Giuliani welcomed the correction, while BuzzFeed’s editor and spokesperson defended the reporting, asserting confidence in its accuracy and promising further information. END OF SUMMARY.
