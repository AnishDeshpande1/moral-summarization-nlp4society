SUMMARY:
The IRS and Treasury Department announced that legally married same‑sex couples may now **file** joint federal tax returns and **receive** the same benefits as opposite‑sex couples. The ruling follows the Supreme Court’s decision that **overturned** part of the Defense of Marriage Act, ensuring the new policy will **affect** all legally married gay couples nationwide, regardless of state recognition. Treasury Secretary Jacob Lew said the change gives these couples **access** to the **benefits, responsibilities and protections** they **deserve** and treats them **equally** under federal tax law. Human Rights Campaign leaders hailed the move as ending the **denied** status of gay families. Opponents, such as the National Organization of Marriage, decry the action as **lawlessness** and claim the Obama administration is **forcing** same‑sex “marriage” on the public. END OF SUMMARY.
