SUMMARY: 
Donald Trump announced that he is pulling out of his own Newsmax debate, scheduled for December 27th. In a statement, Trump claimed that the Republican Party was asking him to give up his right to run as an independent candidate in exchange for moderating the debate. This move has been met with public ridicule and skepticism, given Trump's earlier consideration of running as a Republican. The decision comes just days after several remaining Republican presidential candidates expressed their unwillingness to participate in the forum.

The cancellation of the debate highlights Trump's perceived self-interest and potential lack of commitment to the democratic process. His original intention to moderate the debate was seen by many as an attempt to gain endorsements and boost his own political ambitions, rather than providing a fair platform for the remaining candidates. This latest development has sparked further criticism of Trump's motivations and integrity.
