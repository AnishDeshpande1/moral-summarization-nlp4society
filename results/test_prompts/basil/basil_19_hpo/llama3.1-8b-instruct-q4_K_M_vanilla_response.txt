SUMMARY:
Donald Trump announced Tuesday that he is pulling out of his own debate scheduled for December 27th, hosted by Newsmax. In a statement, Trump claimed he was withdrawing as moderator because the Republican Party was asking him to give up his right to run as an independent candidate if he participated in the debate. This decision comes just 11 days after Trump initially partnered with Newsmax to host a forum with remaining Republican presidential candidates. The debate had been met with ridicule and skepticism, with many candidates declining to participate. Trump's announcement appears to be a reversal of his earlier stance, as he had said as recently as Monday night that the debate would still take place. With only Newt Gingrich and Rick Santorum committed to attending, it seems unlikely that the event will proceed without Trump's involvement.

END OF SUMMARY.
