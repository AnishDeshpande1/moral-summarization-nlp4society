SUMMARY:

President Trump attacked "Crooked" Hillary Clinton's aide Huma Abedin, calling for the Justice Department to investigate potential security issues related to her handling of classified material. Trump referenced a recent email dump by the State Department, which included at least four classified documents marked as such. He also brought up the case of U.S. Navy Sailor Kristian Saucier, who was imprisoned for taking unauthorized photos inside a nuclear submarine, suggesting a double standard in punishment. Abedin had forwarded sensitive emails to her personal Yahoo account and sent passwords for her government laptop to the same account before it was hacked in 2013. Former FBI Director James Comey previously stated that Abedin forwarded emails to Weiner so Clinton could see them. Trump's tweet comes after the State Department released a batch of Abedin's emails discovered on Weiner's laptop, which reopened the case against Clinton during the 2016 presidential election.

END OF SUMMARY.
