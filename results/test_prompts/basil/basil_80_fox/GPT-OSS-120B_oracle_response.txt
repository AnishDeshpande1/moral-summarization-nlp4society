SUMMARY: President Trump castigated “crooked” Hillary Clinton aide Huma Abedin, accusing her of disregarding basic security protocols and putting classified passwords into the hands of foreign agents. He urged the “Deep State” Justice Department to investigate and act, likening her conduct to the case of sailor Kristian Saucier, who was sentenced to prison for taking unauthorized photos on a submarine. The State Department’s recent email dump revealed at least four documents marked classified, which Abedin had forwarded to her personal Yahoo account and sent passwords for her government laptop to that account in 2009. The Yahoo breach later hacked millions of user accounts. Former FBI Director James Comey testified that Abedin had forwarded emails to her estranged husband, Anthony Weiner, to print for Clinton, a move he described as extremely careless. Trump suggested a double standard in punishment, demanding that the DOJ finally bring charges against Abedin and others involved in the investigation. END OF SUMMARY.
