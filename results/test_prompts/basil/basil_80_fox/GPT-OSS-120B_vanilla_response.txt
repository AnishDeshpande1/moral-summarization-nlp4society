SUMMARY: President Donald Trump attacked former Secretary of State Hillary Clinton’s aide Huma Abedin on Twitter, calling her “crooked” and demanding that the “Deep State” Justice Department investigate alleged security breaches involving classified material. Trump referenced a recent case where Navy sailor Kristian Saucier was jailed for taking unauthorized photos on a nuclear submarine, suggesting a double standard in how officials are treated. The criticism follows a State Department release of emails found on a laptop belonging to Abedin’s estranged husband, Anthony Weiner, which included at least four documents marked classified and showed Abedin forwarding sensitive State Department emails to her personal Yahoo account and sharing passwords. Former FBI Director James Comey previously testified that Abedin likely forwarded emails to Weiner for Clinton’s use, describing Clinton’s handling of classified information as “extremely careless.” Trump’s tweet also called for action against former FBI Director Comey and others. END OF SUMMARY.
