SUMMARY:  
President Donald Trump castigated former Secretary Hillary Clinton’s top aide, Huma Abedin, as “crooked” and demanded that the “Deep State” Justice Department finally hold her accountable for alleged security breaches. Citing a recent State Department email release that showed Abedin allegedly placed classified passwords in the hands of foreign agents and forwarded sensitive State Department messages to her personal Yahoo account, Trump juxtaposed her conduct with the imprisonment of Navy sailor Kristian Saucier for taking forbidden photos on a nuclear submarine, proclaiming “Jail!” He framed the issue as a moral double‑standard: while a low‑ranking sailor was punished, a high‑level political insider escaped consequences. Trump’s tweet called for the DOJ to act not only on Abedin but also on former FBI Director James Comey and others he deems complicit, portraying the episode as a test of integrity for the nation’s institutions. The moral narrative presented is that abuse of classified information by powerful individuals must be met with the same harsh justice applied to ordinary service members, lest the rule of law be undermined.  

END OF SUMMARY.
