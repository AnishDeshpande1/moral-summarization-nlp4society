SUMMARY: Arizona Sheriff Joe Arpaio, the self‑styled “America’s Toughest Sheriff,” endorsed Texas Governor Rick Perry for the Republican presidential nomination, praising Perry’s record on immigration and border security. Arpaio said Perry has done more to combat illegal immigration than any other candidate and will champion and fund full‑time border security operations from Brownsville to San Diego. Both men pledged to put more personnel, fencing and surveillance on the southern border, arguing that a secure border is vital for protecting national security, public safety and jobs. Perry, who faces criticism from the right for his support of DREAM‑age children, promised to make the border safer within a year by putting thousands of federal boots on the ground, strategic fencing and aerial resources to fight border crime. The endorsement highlights their shared commitment to fighting crime, securing the border and protecting America’s interests. END OF SUMMARY.
