**SUMMARY:** Same-sex couples will now be treated the same under federal tax law.
The US government has taken steps to make sure that all legally married couples, regardless of their state of residency, are treated equally under federal tax laws. This includes recognizing same-sex marriages made in the United States or foreign countries. However, there are still some complexities involved, such as different standards used by various states regarding eligibility for benefits and potential delays in implementing these rules.
