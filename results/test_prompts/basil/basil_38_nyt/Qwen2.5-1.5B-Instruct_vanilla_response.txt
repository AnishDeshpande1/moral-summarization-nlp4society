Washington, D.C. - Effective January 2013, all same-sex couples who are legally married will be considered married for federal tax purposes, regardless of where they reside. This includes both joint filings and separate filings, provided the marriage is legal. Same-sex couples will now file jointly or as individual taxpayers, depending on their circumstances. However, the ruling raises concerns among some experts regarding the potential impact on tax liabilities and the need for additional paperwork.
