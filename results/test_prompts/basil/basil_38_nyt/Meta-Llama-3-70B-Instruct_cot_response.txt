The Treasury Department and the Internal Revenue Service have announced that all same-sex couples who are legally married will be recognized as such for federal tax purposes, regardless of the state where they live. This ruling provides certainty and clear guidance for all legally married same-sex couples nationwide, ensuring they are treated equally under federal tax laws.
