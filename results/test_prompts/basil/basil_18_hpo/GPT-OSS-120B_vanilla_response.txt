SUMMARY: Republican presidential hopeful Rick Perry aired a TV ad in Iowa promising to end “Obama’s war on religion.” The ad criticizes the open service of gays and lesbians in the military and bans on school prayer, asserting that faith made America strong. Perry also condemned the Obama administration’s use of foreign aid to promote LGBTQ rights abroad, calling it an attack on religious people. He has purchased $1 million in broadcast time ahead of the Jan. 3 caucuses and plans to bring over 600 influential Texans to Iowa in December. Despite the aggressive cultural‑issue strategy, a Des Moines Register poll shows Perry with only 6 % support among likely Republican caucus voters. END OF SUMMARY.
