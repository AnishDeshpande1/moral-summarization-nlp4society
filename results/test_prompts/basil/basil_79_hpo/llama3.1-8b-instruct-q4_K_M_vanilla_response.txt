SUMMARY:
President Donald Trump defended his former national security adviser, Michael Flynn, by comparing him to Hillary Clinton. Trump claimed that Clinton lied to the FBI multiple times without consequences, while Flynn's lies led to his indictment and ruined his life. This comes after Flynn pleaded guilty on Friday for lying to the FBI in special counsel Robert Mueller's investigation into possible collusion between Trump's campaign and Russia.

Trump and his lawyer, John Dowd, have been trying to distance him from the investigation. However, Flynn's indictment makes it increasingly difficult for Trump to do so. The White House has claimed that the investigations have nothing to do with Trump himself, but Trump's comments suggest otherwise. Additionally, Dowd argued in an interview that Trump cannot obstruct justice because he is the chief law enforcement officer and has every right to express his view on any case.

END OF SUMMARY.
