Donald Trump criticized Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg for speaking out on the presidential race, calling her remarks "highly inappropriate" and suggesting she should apologize to the court. Trump found it unbefitting a member of the high court for Ginsburg to make such comments, saying they were beneath the court's dignity. He also implied that Ginsburg should get off the court as soon as possible. The criticism comes after Ginsburg expressed her fears about what the nation would be like with Trump as president, saying she couldn't imagine what the country and the court would be like in his hands. Trump said he was basing his vice-presidential selection on instinct, rather than fund-raising prowess or potential, and that he had already made up his mind.
