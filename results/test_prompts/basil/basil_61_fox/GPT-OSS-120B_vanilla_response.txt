SUMMARY: Senator Ted Cruz publicly rebuked Donald Trump after a National Enquirer story claimed Cruz had multiple affairs and Trump’s campaign attacked Cruz’s wife, Heidi. Cruz said he will not back Trump if he becomes the GOP nominee, calling the tabloid story “garbage” and blaming Trump and his allies. Trump’s spokeswoman denied involvement, and Trump threatened to “spill the beans” about Cruz’s wife, prompting Cruz to label Trump a coward. The feud escalated when an anti‑Trump super PAC ran ads using a nude photo of Melania Trump from a past GQ shoot, prompting Trump to accuse Cruz of using the image. Cruz clarified the ads were not from his campaign. The personal attacks come amid Trump’s strong delegate lead in the Republican primary, while Cruz faces an uphill battle for the nomination. END OF SUMMARY.
