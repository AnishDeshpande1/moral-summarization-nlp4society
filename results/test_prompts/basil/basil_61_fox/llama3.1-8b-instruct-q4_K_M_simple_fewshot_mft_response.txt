SUMMARY:

Sen. Ted Cruz has withdrawn his support for Donald Trump's presidential campaign, citing the personal attacks on his wife Heidi. Cruz accused Trump of being responsible for the National Enquirer story alleging infidelity and threatened to "spill the beans" about Heidi. Trump had previously retweeted a post comparing an unflattering photo of Heidi to a glamorous photo of Melania Trump. The incident has escalated into a heated feud, with both candidates trading insults on social media. Cruz's decision to abandon his pledge to support Trump comes after Trump's campaign attacked Heidi's mental health and published nude photos of Melania Trump in the National Enquirer. Our Principles, an anti-Trump super PAC, is running ads featuring women criticizing Trump for his derogatory comments about women. The controversy has raised concerns about Trump's behavior towards women and his commitment to party loyalty.

END OF SUMMARY.
