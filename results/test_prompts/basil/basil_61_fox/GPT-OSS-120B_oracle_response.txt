SUMMARY:  
Sen. Ted Cruz has thrown down the gauntlet in a heated war of words with Donald Trump over attacks on their wives. Cruz blamed Trump for a National Enquirer **smear** alleging **extramarital affairs**, calling the story **garbage**, **lies**, and **false**. After Trump’s campaign launched **hits** on Cruz’s wife Heidi, the Texas senator said he will not support a nominee who **attacks** his family, declaring “Donald Trump is not going to be the Republican nominee.” Trump’s spokeswoman dismissed the Enquirer claim as “stupidity on all levels,” while Trump himself threatened to **spill the beans** about Heidi, prompting Cruz to call the president a **coward** and label the threats **classless**. The feud escalated when a super‑PAC ran a meme ad using a naked photo of Melania Trump, prompting Trump to accuse Cruz of **using** the image and to launch further **derogatory** attacks on women. Amid these personal attacks, Cruz’s campaign faces a steep uphill battle against Trump’s delegate lead, and his chances hinge on a contested convention later in the summer.  

END OF SUMMARY.
