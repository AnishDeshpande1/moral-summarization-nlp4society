SUMMARY:

A new political advertisement is causing controversy in Florida, targeting areas with large Jewish populations. The ad features Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu warning about the dangers of a nuclear Iran, with a dramatic soundtrack to emphasize the urgency. The ad's message is clear: the world needs American strength, not apologies, a veiled criticism of President Obama's foreign policy. The group behind the ad, Secure America Now, is run by Republican strategists and has reserved $500,000 of airtime in Florida. The Israeli government was not consulted on the ad and did not approve it. The ad's intention is to stoke anxiety over American policies in the Middle East, implying that President Obama's approach is weak and apologetic. This ad is a clear example of how Israel and Middle East policy can be used as a political tool to sway public opinion.

END OF SUMMARY.
