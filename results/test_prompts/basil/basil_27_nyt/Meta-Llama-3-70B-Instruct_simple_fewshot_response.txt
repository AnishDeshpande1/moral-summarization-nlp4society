SUMMARY:

A new advertisement by Secure America Now, a Republican-led organization, is running in areas of Florida with large Jewish populations, stoking anxiety over American policies in the Middle East. The ad features Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu warning of the risks of a nuclear Iran, with a dramatic soundtrack and an announcer concluding that "the world needs American strength, not apologies." This is a clear swipe at President Obama, whom Republicans have criticized for his foreign policy. The ad has been reserved for $500,000 of airtime in Fort Myers, West Palm Beach, and Miami. Notably, the Israeli government was not consulted on the ad and did not approve it. The moral framing of this article suggests that Secure America Now's actions are misleading and hypocritical, using Netanyahu's words to further their own political agenda while undermining the Israeli government's autonomy.
