**SUMMARY:**  
A new political ad targeting Florida’s large Jewish communities tries to heighten anxiety about U.S. Middle‑East policy by using a clip of Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu warning of the growing risk of a nuclear Iran. Set to a dramatic “Homeland‑style” soundtrack, the ad portrays the situation as increasingly combustible and volatile, accusing the world of offering only apologies and an apologetic stance toward Iran. The unseen announcer declares, “The world needs American strength, not apologies,” a direct swipe at President Obama’s foreign‑policy approach. Produced by Secure America Now, a group run by veteran Republican strategists, the ad has secured $500,000 in airtime across Fort Myers, West Palm Beach, and Miami. An Israeli official confirmed the government was not consulted nor approved the advertisement.
