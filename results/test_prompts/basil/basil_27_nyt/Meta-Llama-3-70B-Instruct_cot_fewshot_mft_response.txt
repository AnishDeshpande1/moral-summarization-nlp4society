SUMMARY:
A new advertisement by Secure America Now, a Republican-backed organization, aims to stoke anxiety over American policies in the Middle East, particularly regarding Iran's nuclear program. The ad features Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu warning of the risks of a nuclear Iran, emphasizing the need for American strength, not apologies. This is a not-so-veiled swipe at President Obama, who has been criticized by Republicans, including Mitt Romney, for his allegedly apologetic foreign policy towards volatile countries like Iran. The ad will run in areas of Florida with large Jewish populations, with a reserved $500,000 of airtime. Notably, the Israeli government was not consulted on the ad and did not approve it. The ad's message is clear: American strength is needed to counter the threats in the region, and apologies will not suffice. END OF SUMMARY.
