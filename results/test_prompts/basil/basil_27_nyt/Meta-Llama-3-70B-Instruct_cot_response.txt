SUMMARY:
A new advertisement targeting Jewish populations in Florida aims to stoke anxiety over American policies in the Middle East. The ad features a news clip of Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu warning of the risks of a nuclear Iran, with a dramatic soundtrack playing in the background. Netanyahu's message is clear: every day that passes, Iran gets closer to nuclear arms, and waiting is not an option. The ad concludes with the line "The world needs American strength, not apologies," a not-so-veiled swipe at President Obama's foreign policy, which has been criticized by Republicans like Mitt Romney as apologetic towards volatile countries like Iran. The group behind the ad, Secure America Now, has reserved $500,000 of airtime in key Florida cities. Notably, the Israeli government was not consulted on the ad and did not approve it. END OF SUMMARY.
