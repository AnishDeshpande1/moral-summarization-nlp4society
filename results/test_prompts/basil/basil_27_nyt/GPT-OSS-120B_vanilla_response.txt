SUMMARY: A new political ad targeting Florida’s large Jewish communities uses a clip of Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu warning that Iran is nearing nuclear capability, accompanied by dramatic music. The ad’s voice‑over claims “the world needs American strength, not apologies,” implicitly criticizing President Obama’s foreign‑policy approach toward Iran. Produced by the Republican‑leaning group Secure America Now, led by veteran strategists Nelson Warfield, John McLaughlin, and former Carter aide Pat Caddell, the campaign has purchased $500,000 in airtime in Fort Myers, West Palm Beach and Miami. Israeli officials say the ad was created without their input or approval. END OF SUMMARY.
