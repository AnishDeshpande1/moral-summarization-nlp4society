SUMMARY: 
President Trump launched a series of Twitter attacks against Kathy Griffin, a comedian who posed for a photo holding a mock-up of his severed head, and Democrats in Congress. Griffin apologized for the stunt, saying she had crossed a line and understood how it offended people. The Trumps, including Melania, criticized Griffin's action as "disturbing" and questioned her sanity. CNN announced that Griffin would no longer host its New Year's Eve program. Additionally, Trump accused Democrats of trying to silence testimony from Carter Page, a former Trump campaign adviser, regarding the investigation into Russian meddling in last year's election.

Trump claimed this was part of a "witch hunt" against him and called for an investigation into alleged collusion between Clinton campaign associates and Obama administration officials. Griffin's actions were widely condemned by politicians on both sides, including the daughter of his 2016 opponent. END OF SUMMARY.
