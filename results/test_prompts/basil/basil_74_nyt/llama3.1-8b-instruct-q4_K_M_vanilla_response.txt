SUMMARY:
President Trump lashed out at Democrats and comedian Kathy Griffin in his latest Twitter blasts. He criticized Griffin for posing with a mock severed head of himself, which sparked protests and led to her being fired from CNN's New Year's Eve program. Griffin apologized, saying she had crossed the line and made a mistake. The first lady, Melania Trump, also weighed in, questioning Griffin's sanity.

Trump also attacked Democrats in Congress, accusing them of trying to silence testimony related to the Russian meddling investigation. He claimed that Carter Page, a former Trump campaign adviser, was being silenced by Democrats who are embarrassed by his potential testimony. Page wrote a letter to the House Intelligence Committee, stating that he wanted to correct false or misleading testimony given by James Comey and John Brennan.

The feud comes after Trump's return from an overseas trip, which saw him largely abstain from social media attacks. END OF SUMMARY.
