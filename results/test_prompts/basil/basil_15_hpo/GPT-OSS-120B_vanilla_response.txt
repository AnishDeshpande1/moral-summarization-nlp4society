SUMMARY: Newsweek’s newest issue features a controversial cover of former Congresswoman Michele Bachmann, titled “THE QUEEN OF RAGE,” showing her against a blue background with a wide‑eyed stare. Although the word “rage” does not appear in the accompanying profile, author Lois Romano critiques the “radical” aspects of the Tea Party movement Bachmann supports. Conservative outlets have condemned the cover as portraying Bachmann as “crazy” and claim media bias against conservatives. This follows previous conservative backlash against Newsweek, including a June cover depicting Mitt Romney in a “Book of Mormon” parody and the 2009 Sarah Palin cover that sparked widespread criticism. The article invites readers to judge whether the cover crosses a line. END OF SUMMARY.
