SUMMARY:  
Newsweek’s newest issue **advertising** a story about Michele Bachmann features a striking cover that many conservatives deem unfair. The picture shows Bachmann against a stark blue backdrop, eyes wide, while the headline **reads** “THE QUEEN OF RAGE.” Although the word “rage” never appears in Lois Romano’s profile, she does criticize the “radical” Tea Party Bachmann backs. Conservative sites have already decried the image as making her look “crazy,” and a blogger asked whether anyone can honestly claim the mainstream media isn’t **biased** against conservatives. This is not the first time Newsweek has been **portrayed** as partisan; earlier covers depicted Mitt Romney as a star of *The Book of Mormon* and, famously, Sarah Palin in 2009. The article invites readers to decide whether the Bachmann cover **crosses a line**.  

END OF SUMMARY.
