A group of nearly 90 professors and administrators at Georgetown University have sent a letter to Representative Paul Ryan, criticizing his misuse of Catholic teaching to defend his budget. The letter argues that Ryan's interpretation of "subsidiarity" is a "rationale for gutting government programs," which is not in line with the doctrine's true meaning.
