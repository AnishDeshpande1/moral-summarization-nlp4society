SUMMARY:

Arizona Sheriff Joe Arpaio, a hardliner on immigration, has endorsed Texas Governor Rick Perry for the Republican presidential nomination. Arpaio praised Perry's record on border security, citing his efforts to combat illegal trafficking of weapons, drugs, and people. Perry has faced criticism for his immigration platform, which opposes deportation of all 11 million undocumented immigrants and a full border fence. However, he promises to "secure the border within a year" if elected president, with increased personnel, strategic fencing, and aerial resources. Arpaio's endorsement lends credibility to Perry's tough-on-crime and border security stance, which prioritizes protecting America's national security, public safety, and jobs. This endorsement is seen as a boost to Perry's campaign, as it highlights his commitment to securing the nation's borders and combating illegal immigration.

END OF SUMMARY.
