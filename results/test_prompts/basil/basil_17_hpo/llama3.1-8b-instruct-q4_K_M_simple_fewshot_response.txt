SUMMARY:

Arizona Sheriff Joe Arpaio, known for his hardline stance on immigration, has endorsed Texas Governor Rick Perry for the Republican presidential nomination. Arpaio praised Perry's record on border security, saying he has done more to combat illegal immigration than any other candidate. Perry has faced criticism from the Right for his support of DREAM children and has proposed increasing border security measures, including strategic fencing and aerial resources. Arpaio's endorsement is seen as a boost to Perry's campaign, lending credibility to his tough-on-crime and border security stance. Perry has stated that he will secure the border within a year by putting thousands more federal boots on the ground, installing strategic fencing, and deploying aerial resources. Arpaio's endorsement comes after he previously backed Mitt Romney in 2008.
