SUMMARY:

Controversial Arizona Sheriff Joe Arpaio has endorsed Texas Governor Rick Perry for the Republican presidential nomination, citing Perry's tough stance on immigration and border issues. Arpaio, a hardliner on immigration, praised Perry's efforts to secure the border, saying he has "done more to secure the border than any other candidate." Perry's plan involves increasing enforcement along the southwest border with Mexico, and he has promised to "secure the border within a year" if elected president. Arpaio commended Perry's border surge operations, which have helped shut down the illegal trafficking of weapons, drugs, and people. The sheriff believes Perry will champion and fund full-time border security operations, making the nation safer. Perry's commitment to fighting border crime, increasing personnel, and implementing strategic fencing and surveillance has earned him Arpaio's support.

END OF SUMMARY.
