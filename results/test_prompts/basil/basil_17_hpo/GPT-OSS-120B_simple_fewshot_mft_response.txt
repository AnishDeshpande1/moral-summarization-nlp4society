SUMMARY:
Arizona Sheriff Joe Arpaio, a notorious hard‑line authority on immigration, announced his official endorsement of Texas Governor Rick Perry for the Republican presidential nomination, positioning Perry as the candidate who will uphold lawful authority on the border. Arpain’s statement praises Perry’s “superior border security record” and promises to fund full‑time, militarized enforcement from Brownsville to San Diego, framing this as a fair response to the cheating of illegal traffickers, drug dealers and undocumented migrants who evade the law. Perry echoed the appeal to authority, condemning Washington bureaucrats for failing to protect the nation’s “southern boundary” and pledging to secure the border within a year with more personnel, fencing and surveillance. The endorsement is presented as a validation of Perry’s commitment to enforce existing rules and to restore fairness by stopping those who subvert immigration laws, while casting his opponents as ineffective or complicit in the cheating of the system.  

END OF SUMMARY.
