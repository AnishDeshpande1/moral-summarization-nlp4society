SUMMARY:
Arizona Sheriff Joe Arpaio officially endorsed Texas Governor Rick Perry for the Republican presidential nomination, praising Perry’s record on immigration as a “tough‑on‑crime” approach that has helped shut down illegal trafficking of weapons, drugs and people. Arpaio argued that the federal government has **failed** to **secure** the southern border and that Perry’s “border surge” operations, including increased **fencing**, **surveillance**, and National Guard deployments, have **protected** communities and **fund** ongoing enforcement. Both men pledged to **fight** border crime, promising to **secure** the border within a year by adding thousands of federal boots on the ground and strategic resources, making the nation **safer**. Perry thanked Arpaio for his support, emphasizing their shared goal of **champion**‑ing stronger border security to **protect** America’s national security, public safety and jobs.  
END OF SUMMARY.
