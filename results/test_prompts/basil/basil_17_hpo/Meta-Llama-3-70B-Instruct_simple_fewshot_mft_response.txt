SUMMARY:

Arizona Sheriff Joe Arpaio, a tough-on-crime advocate, has endorsed Texas Governor Rick Perry for the Republican presidential nomination, citing Perry's superior border security record and plan. Arpaio's endorsement is seen as a credibility boost for Perry on immigration and border issues, particularly after Perry faced criticism for his stance on deportation and border fencing. Perry has promised to secure the border within a year, increasing personnel, fencing, and surveillance to combat border crime and protect national security, public safety, and jobs. The endorsement highlights Perry's commitment to upholding the rule of law and combating illegal immigration, in contrast to Washington politicians and bureaucrats who have failed to secure the border. By supporting Perry, Arpaio is backing a candidate who will champion and fund full-time border security operations, ensuring a fair and safe environment for Americans.

END OF SUMMARY.
