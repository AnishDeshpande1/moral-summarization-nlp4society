Republicans claimed they were following the operative language by skipping obsolete parts that had been superseded by amendment. However, Democratic lawmakers felt that exempting these parts undermined the sacred objective of the exercise, which was meant to show Americans that imperfection is not something to be feared but rather a reason for constant improvement.
