SUMMARY:

Special Counsel Robert Mueller's team disputed BuzzFeed's report that President Donald Trump directed Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about negotiations over a Trump Tower in Moscow. In a rare statement, Mueller's office said that BuzzFeed's "description" of statements and characterization of documents and testimony were "not accurate." However, the statement did not directly dispute the claim that Trump had directed Cohen to lie to Congress. BuzzFeed stood by its reporting, saying it saw no indication that any specific aspect of its story was inaccurate. The controversy has added more confusion over what Mueller's team knows in its ongoing investigation into President Trump's ties to Russia. Some top Democrats have called for serious action, including impeachment, if the allegations prove true.

END OF SUMMARY.
