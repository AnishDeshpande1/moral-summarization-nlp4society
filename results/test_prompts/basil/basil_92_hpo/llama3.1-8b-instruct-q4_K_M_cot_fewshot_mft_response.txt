SUMMARY:
Special counsel Robert Mueller's team disputed BuzzFeed's explosive report that President Donald Trump directed attorney Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about negotiations over a Trump Tower in Moscow. The special counsel's statement added more confusion to the ongoing investigation into Trump's ties to Russia. BuzzFeed stands by its reporting, citing multiple witnesses and internal documents as sources. Top Democrats on Capitol Hill are calling for action against Trump if the allegations prove true, including impeachment or criminal charges. Senator Dianne Feinstein threatened serious consequences, stating that "if true, the president committed a crime and must be held accountable." The House Intelligence Committee has begun searching for witnesses and documents to confirm BuzzFeed's report. Trump denies the accusations, claiming Cohen is lying to reduce his jail time.
