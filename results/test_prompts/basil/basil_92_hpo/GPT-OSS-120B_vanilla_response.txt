SUMMARY:  
Special counsel Robert Mueller’s office issued a rare statement disputing BuzzFeed’s claim that President Donald Trump ordered Michael Cohen to lie to Congress about a Trump Tower Moscow deal. Spokesman Peter Carr said BuzzFeed’s description of statements, documents, and testimony obtained by the investigation was inaccurate, though the statement did not directly refute the allegation that Trump directed Cohen to lie. BuzzFeed stood by its reporting, citing sources who said Cohen admitted the instructions during Mueller interviews. The report sparked a Democratic backlash, with Senate Judiciary Ranking Member Dianne Feinstein and House Intelligence Committee Chair Adam Schiff demanding further investigation and warning of possible impeachment if the claims prove true. Trump denied wrongdoing, accusing Cohen of lying to the special counsel to lessen his sentence.  

END OF SUMMARY.
