SUMMARY:  
In a rare statement Friday night, special counsel Robert Mueller’s team said BuzzFeed’s “description” of statements, documents and testimony about Michael Cohen’s congressional lie was inaccurate. The counsel did not directly dispute that President Trump directed Cohen to lie or that any “orders” were given regarding the alleged Trump Tower Moscow deal, but it challenged the accuracy of the reporting. BuzzFeed stood by its story, saying it had re‑confirmed the sources and urged the special counsel to clarify what it is disputing. Democrats reacted sharply, with Sen. Dianne Feinstein warning that if the president indeed directed Cohen to lie, it would constitute a crime and could trigger impeachment and other serious action. Rep. Adam Schiff announced his committee would secure additional witnesses and documents to follow the evidence to the bottom and determine whether the alleged instructions interfered with the investigation. Trump denied the allegations, accusing Cohen of lying to reduce his jail time.  
END OF SUMMARY.
