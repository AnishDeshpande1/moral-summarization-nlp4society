SUMMARY:

Donald J. Trump stirred up controversy in the Republican presidential primary by escalating a war of words on Twitter against Senator Ted Cruz, his main competitor. A super PAC ad featuring an old nude photo shoot of Melania Trump, sparked Trump's response, which included threats to "spill the beans" on Heidi Cruz's past bout with depression. Cruz denounced the ad and defended his wife, quoting from the movie "The American President." Trump continued to attack, retweeting a side-by-side comparison of the two women.

Cruz finally had enough and called Trump a "sniveling coward," telling him to "leave Heidi alone." The incident raises concerns among party leaders that Trump's behavior will drive away women voters from other Republican candidates. This is reminiscent of Todd Akin's 2012 Senate campaign, where he was criticized for making insensitive comments about rape, leading to his isolation by the Republican Party.

END OF SUMMARY.
