SUMMARY:  
From his Mar‑a‑Lago home, Donald J. Trump **escalated** a Twitter **war** against Senate candidate Ted Cruz by retweeting a **comparison** of a nude photo of his wife Melania with a disparaging image of Cruz’s wife, Heidi. After threatening to “spill the beans” about Heidi’s past depression, Trump posted the side‑by‑side pictures, prompting Cruz to call him a “sniveling coward” and demand he **leave Heidi the hell alone**. Party leaders warned that such **threats** and personal attacks could alienate women voters and damage down‑ballot candidates. The episode recalls the 2012 media backlash over Todd Akin’s **offensive** “legitimate rape” comment, which the GOP isolated to contain the damage. Unlike the Akin case, media criticism of Trump’s conduct has been comparatively muted.  

END OF SUMMARY.
