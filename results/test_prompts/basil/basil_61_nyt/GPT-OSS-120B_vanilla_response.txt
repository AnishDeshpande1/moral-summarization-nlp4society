SUMMARY:  
Donald Trump intensified a Twitter feud with Senate GOP front‑runner Ted Cruz by sharing a “super PAC” ad that juxtaposed a nude photo of his wife Melania with a disparaging image of Cruz’s wife, Heidi, who has a history of depression. Cruz, who has no ties to the PAC, condemned the ad and defended his wife, but Trump continued the attacks, prompting Cruz to call him a “sniveling coward” and demand he stop. Party leaders fear Trump’s behavior could alienate female voters and damage down‑ticket Republican candidates. The article notes past GOP attempts to limit attacks on spouses, referencing the 2012 backlash against Senator Todd Akin’s “legitimate rape” comment, and observes that media criticism of Trump’s remarks has been relatively muted compared to earlier incidents.  

END OF SUMMARY.
