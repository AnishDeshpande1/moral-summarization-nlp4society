SUMMARY:

Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu is starring in a new ad in Florida, a key battleground state, to make Iran a major campaign issue. He expresses frustration at the Obama administration's failure to establish "red lines" for reining in Iran's rogue nuclear weapons program. Netanyahu warns that every day that passes, Iran gets closer to a nuclear bomb, and the world needs American strength, not apologies. The ad, paid for by Secure America Now, aims to influence heavily-Jewish markets, including Miami. Meanwhile, the Republican Jewish Coalition has launched a separate $5 million TV advertising blitz aimed at Jewish voters in swing states, highlighting the "buyer's remorse" felt by many in the Jewish community who are disillusioned with Obama's economic policies and his policies toward Israel. Netanyahu urges Obama to take a tougher line with Iran, emphasizing the need to prevent Iran from getting nuclear weapons.

END OF SUMMARY.
