SUMMARY:
Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has appeared in a new ad in Florida, urging the US to take a tougher line with Iran over its nuclear program. Netanyahu expresses frustration at the Obama administration's failure to establish "red lines" for reining in Iran, calling it a "rogue" state. He warns that every day that passes, Iran gets closer to a nuclear bomb, and the world needs American strength, not apologies. The ad, paid for by Secure America Now, aims to make Iran a major campaign issue in the presidential election. Meanwhile, the Republican Jewish Coalition has launched a separate $5 million TV advertising blitz, highlighting the "buyer's remorse" felt by many Jewish voters who are disillusioned with Obama's economic policies and his policies toward Israel. Netanyahu's message is clear: the world needs American strength to prevent Iran from getting nuclear weapons.
