SUMMARY: Rep. Alexandria Ocasio‑Cortez defended fellow freshman Rep. Rashida Tlaib after Tlaib’s expletive‑laden comments calling for Trump’s impeachment. Ocasio‑Cortez denounced Republican hypocrisy, pointing out the GOP’s “faux‑outrage” over a curse word while dismissing Trump’s lewd “locker‑room talk” about assault as merely “saying” something. She warned that the GOP had lost the entitlement to police women’s behavior. Pelosi, while disapproving of Tlaib’s language, refused to engage in censorship, noting the president’s own comments set the tone. Trump called Tlaib’s remarks disgraceful and said she had dishonored herself and her family. Tlaib doubled down, tweeting she will always speak truth to power amid what she calls a constitutional crisis. The clash highlights the partisan scandalizing of language and the ongoing push to impeach the president.
