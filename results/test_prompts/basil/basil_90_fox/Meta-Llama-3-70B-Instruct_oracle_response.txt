SUMMARY:

Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez slammed the GOP for hypocrisy after they criticized Rep. Rashida Tlaib's expletive-laced comments in favor of impeaching President Trump. Ocasio-Cortez accused the GOP of having a double standard, saying they downplayed Trump's admission of sexual assault on tape as "locker room talk" but were scandalized by Tlaib's curse word. She tweeted that the GOP lost entitlement to policing women's behavior a long time ago. Ocasio-Cortez vowed to support Tlaib, saying "I got your back" and that the Bronx and Detroit ride together. Meanwhile, House Republicans, including Minority Leader Kevin McCarthy, fumed over Tlaib's comments, but Speaker Nancy Pelosi downplayed them, saying Trump's own language had started the heated rhetoric in Washington. Trump called Tlaib's comments "disgraceful" and said she "dishonored herself" and her family. Tlaib doubled down, tweeting that she will always speak truth to power and that this is not just about Trump, but about all of us in the face of this constitutional crisis.

END OF SUMMARY.
