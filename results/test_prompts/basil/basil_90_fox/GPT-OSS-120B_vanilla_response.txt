SUMMARY:  
Rep. Alexandria Ocasio‑Cortez defended fellow freshman Rep. Rashida Tlaib after Tlaib’s profanity‑filled video calling for President Trump’s impeachment. Ocasio‑Cortez criticized Republicans for a double standard, pointing out their dismissal of Trump’s 2005 “locker‑room talk” while condemning Tlaib’s language. She pledged support, tweeting “I got your back @RashidaTlaib.” Tlaib’s remarks, recorded after she wrote an op‑ed urging impeachment, included the line “we’re gonna impeach the motherf***er.” House Republicans, led by Kevin McCarthy, demanded action, while Speaker Nancy Pelosi said she disapproved of the language but refused to censor members, noting Trump’s own vulgarity. Trump called Tlaib’s comments “disgraceful” and said she dishonored herself and her family. Tlaib doubled down, asserting she will “always speak truth to power” and framing the issue as a broader constitutional crisis.  

END OF SUMMARY.
