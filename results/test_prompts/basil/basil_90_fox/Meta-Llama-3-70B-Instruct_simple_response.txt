SUMMARY:

Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez defended her fellow freshman Rep. Rashida Tlaib after Tlaib's expletive-laced comments in favor of impeaching President Trump. Ocasio-Cortez accused the GOP of hypocrisy, pointing out that they downplayed Trump's admission of sexual assault on tape as "locker room talk" but are now outraged by Tlaib's curse word. She tweeted that the GOP has lost the right to police women's behavior and vowed to support Tlaib. House Republicans, including Minority Leader Kevin McCarthy, condemned Tlaib's comments, but Speaker Nancy Pelosi downplayed the incident, citing Trump's own inflammatory language. Trump himself called Tlaib's comments "disgraceful" and said she "dishonored herself" and her family. Tlaib refused to back down, tweeting that she would "always speak truth to power" and that the issue is about rising to the challenge of a constitutional crisis.

END OF SUMMARY.
