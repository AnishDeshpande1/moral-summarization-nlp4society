Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez defended her fellow freshman Rep. Rashida Tlaib's controversial comment about impeaching President Trump, accusing the GOP of hypocrisy. Ocasio-Cortez vowed to support Tlaib, saying "the Bronx and Detroit ride together." Tlaib's comment sparked criticism from House Republicans, but House Speaker Nancy Pelosi downplayed the controversy, drawing parallels with Trump's own history of offensive remarks. Trump responded, calling Tlaib's comments "disgraceful" and saying she "dishonored herself." Ocasio-Cortez's defense of Tlaib highlights the double standard in Washington, where Republicans are quick to condemn Democrats for their language while excusing Trump's own transgressions.
