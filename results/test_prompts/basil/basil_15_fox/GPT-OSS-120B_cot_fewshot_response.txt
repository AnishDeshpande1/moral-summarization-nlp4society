**SUMMARY:**
Rep. Michele Bachmann declined to confront Newsweek over its “Queen of Rage” cover, which the National Organization for Women’s president Terry O’Neill called **sexist** and **misogynistic**. O’Neill cited Gloria Steinem’s test—no one calls a man “King of Rage”—and argued the magazine was casting Bachmann as a “nut job,” a treatment not applied to male politicians like Howard Dean. Newsweek defended the photo as one of several, some flattering, images. While Bachmann said she hadn’t seen the cover, conservatives such as Brent Bozell labeled the portrayal **dishonest** and part of a long‑standing **unfair** bias against conservatives in mainstream media. O’Neill added that such attacks discourage women from running for office, even as she vows to defeat Bachmann.  
END OF SUMMARY.
