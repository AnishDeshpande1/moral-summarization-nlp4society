SUMMARY:

Rep. Michele Bachmann has declined to comment on Newsweek's cover story, which features an unflattering photo of her and labels her "The Queen of Rage." The National Organization for Women (NOW) has called out the magazine for its sexist depiction of Bachmann, arguing that the cover would not be used for a male candidate. NOW President Terry O'Neill said the magazine is essentially casting Bachmann as a "nut job" and that the cover is a "misogynistic attack." O'Neill emphasized that her organization, which disagrees with Bachmann on most issues, is standing up for her because they want to encourage women to run for office. Media critics have also weighed in, with Brent Bozell of the Media Research Center calling the cover "sheer journalistic dishonesty." Bozell advised conservatives to steer clear of these magazines, which cannot be trusted, and to demand editorial oversight. END OF SUMMARY.
