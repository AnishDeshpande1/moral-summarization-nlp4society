SUMMARY:
The National Organization for Women (NOW) President Terry O'Neill condemned Newsweek's cover story on Rep. Michele Bachmann, calling it "sexist" and "misogynistic". O'Neill pointed out that the title "The Queen of Rage" would not be used to describe a man in a similar situation, implying that Newsweek is unfairly casting Bachmann as a "nut job". The magazine's defense of the photo, stating that several images taken of Bachmann appeared similarly, was rejected by NOW. Bachmann herself declined to comment on the cover, but O'Neill emphasized that NOW's support for her is rooted in their desire to encourage women to run for office without facing misogynistic attacks. Brent Bozell, head of the Media Research Center and ForAmerica, accused Newsweek of "journalistic dishonesty" and urged conservatives to demand editorial oversight from such venues.

END OF SUMMARY.
