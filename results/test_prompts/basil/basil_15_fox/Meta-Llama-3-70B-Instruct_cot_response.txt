SUMMARY:
Rep. Michele Bachmann has been targeted by Newsweek with a cover story titled "The Queen of Rage," accompanied by an unflattering photo. The National Organization for Women (NOW) President Terry O'Neill has called the cover "sexist" and "misogynistic," arguing that the magazine would not treat a male candidate similarly. O'Neill believes the depiction reduces Bachmann to a "nut job" and implies she is "crazy." Newsweek has defended the photo, but critics like Brent Bozell of the Media Research Center argue that the magazine has a history of "journalistic dishonesty" when it comes to conservatives. Despite disagreeing with Bachmann on most issues, NOW is standing up for her against these attacks, as they want to encourage women to run for office. END OF SUMMARY.
