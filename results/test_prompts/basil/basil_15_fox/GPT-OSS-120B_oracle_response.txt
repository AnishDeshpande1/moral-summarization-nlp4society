SUMMARY:  
Rep. Michele Bachmann refused to engage with Newsweek’s “Queen of Rage” cover, which featured an unflattering close‑up photo. The National Organization for Women’s president, Terry O’Neill, called the cover sexist and misogynistic, arguing that no man would be labeled “King of Rage” and that such a portrayal casts a serious female presidential contender as a nut. She warned that if magazines get away with this, good women may not run for office. Newsweek defended the image, noting other flattering photos were also posted. Conservative media critic Brent Bozell dismissed the cover as journalistic dishonesty, urging oversight of such outlets. Bachmann said she had not seen the cover but would have to look at it.  

END OF SUMMARY.
