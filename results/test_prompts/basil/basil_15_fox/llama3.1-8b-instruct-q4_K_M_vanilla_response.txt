SUMMARY:

Rep. Michele Bachmann has been featured on the cover of Newsweek magazine as "The Queen of Rage," accompanied by an unflattering photo. However, critics are calling out the magazine for its depiction, labeling it sexist and misogynistic. National Organization for Women President Terry O'Neill said that a simple test would determine if the magazine would use such language to describe a man: "Who has ever called a man 'The King of Rage?'". Newsweek defended the photo, saying other images taken of Bachmann appeared similar, but posted additional flattering photos on its website. The backlash has sparked debate about journalistic ethics and the treatment of conservative politicians in mainstream media. Even O'Neill's organization, which disagrees with Bachmann on many issues, is standing up to defend her against what they see as a sexist attack.

END OF SUMMARY.
