SUMMARY: All nine Republican members of the House Intelligence Committee have urged Chairman Rep. Adam Schiff (D‑Calif.) to resign, accusing him of perpetuating a false narrative about Trump‑Russia collusion despite the Mueller report’s inconclusive findings. Schiff continues to assert that “undoubtedly there is collusion” and calls for further counterintelligence investigation, prompting criticism from GOP lawmakers who claim his statements are part of a media campaign and link him to leaked information. President Donald Trump also demanded Schiff’s resignation, labeling his actions unlawful. Republicans argue Schiff’s conduct is incompatible with his oversight duties, while Democrats press for the full release of the Mueller report. Schiff responded by reaffirming his belief in collusion and condemning the situation as immoral, unethical, unpatriotic, and corrupt. END OF SUMMARY.
