SUMMARY:  
All nine Republican members of the House Intelligence Committee have demanded that Rep. Adam Schiff resign as chairman, accusing him of using his platform to **promote** a **misleadingly** false **narrative** about **collusion** between the Trump **campaign** and Russia. In a Thursday letter, the GOP members said Schiff’s **willingness** to **continue** **investigating** and to **leak** information shows a **lying**, **leaking** agenda that **compromises** the Committee’s duty to **provide** proper **oversight**. They linked him to a “well‑orchestrated media campaign” and claimed his **actions** are **immoral**, **unethical**, **unpatriotic** and **corrupt**, asserting that Mueller’s report **conclusively refutes** Schiff’s **statements** about a **crime**. Schiff retorted that it is **immoral**, **unethical**, **unpatriotic** and **corrupt** to ignore what he sees as evidence of **collusion** and to ignore further **investigation** into possible **compromise** by a hostile foreign power. President Trump also called for Schiff’s resignation, urging he be forced out of Congress.  

END OF SUMMARY.
