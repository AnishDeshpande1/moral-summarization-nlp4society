**SUMMARY:**  
All nine Republican members of the House Intelligence Committee have demanded that Rep. Adam Schiff resign as chairman, accusing him of leading a “well‑orchestrated media campaign” that promotes a demonstrably **false** narrative about Trump‑Russia collusion. They claim his continued public statements are **alarming**, constitute **lying**, and amount to a breach of his duty to oversee intelligence. President Trump echoed the call, tweeting that Schiff should be forced out of Congress for “unlawfully **lying** and **leaking**” information. Schiff, however, doubled down, calling the lack of a definitive exoneration “**immoral**, **unethical**, **unpatriotic**, and **corrupt**,” and insisting the investigation must continue to determine whether the president or his associates are compromised by a hostile foreign power. The dispute centers on differing interpretations of Special Counsel Mueller’s report, which neither fully clears nor directly implicates the president.
