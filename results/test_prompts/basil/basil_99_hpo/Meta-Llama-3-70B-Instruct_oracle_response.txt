SUMMARY:

Nine Republican members of the House Intelligence Committee are demanding Rep. Adam Schiff's resignation as chairman, accusing him of promoting a "demonstrably false narrative" about the Trump-Russia connection. Despite the Mueller report's findings, Schiff maintains that there was "undoubtedly" collusion between the Trump campaign and Russia. The GOP committee members claim Schiff's "willingness to continue to promote" this narrative is "alarming" and that his actions are "incompatible" with his duty as chairman. They also accuse him of "unlawfully lying and leaking" information. Trump himself has called for Schiff's resignation, echoing the GOP committee members' claims. Schiff, however, remains resolute, calling the investigation's findings "immoral," "unethical," and "unpatriotic," and vowing to continue investigating counterintelligence issues. The dispute highlights the deepening partisan divide over the Russia investigation.

END OF SUMMARY.
