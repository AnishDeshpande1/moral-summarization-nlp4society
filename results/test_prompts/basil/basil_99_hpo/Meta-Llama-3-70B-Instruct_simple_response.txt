SUMMARY:

The nine Republican members of the House Intelligence Committee are calling for Rep. Adam Schiff (D-Calif.) to resign as chairman due to his continued claims of collusion between the Trump campaign and Russia, despite the Mueller investigation finding no direct evidence. The Republicans accuse Schiff of promoting a "false narrative" and leaking information, which they deem "alarming" and "unlawful". Trump himself has also called for Schiff's resignation, accusing him of "lying and leaking". However, Schiff stands by his assertions, calling it "immoral", "unethical", and "unpatriotic" to dismiss the investigation's findings, which indicted over 30 people, including those close to Trump. Schiff vows to continue investigating counterintelligence issues, suggesting that the president or those around him may be compromised by a hostile foreign power.

END OF SUMMARY.
