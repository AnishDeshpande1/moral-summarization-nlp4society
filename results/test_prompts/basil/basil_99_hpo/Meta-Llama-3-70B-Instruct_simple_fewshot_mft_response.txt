SUMMARY:

House Republicans are demanding Rep. Adam Schiff's resignation as chairman of the Intelligence Committee, accusing him of lying and leaking information about the Russia investigation. Despite Special Counsel Robert Mueller's probe finding no evidence of Trump-Russia collusion, Schiff maintains that there is "undoubtedly" collusion and that the president or his associates may have been compromised by a hostile foreign power. The Republicans' attack on Schiff is a blatant attempt to undermine his authority and subvert the truth. By accusing Schiff of promoting a "false narrative," they are trying to discredit his pursuit of accountability and fairness. The president's tweet calling for Schiff's resignation is a clear example of loyalty to his own interests over the rule of law. The Democrats are standing by Schiff, recognizing that his questions about the investigation are legitimate and necessary. The moral framing of this issue is clear: Schiff's commitment to uncovering the truth is being attacked by those who seek to protect the president at all costs, even if it means subverting the rule of law and undermining the authority of the Intelligence Committee.
