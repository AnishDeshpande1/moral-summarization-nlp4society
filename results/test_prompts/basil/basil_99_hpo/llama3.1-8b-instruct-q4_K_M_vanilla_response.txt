SUMMARY:

All nine Republican members of the House Intelligence Committee have called on Rep. Adam Schiff (D-Calif.) to resign as chairman over his comments about the Russia investigation. They accused him of promoting a "demonstrably false narrative" and linking him to leaked information about the investigation. The Republicans stated that special counsel Robert Mueller's report "conclusively refutes" Schiff's assertions, but this is somewhat misleading as Mueller's report does not exonerate Trump either. Trump himself called on Schiff to resign, accusing him of "unlawfully lying and leaking" information. Schiff responded by doubling down on his questions about the investigation, calling it "immoral", "unpatriotic", and evidence of collusion. The Democrats are pushing for the full report to be released publicly.

END OF SUMMARY.
