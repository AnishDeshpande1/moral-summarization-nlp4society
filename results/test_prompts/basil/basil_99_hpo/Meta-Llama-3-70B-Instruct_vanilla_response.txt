SUMMARY:

Nine Republican members of the House Intelligence Committee are calling for Rep. Adam Schiff (D-Calif.) to resign as chairman due to his public comments about the Russia investigation. Despite the investigation finding no direct evidence of collusion between Trump's campaign and Russia, Schiff maintains that there was collusion and plans to continue investigating counterintelligence issues. The GOP committee members accuse Schiff of promoting a "demonstrably false narrative" and linking him to leaked information about the investigation. President Trump also called for Schiff's resignation, accusing him of "unlawfully lying and leaking" information. Schiff responded by doubling down on his questions about the investigation, calling it "immoral," "unethical," "unpatriotic," and "corrupt." The Democrats are calling for the full report to be released publicly, as Mueller's findings do not exonerate Trump.

END OF SUMMARY.
