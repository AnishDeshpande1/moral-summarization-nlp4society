SUMMARY:

House Republicans are demanding Rep. Adam Schiff's resignation as chairman of the Intelligence Committee, accusing him of promoting a "false narrative" about Trump-Russia collusion despite the conclusion of Special Counsel Robert Mueller's probe. Schiff has maintained that there is "undoubtedly" collusion and has vowed to continue investigating counterintelligence issues. The Republicans' letter claims that Mueller's findings "conclusively refute" Schiff's assertions, but this is misleading, as the report neither concludes nor exonerates Trump. The GOP's attack on Schiff is a clear attempt to discredit his pursuit of truth and accountability, and to protect the president at all costs. Schiff has doubled down on his questions about the investigation, calling the Trump campaign's actions "immoral," "unethical," "unpatriotic," and "corrupt." The moral framing of this issue is clear: Schiff's commitment to oversight and accountability is being attacked by those who seek to undermine the legitimacy of the investigation. END OF SUMMARY.
