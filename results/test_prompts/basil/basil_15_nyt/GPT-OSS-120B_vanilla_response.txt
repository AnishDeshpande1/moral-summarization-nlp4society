SUMMARY: Newsweek’s latest cover features Republican presidential hopeful Michele Bachmann with a wide‑eyed stare and the caption “The Queen of Rage,” sparking criticism from conservative bloggers who call it biased and from the National Organization for Women, which labels it sexist and unprecedented for a man. Newsweek defended the image by releasing additional photos showing Bachmann in similar poses. The cover accompanies a Lois Romano profile of Bachmann’s role in the Tea Party. This is not Newsweek’s first controversial cover; past issues have drawn fire for a Sarah Palin photo in shorts and a CGI image of Duchess Catherine alongside Princess Diana. END OF SUMMARY.
