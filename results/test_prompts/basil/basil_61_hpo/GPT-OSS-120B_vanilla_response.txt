SUMMARY: Senator Ted Cruz (R‑Texas) publicly condemned Donald Trump, accusing him of using the National Enquirer and his political allies to spread false rumors. The tabloid earlier this week published a story alleging Cruz had extramarital affairs with five women, which Cruz denied as “garbage.” The controversy intensified after a heated CNN exchange where Boston Herald columnist Adriana Cohen accused Cruz’s former communications director Amanda Carpenter of also having an affair with Cruz. Trump issued a statement saying he was unaware of the Enquirer story, had no involvement, and dismissed Cruz as “Lyin’ Ted.” The National Enquirer, known for sensational reporting but also for breaking major scandals (e.g., John Edwards, Tiger Woods), has a parent company, American Media Inc., which denied any Trump influence on the story and defended its editorial independence. END OF SUMMARY.
