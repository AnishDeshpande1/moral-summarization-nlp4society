Sen. Ted Cruz (R-Texas) denied allegations of extramarital affairs with five women, published in the National Enquirer. Trump was accused by Cruz of being behind the story, but Trump claimed he had nothing to do with it and did not know about it beforehand. The Enquirer's parent company also denied that the story was influenced by Trump backers. A statement from Cruz called the allegations "garbage," while Trump responded by saying that the Enquirer has a history of breaking sex scandals, including those involving O.J. Simpson and John Edwards.

Trump also took a shot at Cruz, calling him "Lyin' Ted" in his statement. The exchange between Trump and Cruz is seen as an escalation of their feud during the Republican presidential primary. The National Enquirer has been criticized for its tabloid journalism, but it has also broken several major sex scandals over the years.
