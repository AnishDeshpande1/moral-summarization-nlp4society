SUMMARY:
Sen. Ted Cruz (R-Texas) accused Donald Trump of using the National Enquirer to spread false rumors about his personal life, including allegations of extramarital affairs with five women. Cruz vehemently denied the claims, calling them "garbage." Trump responded by stating he had no involvement in the story and that it was Cruz's problem alone. However, some have questioned the timing and tone of Trump's response, suggesting he may be trying to deflect attention from his own controversies.

The National Enquirer has a reputation for breaking major sex scandals, but its integrity has also been called into question. A spokesman for the Enquirer denied that the story was influenced by Trump backers, stating that only reporters and editors influence their reporting. The exchange highlights the tensions between Cruz and Trump, as well as the role of tabloid journalism in politics.
