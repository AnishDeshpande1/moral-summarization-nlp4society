SUMMARY:
Representative Bob Etheridge, a Democratic congressman from a conservative North Carolina district, issued a public apology after a video showed him forcibly grabbing a young man’s hand, arm and neck while the man asked whether he fully supported President Obama’s agenda. Etheridge expressed “deep and profound regret” and pledged to treat all viewpoints with respect and promote civil discourse, acknowledging that partisan intrusions do not justify a harmful response. The incident, captured by a second camera and widely circulated by conservative outlets such as Andrew Breitbart and the NRCC, highlighted the physical harm inflicted on the student and sparked criticism that Etheridge’s conduct was unbecoming of a member of Congress. While the congressman faces a re‑election challenge, the focus of the coverage remains on the damage caused by his aggressive reaction and the need for more compassionate, respectful political engagement.  

END OF SUMMARY.
