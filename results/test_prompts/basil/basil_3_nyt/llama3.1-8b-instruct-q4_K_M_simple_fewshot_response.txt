SUMMARY:

Representative Bob Etheridge, a Democrat from North Carolina, has apologized for physically confronting a young man who asked him about his support for President Obama's agenda. A video recorded the incident, showing Etheridge grabbing the young man's wrist and neck as he demanded to know who he was. Etheridge expressed regret for his actions, saying that no matter how partisan or difficult things get, there is no excuse for his response. The incident has sparked criticism from Republicans, with one calling his conduct "unbecoming of a member of Congress." As a seven-term House member representing a conservative district, Etheridge faces a re-election challenge, but his apology may help mitigate some of the damage.
