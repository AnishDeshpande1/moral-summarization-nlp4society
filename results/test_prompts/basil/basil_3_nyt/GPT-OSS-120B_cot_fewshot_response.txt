Representative Bob Etheridge, a Democrat from North Carolina, issued a statement saying he “deeply and profoundly regret” his reaction when a young man asked if he fully supports the Obama agenda. He apologized, emphasizing that, throughout his many years of service, he has always tried to treat people of all viewpoints with respect. Etheridge condemned the “intrusive and partisan” tone of today’s politics, noting that it does not justify a “poor” response. He pledged to promote a civil public discourse and warned that his conduct was “unbecoming of a member of Congress.” Republican spokesman Jon Thompson said Etheridge “has lost it,” calling his conduct unbecoming. The incident, captured on video and circulated by conservative outlets, comes as Etheridge faces a re‑election challenge in a district that voted for Obama by a narrow margin.
