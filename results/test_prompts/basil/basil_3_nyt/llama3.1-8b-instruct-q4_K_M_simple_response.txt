SUMMARY:

Representative Bob Etheridge (D-NC) apologized for his behavior in a video that went viral on Monday. In the video, he was seen grabbing a young man by the hand, arm, and neck after being asked about President Obama's agenda. The incident occurred on a sidewalk when the young man approached Etheridge with a camera and asked him if he supported the president's policies. Despite his apology, critics are calling for him to be held accountable for his actions. Republicans have seized on the video as an example of Democratic hypocrisy, saying that Etheridge's behavior is unbecoming of a member of Congress.

Etheridge's district is considered conservative and he faces a re-election challenge. The incident has sparked debate about the level of civility in politics and whether it is acceptable for politicians to engage in aggressive behavior when confronted by constituents or media. Etheridge stated that he will continue to work towards promoting civil public discourse.

END OF SUMMARY.
