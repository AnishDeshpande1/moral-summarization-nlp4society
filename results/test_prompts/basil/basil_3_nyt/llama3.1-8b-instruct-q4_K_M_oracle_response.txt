SUMMARY: Representative Bob Etheridge apologized for his actions, admitting that he lost respect for the young man who approached him on the sidewalk to ask about his support for President Obama's agenda. A video shows Etheridge grabbing the young man by the hand, arm, and neck as he repeatedly asked for identification. The congressman claimed that this behavior was not in line with his commitment to promoting a civil public discourse. The incident has sparked criticism from Republicans, who described his conduct as "unbecoming of a member of Congress." Etheridge's actions have also raised concerns about the treatment of citizens by those in power and the importance of respecting differing viewpoints. His apology acknowledged that he had failed to live up to these expectations and promised to continue working towards promoting a more respectful dialogue. END OF SUMMARY.
