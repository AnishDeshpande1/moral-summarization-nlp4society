SUMMARY:

Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg faced criticism from both sides of the aisle for her comments on presumptive GOP nominee Donald Trump, calling him a "faker" who is unqualified to be president. Trump responded with a tweet saying that Ginsburg's mind was "shot" and should resign. Republican leaders, including Senate Majority Leader Mitch McConnell and House Speaker Paul Ryan, echoed Trump's criticism, saying that Ginsburg's comments were "inappropriate" and raised questions about the objectivity of the Supreme Court.

Even some Democratic lawmakers expressed concern, with Sen. Chris Murphy stating that it was unusual for a Supreme Court justice to make such comments in public. Mainstream media outlets also chimed in, criticizing Ginsburg for politicizing the court. However, Vermont Sen. Bernie Sanders came out in support of Ginsburg's comments, agreeing that Trump is an opportunist who lies frequently.

The incident highlights the challenges faced by Supreme Court justices in maintaining their impartiality while navigating the complexities of politics. END OF SUMMARY.
