SUMMARY: Republican senators intensified calls for RNC Chairman Michael Steele to explain and apologize for his on‑camera remark that Afghanistan is a “war of Obama’s choosing” and may not be winnable. Conservatives, including Sen. Jim DeMint and Sen. John McCain, condemned the comment as inaccurate and demanded an apology to the troops, urging Steele to refocus on the upcoming election. Steele issued a statement affirming the necessity of winning the war on terror and emailed RNC members to clarify his remarks, but the backlash persisted. Weekly Standard editor Bill Kristol, who led the push for Steele’s resignation, reiterated that Steele should still step down. END OF SUMMARY.
