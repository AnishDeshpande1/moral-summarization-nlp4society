**SUMMARY:**
Paul Ryan went on the offensive, accusing President Obama of “raiding” Medicare and turning the treasured entitlement into a “piggy bank” that funds ObamaCare. In a Fox News interview and a new TV ad, Ryan claimed the Romney‑Ryan plan will **protect**, **strengthen** and **preserve** Medicare for current and near‑retirees, offering a **clear contrast** to what he called Obama’s **damaging**, **dishonest** and **hypocritical** approach. He argued the Obama administration’s **cuts** are **cheating** seniors, while the Republican budget’s **savings** will **restore** the **guarantee** of benefits. The Obama campaign rebuked the ad as **hypocritical** and **dishonest**, insisting the Republican plan would **end** Medicare as it exists, leaving seniors with only a voucher. Both sides framed the debate in moral terms of **Authority** (who decides Medicare’s future), **Care** (protecting seniors) and **Fairness** (accusing each other of **cheating** and **raiding** the system). **END OF SUMMARY.**
