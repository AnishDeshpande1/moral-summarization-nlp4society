SUMMARY:  
FBI Deputy Director David Bowdich **took** the unusual step of **firing** Special Agent Peter Strzok, ignoring the Office of Professional Responsibility’s recommendation of a 60‑day suspension and demotion. Strzok’s attorney Aitan Goelman called the **decision** a **departure** from normal Bureau practice and argued it **punishes** Strzok for protected political speech, thereby **harm**‑ing the nation and **weakening** democracy. The move follows the Justice Department inspector general’s **failed** attempt to prove that Strzok’s anti‑Trump texts **impacted** investigative **considerations**, though the report noted a message that seemed to **take** official action to **impact** the election. Critics say the firing reflects political pressure rather than a fair, independent review. The episode has drawn praise from President Trump, who urged that the investigations into his campaign be **dropped** and the Clinton probe be **re‑done**.  

END OF SUMMARY.
