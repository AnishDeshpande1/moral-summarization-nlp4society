SUMMARY: Mary Cheney, the openly lesbian daughter of former Vice President Dick Cheney, publicly criticized her sister Elizabeth Cheney’s opposition to same‑sex marriage on Facebook. Mary emphasized that “freedom means freedom for everyone” and called for equal marriage rights for all families. Elizabeth, a Republican Senate candidate in Wyoming, had earlier stated she is “not pro‑gay marriage” and believes the issue should be decided by voters at the state level, not by judges or legislators. Mary responded, urging supporters to “like and share” her post. Elizabeth is running against incumbent Senator Mike Enzi in the 2014 Republican primary. Their father, Dick Cheney, has voiced support for same‑sex marriage and also favors state‑by‑state decision‑making on the issue. END OF SUMMARY.
