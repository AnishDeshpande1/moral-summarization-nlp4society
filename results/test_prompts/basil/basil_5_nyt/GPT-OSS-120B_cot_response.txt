At its Kansas City convention, the NAACP passed a resolution urging Tea Party groups to purge **racist** elements and expel **bigots** from their ranks. President Benjamin Todd Jealous warned that **silence** in the face of **hate** and **violence** makes the movement complicit, insisting that increased **power** and **public** visibility bring a duty to act **responsibly**. He demanded the Tea Party make it **clear** there is **no place** for **bigots** and that “enough is enough.” Tea Party leaders, including Sarah Palin, rejected the call, labeling the NAACP’s move a **partisan** **attack** and urging the IRS to review its tax‑exempt status. The dispute follows accusations of **hate**, **epithet**‑laden protests and even **spitting** incidents directed at Black members of Congress.
