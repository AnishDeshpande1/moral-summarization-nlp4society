Governor Rick Perry aired a new TV ad aimed at hard‑right conservatives, proclaiming “I’m not ashamed to admit I’m a Christian” and condemning what he calls “Obama’s war on religion.” He argues it is wrong that gays can serve openly in the military while kids cannot celebrate Christmas or pray in school, and vows to fight “liberal attacks on our religious heritage.” Perry repeats that Faith made America strong and can make it strong again.
