SUMMARY: Governor Rick Perry aired a TV spot aimed at hard‑right conservatives, positioning himself as a proud Christian fighting “Obama’s war on religion.” The ad contrasts the ability of gays to serve openly in the military with the perceived loss of public Christmas celebrations and school prayer, claiming America’s “religious heritage” is under liberal attack. Perry promises to restore faith‑based strength to the nation and to halt foreign‑policy efforts that promote gay rights abroad. The message, targeted mainly at evangelical voters in Iowa, was condemned by the Log Cabin Republicans, who argued that true faith should support equality and that Perry’s theocratic rhetoric conflicts with America’s founding principles of liberty and individual responsibility. END OF SUMMARY.
