SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox rebuked Donald Trump’s plan to **coerce** Mexico into paying for the proposed **wall** on the U.S. border, calling it a “fucking wall” and insisting “he should pay for it.” Fox, speaking with journalist Jorge Ramos, labeled Trump’s rhetoric toward Latinos as offensive and warned that democracy should not “pick crazy people.” He dismissed the notion that the **wall** would be effective, describing it as **useless** and the **closing** of borders as a delusional strategy. Former President Felipe Calderón echoed the sentiment, saying Mexico will not fund the **wall** and that the **closing** of trade and migration would not bring prosperity. Both leaders condemned Trump’s **calling** of Mexican immigrants “criminals” and “rapists,” emphasizing that Mexico will not pay a single cent for the proposed barrier. END OF SUMMARY.
