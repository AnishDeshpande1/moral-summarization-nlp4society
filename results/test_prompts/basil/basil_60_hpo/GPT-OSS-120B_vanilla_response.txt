SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox publicly rejected the notion that Mexico would finance Donald Trump’s proposed border wall, calling it “fucking” and insisting Trump should pay himself. In a Fusion interview with Jorge Ramos, Fox dismissed the idea as unrealistic, noting Trump’s wealth and labeling the demand as absurd. He also expressed doubt that democracy would elect “crazy people” like Trump and questioned the significance of Trump’s alleged Hispanic support in the Nevada caucus. Former President Felipe Calderón echoed Fox’s stance, stating Mexico will not pay a cent for the wall and calling the plan “stupid” and ineffective. Both leaders criticized Trump’s rhetoric toward Latinos and his broader border‑security proposals. END OF SUMMARY.
