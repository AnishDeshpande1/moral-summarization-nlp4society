SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox flat‑out rejected former U.S. President Donald Trump’s demand that Mexico fund the proposed border wall, calling it a “fucking wall” and insisting that Trump, who has repeatedly vilified Latino immigrants as “criminals” and “rapists,” should pay for his own scheme. Fox warned that democracy should not empower “crazy people” who are out of touch with reality, and dismissed the notion that any Mexican taxpayer would contribute a single cent to what he called a “stupid wall” that would be useless. He also questioned the reported Hispanic support for Trump, labeling those voters as “followers of a false prophet.” Former Mexican President Felipe Calderón echoed Fox’s stance, rejecting any financial responsibility and denouncing the wall as a futile, delusional attempt to bring prosperity to the United States. Together, the former leaders frame the wall as an immoral, hateful, and economically nonsensical project that should not be funded by Mexico or its people. END OF SUMMARY.
