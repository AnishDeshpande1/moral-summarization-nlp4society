SUMMARY:
Former Mexican President Vicente Fox blasted Donald Trump’s plan to make Mexico foot the bill for a U.S. border wall, declaring “I’m not going to pay for that fucking wall” and insisting the billionaire should fund it himself. Fox condemned Trump’s rhetoric toward Latinos—labeling Mexican immigrants “criminals” and “rapists”—and warned that Hispanic voters who support the “false prophet” are being misled. He dismissed the notion that democracy could ever elect such a “crazy” leader and echoed former President Felipe Calderón’s refusal to finance a “stupid” and “useless” wall. Both leaders argue the wall would not bring prosperity and that Mexico will not pay a single cent for Trump’s delusional scheme.  
END OF SUMMARY.
