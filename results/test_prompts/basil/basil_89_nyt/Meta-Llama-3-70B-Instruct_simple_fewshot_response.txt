SUMMARY:

Defense Secretary Jim Mattis has resigned in protest of President Trump's decision to withdraw American forces from Syria, citing a rejection of international alliances and a failure to recognize the threat of Russia and China. Mattis' resignation letter condemned Trump's approach to the world as destructive to American influence and power, and emphasized the importance of alliances and competition with authoritarian powers. The decision to withdraw from Syria was the final straw, as Mattis viewed it as abandoning Kurdish fighters and ceding territory to Russia and Iran. Mattis' resignation marks a significant loss of stability and experience in the Trump administration, and has been met with criticism from both parties. The move has sparked concerns about the future of American national security and the implications of Trump's isolationist policies.

END OF SUMMARY.
