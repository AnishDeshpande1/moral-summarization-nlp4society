SUMMARY:

In the vice presidential debate, Joe Biden dominated the spotlight, charging at Paul Ryan with a flurry of eye rolls, interjections, and accusations. Biden's aggressive and condescending approach was a deliberate attempt to rally the Democratic base after President Obama's dismal debate performance last week. While Ryan maintained his composure, Biden repeatedly interrupted him, making it difficult for the GOP nominee to get out his answers. The debate focused on domestic and foreign policy, with Biden pushing back hard against Ryan's arguments. Republicans criticized Biden's behavior, calling him "unhinged" and "immature," while Democrats praised his passion and concern for the middle class. The moral of the story is that Biden's aggressive approach paid off, allowing him to dominate the debate and rescue the Obama campaign.
