**SUMMARY:**
Former Rep. Asa Hutchinson released a 225‑page NRA‑funded report that frames arming teachers as an “important” step to improve school **safety** and **security**. The study argues that **trained**, **willing** staff can **protect** children and that local authorities are best positioned to decide if an armed guard is needed. It recommends 40‑60 hours of firearms training, background checks, and a flexible choice of weapons—from sidearms to **AR‑15s**—at a cost of $800‑$1,000 per employee. The report also calls for states to **loosen** restrictions on who may carry guns on campus, create threat‑assessment teams, and coordinate funding. Critics, including the American Federation of Teachers, denounced the plan as a **cruel hoax** that would **risk** children’s lives and **interfere** with parental **rights** and **religious** **freedom**, arguing it would make schools **dangerous** rather than **safe**. The NRA says it will review the findings, while supporters like Sandy Hook parent Mark Mattioli praise the effort as a **responsibility** to keep children **safe**.
