SUMMARY:
A National Rifle Association (NRA) task force, led by former Rep. Asa Hutchinson (R-Ark.), has released a 225-page report on school safety. The report recommends arming properly trained teachers and staff to provide security in schools, loosening gun laws, and designating willing staff as armed school resource officers. The committee also suggested creating threat assessment teams at schools, coordinating state and federal funding for school security, and offering an online assessment tool. Critics have condemned the report, calling it a "cruel hoax" that will fail to keep children safe. Hutchinson defended the recommendations, stating that local school authorities are best equipped to determine their own school security measures. He also encouraged the NRA to continue funding pilot programs to train school employees to carry weapons.
