SUMMARY: Former Mexican President Vicente Fox publicly declared that Donald Trump is “not welcome” in Mexico, criticizing President Enrique Pena Nieto’s decision to invite the U.S. billionaire for a meeting. Fox told CNN that Trump’s visit was a political risk and called the president a potential “traitor” for allowing it. Trump responded on Twitter, mocking Fox’s remarks and referencing Fox’s earlier profanity about the border wall. Fox retorted, demanding an apology and accusing Trump of lying. END OF SUMMARY.
