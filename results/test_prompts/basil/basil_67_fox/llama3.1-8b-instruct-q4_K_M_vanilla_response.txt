SUMMARY:

Former Mexican President Vicente Fox expressed his opposition to Donald Trump's visit to Mexico, stating that the Republican nominee is "not welcome" in the country. Fox criticized current President Enrique Pena Nieto for inviting Trump, calling it a "big mistake." He accused Trump of trying to offend Mexico and its people.

Trump responded on Twitter, accusing Fox of hypocrisy after he previously invited Trump to visit Mexico when he apologized for using profanity regarding the proposed border wall. Fox fired back with a tweet, asking Trump to apologize and stop lying.

The exchange sparked a heated debate between the two leaders, with both sides exchanging barbs on social media. The controversy has highlighted the tense relationship between Trump and Mexico's government, with Fox's comments reflecting the widespread criticism of Trump in Mexico.
