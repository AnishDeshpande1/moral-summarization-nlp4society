SUMMARY:
Former Mexican President Vicente Fox launched a scathing Twitter attack on Donald Trump before the billionaire’s arrival in Mexico, denouncing the Republican nominee as “not welcome” and urging President Enrique Peña Nieto not to host him. Fox warned that Peña’s decision could label the Mexican president a “traitor” for allowing Trump, whom he called an opportunistic figure exploiting Mexico for political gain. Trump retaliated on Twitter, mocking Fox’s earlier profanity‑laden criticism of the wall and demanding an apology, while Fox replied that Trump must stop lying and apologize for his disrespect. The exchange highlights Fox’s moral condemnation of Trump’s anti‑Mexican rhetoric and the political risk Peña assumes by entertaining the controversial visitor. END OF SUMMARY.
