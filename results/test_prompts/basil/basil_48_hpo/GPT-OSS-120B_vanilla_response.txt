SUMMARY: A Republican staffer, Elizabeth Lauten, communications director for Rep. Stephen Fincher (R‑Tenn.), posted a scathing Facebook rant criticizing the Obama daughters, Malia and Sasha, for their behavior and attire during the White House turkey‑pardon ceremony. She urged the teens to “show a little class,” dress respectfully, and stop making faces on TV, also disparaging their parents. After the post was highlighted by Gawker, Lauten issued an apology, saying she reacted hastily, recognized the hurtful nature of her comments, and pledged to learn from the experience. END OF SUMMARY.
