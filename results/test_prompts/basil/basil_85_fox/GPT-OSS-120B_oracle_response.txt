SUMMARY: President Trump blasted social‑media giants on Saturday, accusing them of **discriminating** against Republicans and conservatives and of **destroying** too many voices. He warned that the administration would not allow such **discrimination** to continue, insisting that “everyone should participate, good & bad.” Trump cited recent reports that top GOP figures, including Ronna McDaniel, suffered **limited** visibility on Twitter, which the platform blamed on an algorithmic glitch and denied any **banning** or **illegal** **discriminatory** practices. Twitter defended its ranking system as a relevance tool, not a means to **ignore** or **manipulate** political viewpoints, while Facebook’s executive apologized for **mistreating** pro‑Trump personalities Diamond and Silk but denied broader **bias**. Trump also condemned the **closing** of right‑wing opinions, calling censorship a “dangerous thing,” and contrasted it with the “fake” coverage of CNN and MSNBC, which he says he merely watches with a grain of salt. END OF SUMMARY.
