SUMMARY:

President Trump accused social media giants, Facebook and Twitter, of discriminating against Republicans and conservatives, stating that "too many voices are being destroyed" and promising that his administration would not allow it to happen. Trump claimed that the tech giants are "closing down the opinions of many people on the right" while doing nothing to others. He also referred to alleged "shadow banning" as "discriminatory and illegal." Twitter and Facebook have denied allegations of censorship, citing algorithmic glitches and efforts to promote healthy conversation. Trump's comments come amid controversy over alleged bias against conservatives, with some accusing the platforms of limiting visibility of top Republican accounts. Trump also took a swipe at CNN and MSNBC, calling them "fake" but saying he doesn't demand that offending content be taken off air.

END OF SUMMARY.
