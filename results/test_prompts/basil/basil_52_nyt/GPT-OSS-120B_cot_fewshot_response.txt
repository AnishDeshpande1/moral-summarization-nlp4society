Indiana Governor Mike Pence, a Republican, said he would seek legislation to **clarify** the intent of the state’s newly‑passed **Religious Freedom Restoration Act** after a wave of backlash. Pence insisted the law was about **religious liberty** and **protect**ing individuals from actions that burden their **religious** beliefs, not about **discrimination** against gays and lesbians.
