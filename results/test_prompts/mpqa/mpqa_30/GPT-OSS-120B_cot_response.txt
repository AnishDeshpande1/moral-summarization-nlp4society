**SUMMARY:**  
Opposition leader Morgan Tsvangirai accuses President Robert Mugabe of a deliberate **punishment** policy, using **starvation** and **hunger** as tools of **retribution** against MDC supporters after the disputed election. He describes the government’s food distribution as **discriminatory**, requiring a ZANU‑PF card, a forced **confession**, and a **purge** of “MDC sympathizers” before allowing purchases of staple **mealie meal**. Tsvangirai calls the land‑reform scheme a “Last Supper,” a **vendetta** that enriches party cronies while the nation suffers **violence**, **repression**, and **casualties**. He condemns the **illegitimate** regime’s “shackles” on freedom, urging peaceful mass mobilization despite the risk of **reckless** action. While thanking Canada and other nations for sanctions, he stresses that Zimbabwe’s future depends on its own people confronting a **stolen** victory and ending the **cruel** hunger campaign. He also notes his pending treason trial, questioning the credibility of the key witness.
