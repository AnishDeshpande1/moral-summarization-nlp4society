The debate stresses the urgent need for solidarity and responsible authority: either to block reckless “umbrella” schemes and invest in genuine emission reductions, or to exploit the market mechanism at the expense of broader environmental care. The consensus is that without coordinated action, the protocol’s benefits will be undermined and the climate threat will worsen for all.
