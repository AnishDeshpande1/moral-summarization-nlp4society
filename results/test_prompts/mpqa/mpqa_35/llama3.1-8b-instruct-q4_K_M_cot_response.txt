**SUMMARY:**

The US State Department's annual human rights report highlights the "axis of evil" countries' abuses, but also reveals a more sinister reality. The report excuses Israel's treatment of Palestinians as understandable due to its struggle against terrorists. However, it ignores similar abuses by other anti-terror coalition members, such as Russia's suppression of resistance in Chechnya and China's crackdown on independence advocates in Xinjiang and the Falun Gong movement. This selective approach undermines the universal values of democracy, peace, and human rights. The US is prioritizing its interests over these values, perpetuating a "two-sided justice" where allies are exempt from scrutiny. To truly combat terrorism, the US must promote human rights and not become complicit in their erosion. As the world's policeman, the US should uphold the principles of tolerance and legitimacy.
