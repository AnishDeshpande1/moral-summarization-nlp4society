SUMMARY:  
Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman Kong Quan voiced China’s disapproval of the phrase “axis of evil” in international relations, warning that such labeling could lead to serious consequences if its logic is followed. He noted that U.S. public opinion largely rejects the wording and stressed that anti‑terrorism actions must be based on irrefutable evidence rather than arbitrarily expanded accusations. Kong called for state‑to‑state interactions to be treated in line with the United Nations Charter, the Five Principles of Peaceful Co‑existence, and the principle of equal footing for all nations. He argued that only by adhering to these standards can the international community safeguard long‑term peace and stability in the world and its regions.  

END OF SUMMARY.
