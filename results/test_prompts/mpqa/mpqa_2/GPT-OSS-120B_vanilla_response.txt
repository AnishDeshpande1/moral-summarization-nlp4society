SUMMARY: Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman Kong Quan expressed disapproval of the term “axis of evil” used in international relations, referencing the U.S. leader’s labeling of North Korea, Iran and Iraq. Kong noted that U.S. public opinion does not support such wording and warned that adopting this logic could have serious consequences. He emphasized that anti‑terrorism efforts must be based on solid evidence and not be arbitrarily expanded. Kong called for state‑to‑state interactions to adhere to the UN Charter, the Five Principles of Peaceful Co‑existence, and equal treatment of all nations, arguing this approach best preserves long‑term global and regional peace and stability. END OF SUMMARY.
