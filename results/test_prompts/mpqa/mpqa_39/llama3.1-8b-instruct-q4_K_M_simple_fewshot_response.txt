SUMMARY:

The Islamic Human Rights Commission (IHRC) in Tehran expressed concern about the resurgence of force and violence in international relations, marking the International Day for Human Rights on December 10. Despite numerous documents promoting human rights, the IHRC lamented that provisions have not been materialized, leading to heavy losses for humanity. The organization called for an end to discrimination, torture, arbitrary detention, and honoring human rights as recommended by divine religions. The IHRC also condemned injustices against Palestinians, violation of human rights in various parts of the world, and the use of military power that tramples on civilian rights. In a separate development, the United Nations High Commissioner for Human Rights, Mary Robinson, urged Israel to halt bombing Palestinian targets and allow international monitors, denouncing targeted killings of Palestinians.

END OF SUMMARY.
