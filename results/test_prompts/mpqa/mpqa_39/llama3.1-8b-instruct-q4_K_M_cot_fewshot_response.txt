The Islamic Human Rights Commission (IHRC) has expressed concern about the return of a period where force and weapons dominate international relations. The IHRC calls for an end to discrimination, torture, arbitrary detention, and honoring human rights, including political rights, association rights, women's rights, and the rights envisaged in the international declaration for human rights as recommended by divine religions. However, provisions of the international declaration have not been materialized, resulting in heavy human losses. The IHRC deplored the injustice against the Palestinian nation and violation of human rights of political activists, journalists, and religious minorities. Meanwhile, the United Nations High Commissioner for Human Rights Mary Robinson called on Israel to stop bombing Palestinian targets and allow international monitors. She denounced Israeli terrorist attacks against Palestinian activists and urged them to halt targeted killings. The IHRC emphasized that no peace will be established without honoring human rights.
