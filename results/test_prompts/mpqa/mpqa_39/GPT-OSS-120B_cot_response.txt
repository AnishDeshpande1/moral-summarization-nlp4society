**SUMMARY:**  
The Tehran‑based Islamic Human Rights Commission (IHRC) warned that the world is slipping back into an era where *force* and *weapon* dominate international relations, undermining *humanity*’s hard‑won *rights*. While hundreds of *documents* have been signed to combat *discrimination*, *torture*, *arbitrary* *detention* and to promote *honoring* *political* *rights*, *women*’s *rights* and the principles of *peace* and *security*, the IHRC says many of these promises remain unfulfilled. It condemned the *injustice* and *violation* of *human* rights suffered by Palestinians, political activists, journalists, religious minorities, children and women, and decried the *trampling* of civilian *rights* by some states that resort to *terrorist* tactics. The statement called for *solidarity* rooted in religious teachings to eradicate *terrorism* and achieve lasting *peace*. Meanwhile, UN High Commissioner Mary Robinson urged Israel to halt bombings in the West Bank and Gaza, allow international monitors, and stop *targeted* *killings* of Palestinians.
