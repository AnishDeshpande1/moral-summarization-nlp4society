The US State Department Report on human rights has been rejected by the Pakistani government as "factually inaccurate and unwarranted". Despite these measures, the report still labels Pakistan's human rights record as "poor", calling it "unacceptable" and a "gross misstatement of facts". The Pakistani government feels that this judgment is an example of some states sitting in judgment over others without showing cooperation or understanding towards promoting human rights worldwide.
