Environmental NGOs staged a protest outside the U.S. Embassy in Tokyo to denounce the “negative” U.S. alternative to the Kyoto Protocol, calling the plan “regrettable” and “dangerous.” Representatives from Friends of the Earth Japan, Kiko Network and Peace Boat handed a letter to a guard, saying they were “dismayed” by President Bush’s climate‑change proposal and demanding that he “reconsider” and re‑enter the Kyoto Protocol, the only existing framework to “prevent” dangerous climate change.
