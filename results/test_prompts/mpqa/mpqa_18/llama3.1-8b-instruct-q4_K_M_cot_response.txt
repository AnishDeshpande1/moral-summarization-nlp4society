These single words are morally framed as they imply a sense of responsibility, care, and well-being.

**SUMMARY:**
New crew settles in at space station. Two American astronauts and one Russian cosmonaut moved into the International Space Station on Saturday, settling in for a half-year stay. Commander Yuri Onufrienko took over from Frank Culbertson as space station commander, eager to make this new home until May. The new residents brought personal items reflecting their hobbies, including fishing gear, weaving material, and a five-octave keyboard that served as a "psychological support device". Meanwhile, the outgoing crew packed up dirty laundry, trash, and used equipment for the ride home. As they settled in, Endeavour's astronauts transferred an Italian-built cargo carrier to the station, carrying three tons of food, clothes, science experiments, and spare parts.

END OF SUMMARY.
