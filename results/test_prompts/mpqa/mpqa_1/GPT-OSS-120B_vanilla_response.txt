SUMMARY: Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso expressed confidence that the United States will not obstruct international talks on greenhouse‑gas reductions and will cooperate with other nations despite having formally withdrawn from the Kyoto Protocol. He urged Brazil’s Congress to ratify the Kyoto Protocol and pledged a 5.2% cut in the country’s emissions by 2012. Cardoso highlighted the United States as the world’s largest emitter (36%), followed by Russia, Japan, Germany, the United Kingdom, Canada, Italy, Poland, and France. END OF SUMMARY.
