SUMMARY: President Fernando Henrique Cardoso expressed confidence that the United States will not interfere with upcoming international talks on curbing greenhouse‑gas emissions. He argued that the U.S. is ready to **adopt** domestic steps and to **fight** climate change alongside other nations, despite having formally withdrawn from the Kyoto Protocol. Cardoso sent a message to Brazil’s Congress urging the ratification of the Kyoto Protocol and pledging a 5.2 % reduction in Brazil’s emissions by 2012. The article notes the world’s largest emitters, with the United States responsible for 36 % of greenhouse gases.  
END OF SUMMARY.
