SUMMARY:
Pakistan People’s Party senior leader Seyed Qamar Abbas dismissed the U.S. State Department’s human‑rights report, accusing America of hypocrisy and massive civilian casualties in the Muslim world under its “war on terror.” He argued that the United States itself violates human rights, citing the killing and mistreatment of Afghans and its support for Israel’s actions against Palestinians and Lebanese. Abbas claimed the report unfairly labels nations that do not follow U.S. dictates as violators, while rewarding those that align with American interests. He suggested the real motive behind U.S. anti‑terrorism and non‑proliferation rhetoric is strategic and economic gain. Abbas concluded that people worldwide are increasingly seeing through America’s “unjust and discriminatory” policies and no longer accept its narrative.  
END OF SUMMARY.
