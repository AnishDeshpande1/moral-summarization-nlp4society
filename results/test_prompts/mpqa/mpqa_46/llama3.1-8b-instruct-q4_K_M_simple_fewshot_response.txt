SUMMARY:

Central Leader of Pakistan People Party (PPP) Seyed Qamar Abbas has rejected the US state department's report on Human Rights in Iran and other countries, accusing America of hypocrisy and double standards. Abbas pointed out that the US itself has committed numerous human rights violations worldwide, particularly in the Muslim world, and has supported Israel's actions against Palestinians and Lebanese civilians. He argued that independent nations who do not bow to US interests are labeled as human rights violators, while those who submit to US policies are rewarded with favors. Abbas accused the US of pursuing its own strategic and economic interests under the guise of combating terrorism and weapons of mass destruction. He expressed hope that people worldwide will see through the US's unjust and discriminatory policies and stop believing everything they say.
