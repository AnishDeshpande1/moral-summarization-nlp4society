SUMMARY:  
Seyed Qamar Abbas, central leader of Pakistan’s People Party and former NWFP minister, flatly rejected the U.S. State Department’s human‑rights report, accusing America of being the real **violator** of rights. He claimed the United States has **killed** thousands of innocent people worldwide, especially in the Muslim world, and that its **guise** of a “war on terror” has left many Afghans **injured** and **maltreated** as prisoners of war. Abbas denounced the double‑standard policy that **denounces** violations while **supporting** Israel in the **perishing** of Muslims in Palestine and Lebanon. He argued that nations that refuse to follow U.S. dictates are **labelled** human‑rights **violators**, whereas those who submit receive favours. According to Abbas, America’s actions are **unjust**, **discriminatory**, and driven by strategic and economic interests, keeping the world in **dark** until people see the truth.  
END OF SUMMARY.
