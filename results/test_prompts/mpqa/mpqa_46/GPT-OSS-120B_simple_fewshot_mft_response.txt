SUMMARY:
Pakistani politician Seyed Qamar Abbas dismissed the U.S. State Department’s human‑rights report, accusing America of being the real violator of human rights. He argued that the United States has caused massive harm, killing and wounding thousands of innocent people—especially in Muslim lands such as Afghanistan—under the pretext of a “war on terror.” Abbas claimed the U.S. applies a double standard: it condemns rights abuses while backing Israel in its assaults on Palestinians and Lebanese, rewarding nations that follow its policies and labeling independent countries as violators. According to him, America’s actions are driven by strategic and economic self‑interest rather than genuine concern for human safety. He warned that people worldwide are increasingly seeing through this unfair hypocrisy and will no longer accept the United States’ false moral narrative.  
END OF SUMMARY.
