SUMMARY:
Central Leader of Pakistan People Party (PPP) Seyed Qamar Abbas rejected the US state department's report on Human Rights in Iran and other countries, calling it hypocritical. He pointed out that America has itself committed numerous human rights violations worldwide, particularly in Muslim-majority countries. Abbas criticized America's "double standard policy" for supporting Israel while denouncing others for violating human rights. He suggested that the US labels independent nations as human rights violators to justify its own interests and agenda.

Abbas argued that America's actions are driven by strategic and economic interests rather than a genuine commitment to fighting terrorism or promoting democracy. He expressed hope that people around the world will see through America's unjust and discriminatory policies, which he believes are based on lies and deception. By exposing these contradictions, Abbas aimed to shed light on the US government's true motives and intentions.

END OF SUMMARY.
