SUMMARY:

Seyed Qamar Abbas, Central Leader of Pakistan People Party (PPP) and former provincial minister, rejected the US state department's report on human rights in Iran and other countries. He criticized America for having a "double standard policy" and violating human rights itself. Abbas pointed out that America has killed thousands of innocent people worldwide, particularly in Muslim countries, and mistreats prisoners of war. He accused the US of supporting Israel to kill Muslims in Palestine and Lebanon.

Abbas stated that independent nations not controlled by the US are labeled as human rights violators, while those who submit to its policies receive favors. He believed America's true motive is to advance its strategic and economic interests, rather than fighting terrorism or protecting human rights. Abbas hoped that people worldwide will no longer be misled by America's unjust and discriminatory policies.

END OF SUMMARY.
