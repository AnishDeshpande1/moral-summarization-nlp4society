SUMMARY: President Fernando Henrique Cardoso warned that the main challenge for Venezuelan leader Hugo Chávez is to restore normalcy after last week’s **conflicts**. Cardoso emphasized the need to open communication channels and achieve **social harmony**. Chávez assured Cardoso he will not seek revenge on the military officers involved in the failed coup attempt. Former U.S. President Jimmy Carter has voiced support for Chávez’s pledge to set up dialogue tables and plans to visit Venezuela soon. END OF SUMMARY.
