SUMMARY: Former Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso said the biggest challenge for Venezuelan President Hugo Chávez is to restore normalcy after last week’s unrest, emphasizing the need for open communication channels and social harmony. Chávez told Cardoso he will not seek revenge against the military officers involved in the failed coup attempt. Former U.S. President Jimmy Carter has voiced support for Chávez’s pledge to initiate dialogue and plans to visit Venezuela soon. END OF SUMMARY.
