SUMMARY:
Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso believes that Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez's main challenge is restoring normalcy following last week's conflicts. Cardoso suggests opening communication channels and achieving social harmony to resolve the issues. In a positive sign, Chavez has assured Cardoso that he will not seek revenge against military personnel who supported the failed coup attempt against him. This stance has been welcomed by former US President Jimmy Carter, who is scheduled to visit Venezuela soon. Carter supports Chavez's plan to establish dialogue tables to address the country's problems.

END OF SUMMARY.
