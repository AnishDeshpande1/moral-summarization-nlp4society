SUMMARY: Space shuttle Endeavour docked with the International Space Station, delivering a new three‑person crew and ending a two‑day chase. Commander Frank Culbertson, who has been aboard the station since August with two Russian cosmonauts, welcomed the arrival and prepared to return home. The departing crew—Culbertson, Vladimir Dezhurov, and Mikhail Tyurin—will leave the station today. Replacing them are Russian commander Yuri Onufrienko and American astronauts Carl Walz and Daniel Bursch, who will remain on the station until May. END OF SUMMARY.
