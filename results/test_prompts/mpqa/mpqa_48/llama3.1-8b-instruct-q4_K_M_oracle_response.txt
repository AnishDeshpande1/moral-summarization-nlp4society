SUMMARY: Vietnamese Minister Chu Tuan Nha emphasized that Vietnam values environmental protection, including prevention of pollution and degradation, bio-diversity protection, and improvement of environments in industrial zones and both urban and rural areas. The government has mobilized all resources to address environmental issues through strengthened international cooperation. The minister highlighted the importance of bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe in dealing with environmental challenges, stating that this meeting will serve as a foundation for future cooperation.

The ASEM EnMM concluded by adopting a Statement of the Presidium, calling for an early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and promoting dialogue and cooperation among members. Minister Nha also proposed the establishment of a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate policy exchange and expert dialogue every two years within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program.
