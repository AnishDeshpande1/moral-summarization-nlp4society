**SUMMARY:** 
Vietnamese Minister Chu Tuan Nha emphasized the importance of environmental protection, highlighting Vietnam's commitment to preventing pollution and degradation, as well as improving the environment in industrial zones and rural areas. He stressed the need for strengthened international cooperation on environmental issues, calling for a cooperative forum every two years under the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program. This forum will facilitate dialogue on policy and information exchange among experts on the environment.

The ASEM EnMM concluded with a call for early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and further cooperation between members. The adoption of the Statement of the Presidium underscores the importance of cooperation and mutual understanding in addressing environmental challenges.
