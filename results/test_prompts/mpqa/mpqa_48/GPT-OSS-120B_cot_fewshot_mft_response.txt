SUMMARY:
Vietnam’s Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, told the first ASEM Environment Ministers’ Meeting in Beijing that Vietnam places a high priority on **environmental protection**, emphasizing **prevention** of **pollution** and **degradation**, **biodiversity** **protection**, and the **improvement** of conditions in industrial zones, cities and rural areas. He noted that the Vietnamese Government has **mobilized** all resources and **strengthened cooperation** internationally to meet these challenges. Highlighting the **importance** of **bilateral** ties, he said the meeting will serve as a **foundation** for future Asia‑Europe collaboration and called for the **establishment** of a biennial cooperation forum under the Asia‑Europe Environment Cooperation Program to **facilitate** **exchange** of **policy**, information and experts. The ASEM EnMM concluded with a Presidium Statement urging early **implementation** of the Kyoto Protocol and continued **dialogue** among members. END OF SUMMARY.
