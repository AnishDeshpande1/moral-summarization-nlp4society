SUMMARY: Vietnamese Minister of Science, Technology and Environment Chu Tuan Nha highlighted Vietnam’s strong commitment to environmental protection—covering pollution control, biodiversity, and improvements in industrial, urban, and rural areas—at the first Asia‑Europe Meeting (ASEM) of Environment Ministers in Beijing. He noted that the Vietnamese government has mobilized resources and deepened international cooperation to tackle environmental challenges, stressing the importance of Asia‑Europe bilateral collaboration. The minister proposed creating a biennial cooperation forum under the Asia‑Europe Environment Cooperation Program to facilitate policy dialogue, information exchange, and expert interaction. The meeting concluded on 17 January with a Presidium Statement urging early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and promoting continued cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members. END OF SUMMARY.
