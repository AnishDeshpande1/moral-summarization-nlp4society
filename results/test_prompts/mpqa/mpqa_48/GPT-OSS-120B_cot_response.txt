**SUMMARY:**  
Vietnam’s Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, told the first Asia‑Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers’ Meeting in Beijing that Vietnam **values** environmental **protection** and **prevention** of **degradation**, pledging **improvement** of biodiversity and the environment in industrial, urban and rural zones. He said the government has **mobilized** all resources and **strengthened** international **cooperation**, stressing the **importance** of bilateral ties between Asia and Europe as a **future** foundation for joint action. Nha proposed a biennial **forum** within the Asia‑Europe Environment Cooperation Program to enable **exchange**, **dialogue**, and expert sharing on policy and information. The meeting concluded with a Presidium Statement urging **early implementation** of the Kyoto Protocol and continued **cooperation** among ASEM members.
