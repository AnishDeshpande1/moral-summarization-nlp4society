SUMMARY:
Vietnamese Minister Chu Tuan Nha emphasized the importance of environmental protection in Vietnam, highlighting the country's commitment to preventing pollution, preserving biodiversity, and improving industrial and urban environments. He stressed that addressing environmental issues requires international cooperation, citing the need for bilateral collaboration between Asia and Europe.

The ASEM EnMM meeting in Beijing concluded with a call for early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and increased cooperation among member countries. The Vietnamese Minister proposed establishing an annual Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program, which would facilitate dialogue on environmental policies and information exchange.

The meeting's adoption of the Statement of the Presidium underscores the shared commitment to addressing environmental challenges through cooperation and dialogue. By strengthening international ties and mobilizing resources, Vietnam aims to address its environmental concerns and promote sustainable development.

END OF SUMMARY.
