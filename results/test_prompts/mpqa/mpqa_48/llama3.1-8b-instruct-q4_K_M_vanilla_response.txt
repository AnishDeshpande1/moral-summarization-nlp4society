SUMMARY:
Vietnamese Minister Chu Tuan Nha emphasized the importance of environmental protection at the Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers' Meeting in Beijing. He stated that Vietnam values environmental protection and has mobilized all resources to address environmental issues, including prevention of pollution and degradation, biodiversity protection, and improvement of environments in industrial zones and urban/rural areas.

The Minister called for bilateral cooperation between Asia and Europe on environmental issues, noting that the meeting will serve as a foundation for future cooperation. He proposed establishing a cooperation forum on the environment to facilitate dialogue and exchange of information and experts every two years within the Asia-Europe Environment Cooperation Program.

The ASEM EnMM concluded with a statement calling for early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and cooperation between member countries. The meeting highlighted the importance of international cooperation in addressing environmental issues.

END OF SUMMARY.
