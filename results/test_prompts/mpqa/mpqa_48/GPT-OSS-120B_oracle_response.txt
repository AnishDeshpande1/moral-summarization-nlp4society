SUMMARY:  
Vietnam’s Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Tuan Nha, told the first Asia‑Europe Meeting (ASEM) Environment Ministers’ Meeting in Beijing that Vietnam **values** environmental **protection** and is committed to the **prevention** of pollution, biodiversity loss, and the **improvement** of conditions in industrial zones as well as urban and rural areas. He said the government has mobilized all resources and is **addressing** environmental challenges through stronger international cooperation, especially **dealing** with them jointly with Europe. The minister proposed establishing a biennial cooperation forum under the Asia‑Europe Environment Cooperation Program to enable policy dialogue, information exchange, and expert collaboration. The ASEM EnMM concluded with a Presidium Statement urging early implementation of the Kyoto Protocol and continued cooperation and dialogue among ASEM members.  

END OF SUMMARY.
