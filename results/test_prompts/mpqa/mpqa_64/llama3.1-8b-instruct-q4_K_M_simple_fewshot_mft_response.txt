British troops will not be sent to fight the Taliban in Afghanistan, according to Commons Leader Robin Cook. This decision comes as the Taliban begins talks on surrendering its stronghold of Kandahar after Northern Alliance troops entered Kunduz. The collapse of Taliban control in northern Afghanistan has left Kandahar as its last major stronghold. However, concerns about instability and humanitarian aid efforts persist, with the United Nations Commissioner for Refugees warning that the environment is still not secure enough for aid agencies to operate.
