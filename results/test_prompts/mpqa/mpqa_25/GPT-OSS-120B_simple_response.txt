While the country reels from the shock of eleven deaths and a power vacuum, the piece emphasizes the moral imperative to heal social divisions and hold former leaders accountable for the killings. Citizens express cautious optimism, celebrating Chávez’s fall as a victory for freedom, yet acknowledge uncertainty about rebuilding a fair and democratic Venezuela. END OF SUMMARY.
