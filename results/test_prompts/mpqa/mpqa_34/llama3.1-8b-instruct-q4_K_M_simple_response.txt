SUMMARY: China's government has released an annual report criticizing the United States for human rights abuses, accusing Washington of hypocrisy in condemning other countries while ignoring its own problems. The report cites evidence of police misconduct, poverty, racial discrimination, and lack of personal safety in the US, as well as allegations that the US is "wantonly infringing" on the sovereignty of other nations through military interventions. China also criticizes the US for withdrawing from the Kyoto Protocol on greenhouse gas emissions.

The Chinese government's report responds to a recent US State Department report on global human rights conditions, which typically accuses China of abuses. In its report, China argues that the US is "assuming the role of 'world judge of human rights'" while ignoring its own shortcomings and selectively condemning other countries. The report suggests that the US has a double standard in addressing human rights issues, both at home and abroad.

END OF SUMMARY.
