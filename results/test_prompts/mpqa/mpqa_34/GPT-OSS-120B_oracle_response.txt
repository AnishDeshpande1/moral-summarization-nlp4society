SUMMARY:  
China’s State Council released its annual human‑rights report on the United States, accusing Washington of **turning** a blind eye to its own **abuses** while denouncing other nations. The Chinese report lists US **poverty**, **discrimination**, and a **lack** of personal safety as evidence of systemic problems. It also condemns America for **infringing** on other nations’ sovereignty through military **operations** and the **stationing** of forces abroad. Additionally, the document criticizes the Bush administration’s **withdrawing** from the Kyoto Protocol on greenhouse‑gas reductions. The Chinese narrative frames the United States as a self‑appointed “world judge of human rights” that **distorted** global conditions and leveled unfounded **violations** against other countries while ignoring its own issues.  

END OF SUMMARY.
