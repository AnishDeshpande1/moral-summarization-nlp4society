SUMMARY:
China's government has released an annual report criticizing the US for human rights abuses, accusing Washington of hypocrisy in its own land while condemning other countries for theirs. The report, issued by China's Cabinet, lists evidence of police abuse, poverty, racial discrimination, and lack of personal safety in the US. It also accuses the US of infringing on the sovereignty of other nations through military operations and withdrawing from the Kyoto Protocol on greenhouse gases. The report comes in response to a recent US State Department report on global human rights conditions, which typically criticizes China for abuses.

China's government claims that the US is "distorting" human rights conditions in many countries, including China, while ignoring its own problems. This move is seen as a counter-attack by Beijing against Washington's criticism of China's human rights record. The Chinese report highlights what it sees as double standards in the US approach to human rights.

END OF SUMMARY.
