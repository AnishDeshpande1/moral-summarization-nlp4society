SUMMARY:
Brazilian President Fernando Henrique Cardoso expressed approval of the US decision to control greenhouse gases, despite not ratifying the Kyoto Protocol. He believes that the measures taken by the US domestically will have a similar impact as adhering to the protocol. This stance highlights the importance of cooperation and collective action in addressing global environmental issues.

The president's words convey a sense of optimism and hope for international collaboration on climate change mitigation, even when parties do not adhere to formal agreements like the Kyoto Protocol. By acknowledging the significance of the US contribution, Cardoso emphasizes the need for joint efforts to address this pressing global issue.

END OF SUMMARY.
