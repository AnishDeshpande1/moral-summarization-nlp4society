SUMMARY:
Brazil and Germany have joined forces to promote the implementation of the Kyoto Protocol by September, coinciding with the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in South Africa. In their partnership document, they urge other countries to ratify and enforce the protocol. The two nations also plan to expand cooperation on environmental issues and sustainable development. They aim to present a united front at the Rio+10 meeting, showcasing their commitment to addressing global environmental concerns.

END OF SUMMARY.
