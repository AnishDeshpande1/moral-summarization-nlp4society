SUMMARY:
Brazil and Germany have jointly expressed their desire for the Kyoto Protocol to take effect by September, coinciding with the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in South Africa. In a partnership document, they also urge other countries to ratify the protocol and work towards its implementation. The two nations aim to present common positions at the Rio+10 meeting and are committed to expanding cooperation on environmental and sustainable development issues. Additionally, the document highlights the importance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG-7) and German participation in the program.

END OF SUMMARY.
