SUMMARY: Brazil and Germany have issued a joint “Partnership Brazil‑Germany: Action Plan” urging that the Kyoto Protocol be implemented by the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in South Africa next September. They call on all nations to ratify and enforce the protocol, and pledge to deepen cooperation on environmental and sustainable development issues. Both countries plan to present a unified stance at Rio+10 and highlight the significance of the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG‑7), noting continued German participation. END OF SUMMARY.
