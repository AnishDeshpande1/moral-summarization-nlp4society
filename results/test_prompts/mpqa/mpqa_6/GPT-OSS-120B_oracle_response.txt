SUMMARY: Brazil and Germany have jointly issued a “Partnership Brazil‑Germany: Action Plan,” urging that the Kyoto Protocol be fully implemented by the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in South Africa next September. The two nations call on the international community to ratify and enforce the protocol, emphasizing the need for collective **development** that respects the environment. They also pledge to deepen cooperation on **protection** of natural resources, highlighting the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG‑7) and Germany’s continued participation. Both countries will present a unified stance at Rio+10, aiming to advance global **development** goals while ensuring the **protection** of ecosystems. END OF SUMMARY.
