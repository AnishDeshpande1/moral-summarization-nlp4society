SUMMARY:
Brazil and Germany have issued a joint “Partnership Brazil‑Germany: Action Plan” urging the swift implementation of the Kyoto Protocol by the Sustainable Development Summit (Rio+10) in South Africa next September. By appealing to the authority of the international community, they call on all nations to ratify and enforce the treaty, presenting a united front and promising coordinated positions at the summit. The document also highlights the moral imperative of caring for the planet, emphasizing the Pilot Program for Protection of Tropical Forests (PPG‑7) and Germany’s role in safeguarding Brazil’s tropical forests. This appeal blends respect for global governance structures with a concern for environmental harm, urging collective action to protect vulnerable ecosystems. END OF SUMMARY.
