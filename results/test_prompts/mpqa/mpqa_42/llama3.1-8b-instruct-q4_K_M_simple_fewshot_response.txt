SUMMARY:
Pakistani defense analyst Hameed Gul has rejected this year's US State Department report on human rights, calling it a "pack of blatant lies and propaganda". He accuses the US of using its influence to suppress dissenting voices and uphold its own interests. Gul criticizes the US for its silence on human rights abuses in countries like India, where over 600 Muslims have been killed in recent riots. He also condemns Israel's treatment of Palestinians and argues that the US is denying them their right to self-defense. The solution to the Palestinian issue, he suggests, lies in a free and independent state as enshrined in UN resolutions. Gul calls for a concerted moral resistance by Muslim scholars and rulers to resist the growing influence of imperialism led by the US.
