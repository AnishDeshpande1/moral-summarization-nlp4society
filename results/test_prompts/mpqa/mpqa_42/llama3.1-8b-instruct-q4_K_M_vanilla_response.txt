SUMMARY:

Pakistani defense analyst Hameed Gul has rejected the US State Department's human rights report, calling it "a pack of blatant lies and propaganda". He claims that the US uses its power to label countries that resist its influence as human rights violators. Gul accuses the US of being a hegemonic force that disregards all religions, norms, and human rights in pursuit of its interests.

Gul points out that the US has committed atrocities against Afghan people, particularly women and children, and has remained silent on the recent killings of Muslims in India. He also criticizes the US for ignoring Israeli abuses against Palestinians, while condemning Palestinian reactions as terrorist acts.

Gul warns that Muslims need to unite against the growing influence of imperialist forces led by the US and calls for a concerted moral resistance from Muslim scholars. He believes that if Muslims remain divided, they will be crushed one by one.

END OF SUMMARY.
