SUMMARY:
Former ISI chief and Pakistani defense analyst Hameed Gul dismissed the U.S. State Department’s 2024 human‑rights report as “blatant lies and propaganda,” arguing that any nation challenging American hegemony is branded a rights violator. Gul claimed the United States uniquely disrespects religion, decency and human‑rights norms, accusing it of terrorist, extremist behavior and of perpetrating atrocities against Afghans, Indians and Palestinians while remaining silent on abuses by Israel. He portrayed the U.S. as using military force and oppression to enforce its strategic interests and to suppress Muslim cultural and religious values. Gul called for a coordinated moral resistance by Muslim scholars and leaders, urging unity among Muslim‑majority states to counter what he described as an imperialist campaign that seeks total submission of Muslims worldwide. He warned that without such unity, Muslim nations will be isolated and crushed one by one by American influence.

END OF SUMMARY.
