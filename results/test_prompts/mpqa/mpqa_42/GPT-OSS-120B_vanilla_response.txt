SUMMARY: Former ISI chief and Pakistani defense analyst Lt. Gen. (ret.) Hameed Gul dismissed the U.S. State Department’s 2024 human‑rights report as “blatant lies and propaganda,” accusing the United States of using human‑rights criticism to suppress nations that challenge its hegemony. Gul claimed the U.S. disregards religion, culture, and basic decency, citing alleged atrocities in Afghanistan, the killing of Indian Muslims, and Israel’s actions in Palestine, while remaining silent on other abuses. He argued that America blocks a free and independent Palestinian state and seeks total submission of Muslims worldwide. Gul called for united moral resistance by Muslim scholars and leaders against U.S. influence, warning that failure to act will lead to further targeting of Muslim countries. END OF SUMMARY.
