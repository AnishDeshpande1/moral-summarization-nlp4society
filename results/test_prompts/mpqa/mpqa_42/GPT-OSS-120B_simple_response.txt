SUMMARY: In an IRNA interview, retired Pakistani lieutenant‑general and former ISI chief Hameed Gul denounced the U.S. State Department’s 2024 human‑rights report as “blatant lies and propaganda,” arguing that it is a tool of American hegemony used to vilify any nation that challenges U.S. dominance. Gul claimed the United States uniquely disregards religion, decency, and human‑rights norms, accusing it of terrorist and extremist behavior, especially toward Afghanistan, Indian Muslims, and Palestinians. He asserted that the U.S. remains silent on atrocities committed by Israel and Hindu extremists while branding Muslim resistance as illegitimate. Gul called for a “concerted moral resistance” by Muslim scholars and leaders, urging unity among Muslim governments to oppose what he described as imperialist U.S. influence that seeks total submission of Muslims worldwide. END OF SUMMARY.
