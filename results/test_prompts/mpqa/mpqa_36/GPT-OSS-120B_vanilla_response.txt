SUMMARY: A Chinese State Council document sharply condemns the United States for expanding its global military presence and for its post‑9/11 actions, accusing Washington of unjustly blaming Afghanistan, conducting ongoing bombings, and launching a “second phase” of the war on terror that targets Iraq, Iran, North Korea, Russia and China. The paper denounces U.S. human‑rights violations, secret detention practices, and alleged anti‑Jewish media bias, citing Noam Chomsky’s analysis of American imperialist history. It also criticizes Pakistan for unquestioningly supporting U.S. policies, warning that such alignment harms Pakistan’s reputation and interests. China calls on the U.S. to cease interference in other nations’ affairs and to reassess its global dominance. END OF SUMMARY.
