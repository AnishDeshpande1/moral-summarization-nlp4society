SUMMARY:

China has issued a detailed document criticizing the United States' expanding military presence and its policy of dominating the world. China accuses the US of putting all the blame for terrorism on Afghanistan after the 9/11 attacks, leading to bombardment of defenseless Muslims. The US is also accused of ignoring human rights conventions and violating its own constitution in the process. China demands that the US stop interfering in other countries' affairs under the guise of human rights and notes the hypocrisy of US actions, including the treatment of prisoners at Guantanamo Bay. According to China, the US has created conflicts and pursued a policy of oppression and supremacy, disregarding moral values. The report also highlights the US's double standards, as evidenced by its own internal problems with violence, racial prejudice, and lack of women's rights protection.

The Chinese government calls on other countries to view the US in its true perspective and for Pakistan to reevaluate its relationship with the US. China argues that Pakistan has suffered from blindly supporting the US and urges caution against accepting every American demand without scrutiny. The document serves as a warning about the dangers of US hegemony and the need for countries to stand up against it.

END OF SUMMARY.
