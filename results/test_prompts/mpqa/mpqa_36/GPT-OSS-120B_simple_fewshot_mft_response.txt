SUMMARY:
China’s new State Council document denounces the United States for expanding its military dominance and for “damaging the solidarity of other nations” and violating human‑rights norms. It accuses the U.S. of using the 9/11 attacks as a pretext to launch an unjust war on Afghanistan, killing defenseless Muslims, and then extending that aggression to Iraq, Iran, North Korea and even to countries such as Russia and China under the “axis of evil” label. The paper frames these actions as a subversive abuse of authority that harms innocent peoples, cheats the principles of fairness by imposing double standards, and degrades the sanctity of life and moral order. It cites historical U.S. interventions and covert operations as evidence of a long‑standing pattern of hegemony, oppression, and disregard for human dignity. The statement calls on the United States to cease its oppressive policies, warning that its violent, racially‑biased, and morally corrupt society should be exposed. It also urges Pakistan to reconsider its blind support for U.S. initiatives and to act more prudently. END OF SUMMARY.
