SUMMARY:

China has criticized the United States for its expanding military presence and policy of dominating the world. China's document highlights the US's use of aggression and terrorism to achieve its goals, dating back to the treatment of Native Americans in 1818. The US is accused of promoting artificial slogans of democracy and human rights while protecting its own interests worldwide.

The document also criticizes the US for targeting Afghanistan, Iraq, Iran, North Korea, Russia, and China, labeling them as a threat to its security. China argues that the US has become a major violator of human rights, ignoring international conventions and its own constitution after 9/11. The report accuses the US of maintaining a double standard, keeping 500 Taliban prisoners in inhumane conditions on an island near Cuba.

China demands that the US stop interfering in other countries' affairs under the guise of promoting democracy and human rights. China's document is seen as a wake-up call for the world to view the US in its true perspective.
