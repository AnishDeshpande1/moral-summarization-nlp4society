SUMMARY:

China's State Council has issued a document criticizing the US for its expanding military presence and dominance of the world. China accuses the US of using terrorism as an excuse to invade Afghanistan, Iraq, Iran, and North Korea, and of violating human rights in these countries. The document also mentions that the US has been involved in various covert operations since World War II, including toppling governments in Latin America and the Middle East.

China's criticism is not limited to the US actions, but also its hypocrisy on human rights issues. China points out that the US has ignored international conventions on human rights and has violated its own constitution after 9/11. The document suggests that the US may have planned the 9/11 attacks itself in order to maintain its supremacy.

The report concludes by calling for an end to US interference in other countries' affairs under the guise of promoting democracy and human rights, and urges Pakistan to rethink its support for the US after 9/11.
