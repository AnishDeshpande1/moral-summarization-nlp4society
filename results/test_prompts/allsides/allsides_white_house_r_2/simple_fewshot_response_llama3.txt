SUMMARY:

President Obama expressed concerns about Russian hacking into the US election system, calling on President-elect Trump to take action to ensure foreign influence is not involved in future elections. Obama criticized Republicans for downplaying the issue and praised journalists for reporting on it. He also blasted Republican officials and voters who express positive views of Russian President Vladimir Putin, saying Ronald Reagan would be "rolling over in his grave" if he knew. The president warned that Russia's actions will have consequences, but emphasized the need for a thoughtful and methodical response. Obama reiterated his commitment to ensuring a smooth transition to Trump's administration, despite their differences on this issue.

END OF SUMMARY.
