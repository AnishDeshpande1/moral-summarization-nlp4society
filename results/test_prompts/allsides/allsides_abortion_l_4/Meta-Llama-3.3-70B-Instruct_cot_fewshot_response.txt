SUMMARY:
Wendy Davis' statement that the goal should be "zero abortions" is seen as a misguided and false objective. Dr. Anne Davis and Dr. Lin-Fan Wang argue that abortion is a necessary medical procedure that saves women's lives and protects their autonomy. Framing abortion as a "necessary evil" is shaming and erases the fact that women choose abortion in various circumstances. The doctors emphasize that there will always be a need for abortion due to human error, bad luck, and changing circumstances. They advocate for policies that understand each woman's unique situation and empower them to make informed decisions. The focus should be on supporting women's health and autonomy, rather than striving for a false and unrealistic goal of "zero abortions". This approach is not only more nuanced but also safer and more common sense. END OF SUMMARY.
