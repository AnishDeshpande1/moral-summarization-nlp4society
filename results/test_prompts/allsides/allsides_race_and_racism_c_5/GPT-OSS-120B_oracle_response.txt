SUMMARY:
Democratic front‑runner Joe Biden blasted President Donald Trump for “fanning” the flames of white **supremacy** and **racism**, saying the president has **aligned** himself with the nation’s darkest forces and **embraced** a political strategy of **hate**, **bigotry** and **division**. Biden linked Trump’s rhetoric about a Hispanic “**invasion**” to the El Paso **shooting** that **killed** 22 and the Dayton **rampage** that **killed** nine, calling the connection “not far at all.” Senator Cory Booker echoed the theme, warning that **hatred** and **white nationalism** are **harvested** only after they are **planted**, and urging action rather than debate over who is a racist. Trump, denying any **racist** intent, condemned **racism**, **bigotry** and **white supremacy**, and proposed mental‑health reforms, social‑media monitoring and keeping guns from potentially violent people, but stopped short of major gun‑law changes. Democrats, including Biden and Booker, are calling for stricter **checks**, **banning** of assault weapons and **requiring** licenses to own firearms as part of their campaign against the cycle of **shootings**, **attacks** and **dead** victims.  
END OF SUMMARY.
