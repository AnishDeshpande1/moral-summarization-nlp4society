SUMMARY:
George Floyd's death at the hands of a Minneapolis police officer has sparked widespread protests and a national movement against police violence, joining a list of high-profile racial incidents that have marked America's history. Floyd's case is unique due to the graphic video footage of his death, which shows an officer kneeling on his neck for nearly nine minutes. This incident may be the tipping point of anger building over recent police killings and systemic racism, exacerbated by the health and economic crises caused by the coronavirus pandemic. The Black Lives Matter movement has gained momentum, with ongoing court cases likely to keep the issue in the news. Experts weigh in that Floyd's death was not just a tragic event but a catalyst for change, highlighting the senselessness of his killing and the weight of systemic racism.

END OF SUMMARY.
