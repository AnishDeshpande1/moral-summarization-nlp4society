SUMMARY:

House Republicans stormed a secure room where lawmakers were conducting an impeachment probe against President Trump, disrupting a scheduled deposition and demanding fairness from Democrats. Republicans claimed Democrats were bypassing constitutional norms and due process, while Democrats accused them of creating a potential security lapse by bringing electronics into the secure area. The protest was sparked by Democrats' restriction of Republican access to evidence and witnesses in the impeachment inquiry. Republicans argue that Trump's lawyers have been denied the chance to see evidence or call their own witnesses, and that Republicans on the committees have been denied subpoena power. Democrats counter that Republicans on the committees can participate and ask questions, and that is good enough. The incident highlights the deepening partisan divide and accusations of unfairness and subversion of the democratic process.

END OF SUMMARY.
