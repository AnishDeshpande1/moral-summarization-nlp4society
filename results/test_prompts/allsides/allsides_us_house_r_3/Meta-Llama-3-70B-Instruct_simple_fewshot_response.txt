SUMMARY:

House Republicans stormed a secure room where lawmakers were conducting an impeachment probe against President Trump, disrupting a scheduled deposition and demanding fairness from Democrats. The move enraged Democrats, who accused Republicans of creating a potential security lapse by bringing electronics into the secure area. Republicans claimed Democrats have broken the rules of fairness by shutting them out of the impeachment process. The protest involved lawmakers who aren't on the impeachment committees, who say they have a right to see what's going on. Democrats countered that Republicans on the committees can participate and ask questions, and that is good enough. The incident highlights the intense partisan tensions surrounding the impeachment inquiry into Trump's communications with Ukraine over former Vice President Joseph R. Biden.

END OF SUMMARY.
