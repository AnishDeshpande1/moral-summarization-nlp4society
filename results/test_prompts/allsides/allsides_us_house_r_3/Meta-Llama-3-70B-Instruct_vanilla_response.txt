SUMMARY:

House Republicans stormed a secure room where lawmakers were conducting an impeachment probe against President Trump, disrupting a scheduled deposition and demanding fairness from Democrats. The move enraged Democrats, who accused Republicans of bringing electronics into the secure area, potentially compromising security. Republicans claimed the hearing was not classified and that Democrats have broken rules of fairness by shutting them out of the impeachment process. The protest was led by lawmakers who aren't on the impeachment committees but claim they have a right to see what's going on. Democrats counter that Republicans on the committees can participate and ask questions. The incident comes as the impeachment inquiry into Trump's communications with Ukraine continues, with Democrats seeking documents and testimony from various officials. A federal judge has ordered the State Department to release Ukraine-related documents within 30 days.

END OF SUMMARY.
