SUMMARY:
House Republicans stormed the secure room where lawmakers have been conducting the impeachment probe against President Trump, demanding fairness from Democrats running the show. They accused Democrats of bypassing constitutional norms and basic standards of due process with their "impeachment obsession." Republicans claim they were denied access to evidence and subpoena power, and that the investigation is not following any precedents for impeachment proceedings. Democrats counter that Republicans on the committees can participate and ask questions, and that is good enough. The protest disrupted a scheduled deposition, and members on both sides are mum on the details of what witnesses have said so far.
