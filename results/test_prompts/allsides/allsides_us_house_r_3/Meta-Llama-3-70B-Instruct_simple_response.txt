SUMMARY:

House Republicans stormed a secure room where lawmakers were conducting an impeachment probe against President Trump, demanding fairness from Democrats. They claimed Democrats were bypassing constitutional norms and due process in the impeachment inquiry. Republicans argued that they were shut out of the process, with Trump's lawyers denied access to evidence and witnesses, and Republicans on the committees restricted from participating. Democrats countered that Republicans on the committees could participate and ask questions, and had access to transcripts and information. The protest led to a security breach, with Republicans bringing electronic devices into the secure area. Democrats threatened ethics charges and a probe into the breach. The incident highlights the intense partisan divide in the impeachment inquiry, with both sides accusing each other of unfairness and lack of transparency.

END OF SUMMARY.
