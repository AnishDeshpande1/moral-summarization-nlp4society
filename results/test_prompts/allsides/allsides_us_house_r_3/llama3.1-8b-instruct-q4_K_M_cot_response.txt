**SUMMARY:**
House Republicans stormed the secure room where lawmakers were conducting an impeachment probe against President Trump, demanding fairness from Democrats. They claimed that the hearing was not classified and that Democrats have broken rules of fairness by shutting them out of the process. Democrats countered that Republicans on the committees can participate and ask questions. The protest involved a sit-in, which lasted for hours, and included claims of a potential security lapse due to unauthorized electronics being brought into the secure area. The hearing was related to Trump's communications with Ukraine over former Vice President Joe Biden, a political opponent. The Democrats' impeachment inquiry is not following precedents for impeachment proceedings, with Republicans denied subpoena power and access to evidence. The situation has sparked outrage among both parties.
