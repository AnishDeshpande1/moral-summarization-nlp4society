House Republicans stormed the secure SCIF where a scheduled deposition of Defense Department official Laura Cooper was to take place, claiming the Democrats running the impeachment inquiry were violating **fairness**, **due process** and **constitutional** **norms**. Democrats countered that Republican members of the Intelligence Committee already have full **access** and that the GOP protest was an unnecessary **obstruction** of the inquiry.
