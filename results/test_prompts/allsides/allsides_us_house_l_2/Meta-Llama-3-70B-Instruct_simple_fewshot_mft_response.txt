Democrats argue that Trump's actions constitute obstruction of justice and an abuse of power, with some even calling for impeachment if McGahn does not respond to the subpoena. The administration's actions are seen as a betrayal of the public's trust and a threat to the integrity of the presidency. By blocking McGahn's testimony, Trump is trying to cheat the system and avoid accountability for his alleged misconduct.
