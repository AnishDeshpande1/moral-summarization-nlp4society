SUMMARY:

As the nation grapples with the aftermath of mass shootings, mayors from across the country are leading their communities forward in the face of unimaginable tragedy. These tragedies are driven by intolerance and a sick obsession with guns, fueled by hate speech broadcast from those in power. The consequences are devastating, resulting in scores of deaths and unimaginable loss for families who have had to mourn the senseless killing of loved ones. Mayors are taking action, but acknowledge that meaningful change will require courageous steps to keep weapons out of the hands of those who should not have them.

The author, a mayor herself, has been inspired by young people who are fighting against gun violence and advocating for a chance to grow up free from fear of violence. These young leaders are part of a larger movement that seeks to empower communities and promote a culture of nonviolence. As the nation navigates this crisis, it is clear that only through collective action can we hope to end the cycle of violence and create a safer future for all.

END OF SUMMARY.
