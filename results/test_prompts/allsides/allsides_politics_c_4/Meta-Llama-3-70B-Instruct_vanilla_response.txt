The article discusses how President Donald Trump continues to deny collusion with Russia, despite mounting evidence against him. At a recent rally, Trump prodded the crowd to chant "Where is the collusion?" just hours after his former attorney Michael Cohen pleaded guilty to crimes including illegal campaign contributions, and his former campaign manager Paul Manafort was found guilty of felony tax and bank fraud charges.
