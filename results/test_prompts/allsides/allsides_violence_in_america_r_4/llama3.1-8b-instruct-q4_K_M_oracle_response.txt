SUMMARY:

Violence erupted in Minnesota on Saturday night, resulting in the deaths of black men Philando Castile and Alton Sterling at the hands of police officers. This incident has led to widespread protests nationwide, including a violent riot in St. Paul where over 100 people were arrested and 21 police officers were injured, some with significant injuries. The protesters pelted police with rocks, rebar, and incendiary devices, including Molotov cocktails, which caused harm to the officers. The situation turned violent when protesters refused to clear Interstate 94, leading police to use smoke bombs to disperse the crowd. The acts of violence towards public servants were condemned by authorities, who stated they will not tolerate such behavior. The incident has raised concerns about police brutality and racism, with many calling for greater accountability and reform.

END OF SUMMARY.
