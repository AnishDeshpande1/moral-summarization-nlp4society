SUMMARY:  
More than 80 people were **arrested** Sunday night after a **protest** in St. Louis **turned** violent for the third consecutive night. Demonstrators **defied** police **orders** and a small group **smashed** windows, **broke** ceramic flowerpots and **threw** debris to **block** a highway ramp. Officers in riot gear used **pepper spray**, tackled protesters and **arrested** them en masse, while police reported confiscating **weapons** and **plastic spray bottles** that **hit** officers. The unrest followed the **acquittal** of former officer Jason Stockley of **first‑degree murder** for the 2011 **shooting** death of Anthony Lamar Smith, a **killing** that many cite as evidence of systemic **racism**. Mayor Lyda Krewson called the trouble caused by “a group of agitators,” and Acting Police Commissioner Lawrence O’Toole vowed to **protect** the city. The three‑night **riots** evoked memories of the 2014 Ferguson **clashes** after the **shooting** of Michael Brown. **Injured** officers and **arrests** rose each night, while residents **feared** further **violence** and **shootings**.  

END OF SUMMARY.
