SUMMARY:

In a shocking display of moral inconsistency, Maryland Democrats rejected a bill aimed at combating gun violence, even as Baltimore Mayor Jack Young begged residents to stop shooting each other to free up hospital beds for coronavirus patients. The rejected bill, pushed by Republican Governor Larry Hogan, would have strengthened penalties for violent criminals. Democrats claimed the bill was too harsh, but their actions are particularly galling given that 85% of Baltimore residents and 84% of African Americans statewide supported the bill. Meanwhile, violent crime has spiked in Baltimore, with seven people shot on Tuesday night alone. The mayor's plea for residents to stop the senseless violence rings hollow when his own party is unwilling to take concrete action to address the problem. By prioritizing the interests of violent criminals over public safety, Maryland Democrats have demonstrated a stunning lack of moral leadership.

END OF SUMMARY.
