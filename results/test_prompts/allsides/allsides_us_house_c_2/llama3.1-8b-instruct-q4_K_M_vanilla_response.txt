SUMMARY:

Sondland testified that US aid to Ukraine was contingent on investigations into Trump's desired topics, including the D.N.C. server conspiracy theory. He stated that Trump didn't care about actual investigations but rather public announcements of them. Sondland also confirmed that he overheard a call from Trump asking if Ukraine would pursue investigations, and replied that they'd do whatever the president wanted.

David Holmes, a senior US Embassy official, corroborated this account, stating that after the call, Sondland said Trump didn't care about Ukraine but only cared about "big stuff" that benefits him. Nunes attempted to downplay Trump's claims of Ukrainian election interference by pointing to public statements by Ukrainian officials, but Sondland's testimony made clear that this was not what Trump had in mind.

Sondland's credibility is questionable, particularly regarding his claimed ignorance of the Burisma-Biden connection. Despite this, his testimony further incriminates Trump and his administration in the Ukraine scandal.

END OF SUMMARY.
