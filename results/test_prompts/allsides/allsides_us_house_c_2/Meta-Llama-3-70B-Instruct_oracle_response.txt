SUMMARY:

Gordon Sondland, the US Ambassador to the EU, testified that he told a Ukrainian official that US aid would likely not resume until Ukraine took action on a public statement, which was a condition set by President Trump. Sondland also testified that Trump didn't care about Ukraine, only about the "big stuff" that benefited him, and that he only wanted a public announcement of investigations, not actual investigations. This announcement would have allowed Trump to defame Joe Biden and muddy the waters about Russia's role in the 2016 election. Sondland's testimony revealed that Trump's goal was to get Ukraine's help in exonerating Russia for its attack on America, by investigating the conspiracy theory that Ukraine hacked the DNC server. The testimony also showed that Trump's claims of Ukrainian election interference were baseless, and that his actions were a shakedown to get what he wanted. Republicans may try to downplay the testimony, but it's clear that Trump's actions were a blatant attempt to meddle in the 2020 election.

END OF SUMMARY.
