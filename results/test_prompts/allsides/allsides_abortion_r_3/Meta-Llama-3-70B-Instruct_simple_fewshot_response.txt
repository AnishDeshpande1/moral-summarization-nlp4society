SUMMARY:

Democratic presidential hopefuls are intensifying their efforts to protect abortion access in response to recent state laws restricting abortion. Sen. Cory Booker proposes creating a "White House Office of Reproductive Freedom" and taking executive and legislative actions to codify Roe v. Wade into federal law. Sen. Kristen Gillibrand argues that laws restricting abortion access are "against Christian faith" and violate the separation of church and state. She also vows to eliminate the Hyde Amendment, which bars federal funding for abortions. Joe Biden's campaign supports enshrining abortion rights into federal law if necessary. Democratic lawmakers are pushing the "Women's Health Protection Act" to protect abortion and Roe v. Wade. The moral framing of this story highlights the importance of protecting women's reproductive rights and autonomy in the face of concerted efforts to restrict access to abortion. By standing up for reproductive freedom, these Democrats are defending a fundamental human right.

END OF SUMMARY.
