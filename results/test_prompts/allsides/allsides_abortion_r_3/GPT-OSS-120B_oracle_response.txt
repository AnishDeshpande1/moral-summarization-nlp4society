SUMMARY:  
Democratic presidential hopefuls are **doubling** down on **access** to abortion after states such as Georgia and Alabama pass laws that **restrict** it. Sen. Cory Booker vows to **create** a “White House Office of Reproductive Freedom” and to take immediate **action** to **protect** bodily **control** by **pursuing** federal **legislation** that would **enshrine** Roe v. Wade and even **fund** abortions abroad. Sen. Kirsten Gillibrand argues that laws **restricting** abortion are “against Christian **faith**” and that imposing such limits violates the Constitution; she would also **eliminate** the Hyde **Amendment**, which **bars** federal dollars from paying for abortions. Front‑runner Joe Biden says he will back Congress to **enshrine** abortion rights if needed. Democrats are also moving to re‑introduce the **Women's Health Protection Act**, a bill that has been **challenged** since 2013, to keep abortion **legalized**. Sen. Richard Blumenthal warns of a “five‑alarm fire” of **danger** to women’s reproductive rights and calls for a **command** to act now, noting the **risk** to lives if **steps** are not taken.  
END OF SUMMARY.
