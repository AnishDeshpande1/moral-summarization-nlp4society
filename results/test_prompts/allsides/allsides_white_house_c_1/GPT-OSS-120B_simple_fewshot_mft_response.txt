SUMMARY:
President Trump denied using a vulgar “shithole” slur for Haiti, El Salvador and African nations, but his attempts to deflect the backlash only deepened accusations that he is abusing presidential authority to subvert fair immigration policy. By labeling Democratic DACA proposals “outlandish” and threatening to kill the program unless his wall and “merit‑based” measures pass, Trump is portrayed as prioritizing a selective, elitist agenda that harms vulnerable young immigrants and the nations he disparaged. Lawmakers and human‑rights officials condemned the remarks as racist, shameful and contrary to the nation’s commitment to equality, emphasizing the unfairness of targeting people based on skin colour or country of origin. The article frames Trump’s conduct as a subversive misuse of power that inflicts moral and material harm on marginalized groups while cheating the principles of justice and equal treatment that the United States claims to uphold. END OF SUMMARY.
