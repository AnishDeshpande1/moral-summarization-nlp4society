SUMMARY:

House Speaker John Boehner is calling on President Obama to release emails that show how the White House altered Benghazi talking points to downplay the role of extremist groups linked to al Qaeda. Boehner claims that White House officials made deliberate changes to avoid embarrassing the State Department for missing early warnings. The House GOP report also found no evidence that anyone within the administration raised concerns about preserving classified information.

Boehner is seeking the release of an email from a top State Department official, Beth Jones, sent on September 12, which referred to "Islamic terrorists" carrying out the attack. This contradicts earlier statements by Ambassador Susan Rice, who said the attack was a spontaneous demonstration.

Secretary of State John Kerry has vowed to leave no stone unturned in investigating the matter and answering questions about the attack. He promised that any culpability will be handled appropriately.

END OF SUMMARY.
