SUMMARY:

House Speaker John Boehner is calling on President Obama to release emails that allegedly show the White House's involvement in changing the Benghazi attack "talking points". Boehner claims that senior White House officials directed changes to the talking points to remove references to extremist groups linked to al Qaeda, in order to avoid embarrassing the State Department. The emails, which were reviewed privately by House committees, contradict the White House's claim that only stylistic changes were made. Meanwhile, Secretary of State John Kerry has vowed to "leave no stone unturned" in investigating the matter and answering questions about the attack. Kerry promised to get answers and put the issue "to bed", and said that any recent evidence will be considered.

END OF SUMMARY.
