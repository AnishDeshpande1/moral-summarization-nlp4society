SUMMARY: Attorney General William Barr announced a new DOJ task force to **investigate**, **prosecute** and **prevent** “violent anti‑government extremists of all persuasions.” In a June 26 memo he warned that some groups **pretend** to champion **freedom** and **progress** while actually spreading **anarchy**, **destruction** and **coercion**. The memo cites recent **protests** after George Floyd’s **death**—including **kneeling** incidents—that have **descended** into **looting**, **arson** and other **violent** **threats**. Barr says extremists have **engaged** in **attacks** on police, **shooting** incidents, and **patrolling** zones where they **check** IDs and **extort** businesses, thereby **depriving** owners of property and being **complicit** in **tolerance** of lawlessness. He asserts these actors **deny** constitutional rights, **resort** to illegal methods, and are **united** in sowing **chaos** that **poses** a **disorder** threat. The task force will supply training, share intelligence with the FBI and local agencies, and **arrest** perpetrators. The announcement coincided with President Trump’s executive order **signing** a measure to **combat** the **acts** of vandalism, promising **prison** terms for those who **under‑cut** public order and **tried** to become “law unto themselves.”  
END OF SUMMARY.
