SUMMARY:

Attorney General William Barr has established a task force to combat "violent anti-government extremists of all persuasions" who engage in destructive and coercive acts, masquerading as advocates for freedom and progress. The task force aims to investigate, prosecute, and prevent extremist violence, ultimately eliminating it as a threat to public safety and the rule of law. Barr condemned the recent acts of violence, looting, and arson during protests, which have descended into anarchy and chaos. He emphasized that these extremists deny constitutional rights to others and resort to illegal methods, undermining the core values of a democratic society governed by law. The task force will provide training and resources to local law enforcement to arrest perpetrators of violence and work closely with the FBI to develop information about violent anti-government extremist individuals, networks, and movements. This initiative is crucial in maintaining authority, preventing harm, and ensuring fairness and justice in the face of lawlessness and subversion.

END OF SUMMARY.
