**SUMMARY:**  
Attorney General William Barr announced a new DOJ task force to combat “violent anti‑government extremists of all persuasions.” In a June 26 memo he warned that some groups masquerade as champions of “freedom” and “progress” while actually spreading anarchy, destruction, and coercion. The task force will investigate, prosecute, and prevent violent, lawless acts that threaten the constitutional rule of law and public safety. Barr cited recent unrest after George Floyd’s death, noting that while many protests remained peaceful, a minority of extremist actors engaged in assaults, arson, and sabotage, sometimes aided by foreign entities seeking chaos and disorder. The initiative will provide training to local police, share intelligence with the FBI, and target networks that deny others their constitutional rights. The announcement coincided with President Trump’s executive order protecting American monuments from vandalism and “lawless” attacks.
