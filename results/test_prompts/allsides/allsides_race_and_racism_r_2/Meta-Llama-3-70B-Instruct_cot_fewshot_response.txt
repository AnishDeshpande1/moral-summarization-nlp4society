Attorney General William Barr has established a task force to combat "violent anti-government extremists of all persuasions" who engage in "anarchy, destruction, and coercion." The task force will provide training and resources to local law enforcement to arrest perpetrators of violence and work closely with the FBI to develop information about violent anti-government extremist individuals and networks.
