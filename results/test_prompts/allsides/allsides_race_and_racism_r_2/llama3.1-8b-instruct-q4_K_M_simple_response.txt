SUMMARY: 
Attorney General William Barr has established a task force to combat violent anti-government extremists, citing recent protests as evidence of the threat they pose. Barr notes that these groups often pretend to advocate for freedom and progress but are actually forces of anarchy and coercion. The task force will investigate and prosecute those who commit violent acts, prevent extremist violence before it happens, and ultimately eliminate it as a threat to public safety and the rule of law. Barr's memo highlights instances of attacks on police officers, destruction of property, and threats to innocent bystanders during recent protests. He also suggests that some of these extremists may be supported by foreign entities seeking to sow chaos and disorder. The task force will provide training and resources to local law enforcement and work closely with the FBI to gather information about violent anti-government extremist individuals and networks.

END OF SUMMARY
