SUMMARY:

Attorney General William Barr has established a task force to combat "violent anti-government extremists of all persuasions" who engage in destructive and coercive acts, masquerading as advocates for freedom and progress. The task force aims to investigate, prosecute, and prevent extremist violence, ultimately eliminating it as a threat to public safety and the rule of law. Barr condemned the violence and anarchy that has marred some protests, including attacks on police, destruction of property, and threats to innocent bystanders. He noted that extremist groups, including those who identify as Antifa and Bugaloo, seek to deny constitutional rights to others and resort to illegal methods. The task force will provide training and resources to local law enforcement to arrest perpetrators of violence and work closely with the FBI to gather intelligence on extremist individuals and networks. This effort is crucial to uphold the core constitutional values of a democratic society governed by law.

END OF SUMMARY.
