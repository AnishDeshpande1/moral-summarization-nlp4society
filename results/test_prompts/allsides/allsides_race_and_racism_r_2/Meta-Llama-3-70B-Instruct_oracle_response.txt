SUMMARY:

Attorney General William Barr has established a task force to combat "violent anti-government extremists of all persuasions" who pretend to profess a message of freedom and progress, but in fact, are forces of anarchy, destruction, and coercion. The task force aims to investigate and prosecute those who commit violent acts, prevent extremist violence, and ultimately eliminate it as a threat to public safety and the rule of law. Recent protests have descended into scenes of looting, arson, and violence, with attacks on police officers and government officials, destruction of public and private property, and threats to innocent bystanders. Barr noted that extremist groups targeted by the initiative seek to deny constitutional rights to others and resort to illegal methods. The task force will provide training and resources to local law enforcement to arrest perpetrators of violence and work closely with the FBI to develop detailed information about violent anti-government extremist individuals, networks, and movements.

END OF SUMMARY.
