SUMMARY:  
Rev. Franklin Graham, evangelist and son of Billy Graham, had a UK tour stop scheduled for June 2020 at ACC Liverpool. The venue cancelled the event, stating Graham’s biblical view of marriage as between one man and one woman conflicts with its values. Local groups, including the Liverpool Labour LGBT Network, protested, labeling him a “homophobic hate preacher” and petitioning council members. In response, Graham issued an open letter to the UK LGBTQ community, asserting he is coming to share the Gospel, not to condemn. He reiterated his belief that homosexuality is sinful but emphasized that all people are sinners and that the Gospel is inclusive, welcoming everyone. He defended free speech and religious liberty, inviting LGBTQ individuals to attend his events.  

END OF SUMMARY.
