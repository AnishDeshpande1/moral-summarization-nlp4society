SUMMARY:
Evangelist Franklin Graham's event at ACC Liverpool was canceled due to his views on marriage, which the venue deemed "incompatible with our values". Protesters had criticized the venue for allowing Graham to share the Gospel in the space, labeling him a "homophobic hate preacher". In response, Graham wrote an open letter to the LGBTQ community, stating he is coming to the UK not to condemn them but to present the Gospel. He acknowledged that the Bible defines homosexuality as sin, but emphasized that God's message of forgiveness and love is inclusive of all people, including those in the LGBTQ community. Graham defended his right to free speech and religious liberty, assuring those in the LGBTQ community that they are welcome to attend one of his events.

END OF SUMMARY.
