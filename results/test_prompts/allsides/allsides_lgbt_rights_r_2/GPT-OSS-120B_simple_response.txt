SUMMARY:  
Rev. Franklin Graham, the evangelical son of Billy Graham, had been booked to speak at ACC Liverpool as part of a UK tour, but the venue cancelled the event, saying his biblical definition of marriage “one man and one woman” clashes with its inclusive values. Local activists, including the Liverpool Labour LGBT Network, denounced Graham as a “homophobic hate preacher” and pressured councilors to block his appearance at the M&S Arena. In response, Graham issued an open letter to Britain’s LGBTQ community, claiming he is not there to condemn but to share the Gospel, which he described as “inclusive” and motivated by love, even while reaffirming his belief that homosexuality is sinful. He invoked religious liberty and invited LGBTQ people to attend his shows, insisting the message is for everyone.  

END OF SUMMARY.
