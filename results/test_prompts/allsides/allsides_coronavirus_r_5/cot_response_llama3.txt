SUMMARY:

The lockdown imposed due to the coronavirus pandemic is not just a health-versus-health issue, but also a devastating economic crisis that will have long-lasting health implications. With hospital beds and testing available, it's time to reopen the economy and mitigate risks. The current approach of staying-at-home until a cure is found will devastate our economy and nation's health. The statistics are alarming: suicide rates are spiking, cardiac procedures are being delayed, cancer screenings have declined, and childhood vaccinations have nearly ground to a halt. Moreover, economic inequality is widening, which has its own health implications.

It's essential to show sympathy and respect for the working class who are bearing the brunt of this pandemic. They are not just asking for the choice to take risks safely and responsibly but also need support to put food on their table and care for their families. Instead of judgment from the ruling class, we should prioritize the health and well-being of these workers. It's time to ease up restrictions and manage this virus prudently.

END OF SUMMARY.
