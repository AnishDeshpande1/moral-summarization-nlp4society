SUMMARY:
The article argues that Democratic leaders botched the pandemic response, citing New York Governor Andrew Cuomo’s disastrous nursing‑home orders that led to over 6,000 deaths, contrasted with Florida’s far lower nursing‑home fatality rate. It claims that Republican‑led states such as Arizona, Florida and Texas have avoided even basic measures like mask mandates, and that GOP governors blocked localities from imposing them. The piece suggests that liberal support for Black‑Lives‑Matter protests reinforced conservatives’ view that strict distancing is a hypocritical tool of social control, fueling a wave of harassment and threats against public‑health workers. With “red America” lacking political will, the author warns the United States will continue to be pummeled by COVID‑19, while other nations with competent leadership begin to recover. The piece concludes that even as the death toll nears 120,000— a figure cited by Trump ally Lindsey Graham as a “limited” casualty count—President Trump has already lost the “war” against the virus.

END OF SUMMARY.
