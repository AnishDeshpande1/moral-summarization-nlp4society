SUMMARY:

Senate Majority Leader Mitch McConnell is calling for the Senate Intelligence Committee to investigate recent CIA findings that the Russian government tried to influence the U.S. presidential election. While McConnell acknowledges the allegations as "disturbing," he dismisses calls for a special select committee to review the matter, arguing that the Intelligence Committee is capable of conducting a complete review. This approach is criticized as inadequate, as it may not provide a thorough and bipartisan investigation into Russian meddling. The moral framing of this issue is clear: the integrity of the democratic process is at stake, and a comprehensive investigation is necessary to uncover the truth and protect the country from foreign interference. By downplaying the need for a thorough investigation, McConnell and the Trump administration are undermining the integrity of the electoral process and the rule of law. END OF SUMMARY.
