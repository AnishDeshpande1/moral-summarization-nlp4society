SUMMARY:

The Democratic party is increasingly embracing extreme pro-abortion positions, with some lawmakers supporting abortion up to 40 weeks of pregnancy. Virginia state legislator Kathy Tran's bill, backed by Governor Ralph Northam, suggests that in some cases, a full-term child could be delivered and then allowed to die. Similar laws have been passed in New York, and leading Democratic presidential candidates have endorsed federal legislation that would wipe away state laws protecting unborn children. These Democrats claim that late-term abortions are only done for compelling medical reasons, but the evidence doesn't support this, and their laws don't require such reasons anyway. This radical position is morally unjust, as it treats unborn children as non-persons and exposes them to lethal violence. It's the moral obligation of all to reconsider and repudiate this stance, and for journalists, Catholic bishops, and the Supreme Court to expose and correct this grievous error.

END OF SUMMARY.
