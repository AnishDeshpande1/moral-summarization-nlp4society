SUMMARY:

The Democratic party is increasingly embracing an extreme pro-abortion stance, with some lawmakers and presidential candidates supporting abortion "all the way up to 40 weeks" without any meaningful restrictions. This position is morally unjust and harmful, as it denies the dignity and human rights of unborn children, particularly those who are viable and suffer no abnormalities. The laws and bills proposed by Democrats are designed to be unenforceable and deceptive, allowing for abortion on demand at any stage of pregnancy. This radical stance is out of touch with public opinion and ignores the moral obligations that come with protecting human life. It is unjust to treat unborn children as non-persons and expose them to lethal violence, and those who support this position must reconsider and repudiate it. The media, Catholic bishops, and the Supreme Court also have a moral responsibility to expose and correct this grievous error.

END OF SUMMARY.
