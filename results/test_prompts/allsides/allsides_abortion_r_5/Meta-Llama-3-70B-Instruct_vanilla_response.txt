SUMMARY:

The article discusses the increasing support among Democrats for abortion up to 40 weeks of pregnancy, citing examples from Virginia and New York. Virginia state legislator Kathy Tran's bill, supported by Governor Ralph Northam, suggests that a full-term child could be delivered and then allowed to die in certain circumstances. Many leading Democratic presidential candidates have endorsed similar federal legislation, which would override state laws protecting unborn children. The article argues that this stance is unjust and morally wrong, as it treats unborn children differently from born infants. It calls on Democrats to reconsider their position, Republicans to expose it, journalists to report on it accurately, and Catholic bishops to address the moral error of Catholic politicians who support it. The article also urges the Supreme Court to reverse its decision allowing abortion-on-demand at any stage of pregnancy.

END OF SUMMARY.
