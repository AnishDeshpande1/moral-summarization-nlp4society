SUMMARY:

Democrats are increasingly supporting explicit abortion up to 40 weeks, with some even advocating for the denial of legal protections for viable unborn children. This extreme position is unjust and lays moral obligations on all of us. The laws they support are designed to be unenforceable and deceptive, allowing for abortion at any stage of pregnancy without restriction. Leading Democratic presidential candidates have endorsed similar federal legislation, which would wipe away state laws protecting unborn children. They claim that late-term abortions are only done for compelling medical reasons, but the available evidence does not support this contention. In reality, these laws do not require such reasons, and would allow for the killing of unborn children who are viable and suffer no abnormalities. This is a manifestly unjust and outrageous position that must be reconsidered and repudiated. It is the moral obligation of all of us to expose this indefensible stance and to work towards reversing this mistake.

END OF SUMMARY.
