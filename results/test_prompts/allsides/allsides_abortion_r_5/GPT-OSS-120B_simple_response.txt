SUMMARY: The article denounces the growing Democratic support for unrestricted abortion up to 40 weeks, citing Virginia legislator Kathy Tran and Governor Ralph Northam’s extreme proposals. It argues that recent state laws—such as New York’s “health‑exception” rule—are deliberately vague and unenforceable, effectively allowing late‑term abortions without genuine medical justification. The author claims that killing a viable fetus is no different from killing a two‑month‑old infant, labeling it “manifestly unjust” and asserting that even indirect participation carries moral responsibility. Consequently, the piece calls on Democrats to abandon these positions, urges Republicans, journalists, and Catholic bishops to expose and condemn the stance, and demands that the Supreme Court overturn its abortion‑on‑demand precedent as soon as possible. END OF SUMMARY.
