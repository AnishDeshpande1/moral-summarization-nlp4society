SUMMARY:
The article condemns a growing Democratic push to legalize abortion “all the way up to 40 weeks,” labeling it a radical subversion of moral authority. It argues that legislators like Virginia’s Kathy Tran and Governor Ralph Northam are promoting policies that treat viable fetuses as non‑persons, violating the sanctity of unborn life and inflicting grave harm. By framing the proposed health‑exception statutes as deliberately unenforceable and deceptive, the author claims Democrats are cheating the public of honest debate and fairness, while exploiting governmental power to erode legal protections. The piece calls on Republicans, journalists, and Catholic bishops to expose this unjust agenda, restore proper authority, and protect the vulnerable from lethal violence. It urges the Supreme Court to overturn its acceptance of unrestricted late‑term abortion, asserting that allowing such killings is morally wrong and demands collective action to uphold sanctity, care, and fair treatment of the unborn.  
END OF SUMMARY.
