SUMMARY:
The article denounces the growing Democratic support for abortion “all the way up to 40 weeks,” calling it a deceptive, unenforceable policy that seeks to wipe away state protections for unborn children. It argues that Democrats, from Virginia’s Kathy Tran to New York legislators and presidential candidates, intend to deny legal protections even for viable fetuses, allowing killing that is unjust and amounts to lethal violence. The piece insists that such actions differ not from the killing of a two‑month‑old infant, which is prohibited and punished, and therefore imposes moral obligations on everyone to reconsider and repudiate this stance. It urges Republicans, journalists, Catholic bishops, and the Supreme Court to expose the spin, report the truth, and find the earliest chance to reverse this mistake, which it describes as absurd and outrageous. END OF SUMMARY.
