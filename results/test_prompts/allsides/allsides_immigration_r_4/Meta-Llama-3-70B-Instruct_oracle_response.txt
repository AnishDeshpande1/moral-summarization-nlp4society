SUMMARY:

The absence of law enforcement and the lack of consequences for illegal actions have led to a breakdown in civilization and legality. When individuals are not punished for violating the law, it sends a message that laws can be ignored, and this can have far-reaching consequences. The migrant caravan making its way to the US border is a result of this laxity, as is the rise of violent protests on college campuses. Celebrities who fantasize about harming the president also assume they are above the law, and their actions go unpunished. The result is a society where the rule of law is weakened, and individuals feel justified in taking extralegal measures to achieve their goals. This moral justification of illegal means can boomerang, leading to anarchy and a loss of freedom. As Immanuel Kant noted, "anarchy is law and freedom without force." It is essential to reestablish deterrence and enforce laws to maintain a civilized society.

END OF SUMMARY.
