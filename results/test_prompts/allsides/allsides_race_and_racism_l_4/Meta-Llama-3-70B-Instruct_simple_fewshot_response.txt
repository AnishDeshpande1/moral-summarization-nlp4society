SUMMARY:

President Trump responded to former President Obama's statement on mass shootings, citing a Fox & Friends segment that claimed Obama had 32 mass shootings during his presidency. Trump's response came after Obama called on Americans to reject language that feeds fear and hatred, and to hold public officials accountable for changing gun laws. Trump has faced criticism for not addressing gun control and for emboldening white nationalists through his rhetoric.
