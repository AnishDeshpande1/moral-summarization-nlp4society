SUMMARY:

A 17-year-old boy, Dimitrios Pagourtzis, has been charged with fatally shooting 10 people, including 8 students and 2 teachers, at a high school in Santa Fe, Texas. According to the mother of one of the victims, Pagourtzis had made aggressive advances towards her daughter, Shana Fisher, 16, who was killed in the attack, and had been rejected by her. The mother believes that Pagourtzis' actions were motivated by this rejection. Pagourtzis has confessed to the killings, but authorities have not yet released a motive. The shooting is the fourth-deadliest mass shooting at a U.S. public school in modern history. The community is grieving, with many attending vigils and services to remember the victims. The incident has reignited the national debate over gun ownership and control.

END OF SUMMARY.
