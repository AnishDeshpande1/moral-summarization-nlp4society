SUMMARY:

The COVID-19 pandemic has brought a new threat to global stability and society - disinformation. As people turn to the internet for information, misinformation and conspiracy theories about the virus are spreading rapidly. Examples include claims that garlic or water can prevent infection, or that the virus is caused by 5G Wi-Fi or is a bioweapon. Despite the danger of the virus, disinformation is filling the void left by the lack of clear facts and strong leadership from governments. Social media platforms have enabled disinformation to spread quickly, with serious consequences. Even government officials, such as Donald Trump, have spread lies about the virus, hindering public safety efforts. Disinformation not only misinforms but also destroys public trust in objective truth, creating a cycle of confusion and disillusionment. It's essential to hold ourselves responsible for not perpetuating disinformation, which only creates doubt and panic, and instead focus on educating the public on the facts and ways to protect themselves.

END OF SUMMARY.
