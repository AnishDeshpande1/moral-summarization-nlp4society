**SUMMARY:**  
The COVID‑19 pandemic is a public‑health crisis compounded by a parallel outbreak of misinformation and disinformation. False claims—from garlic cures to 5G conspiracy theories—have spread as quickly as the virus, creating dangerous confusion and panic. High‑profile falsehoods, including repeated lies from the White House, have misled the public, undermining trust in objective truth and endangering lives. This wave of harmful misinformation erodes critical thinking, fuels ignorance, and threatens the consensus needed for effective health measures. To protect public safety, individuals must act responsibly, reject falsehoods, and prioritize factual, evidence‑based information.
