**SUMMARY:**

The COVID-19 pandemic has brought about a new and deadly threat to global stability: disinformation. As the virus spreads rapidly, misinformation and disingenuous claims are spreading just as fast, imperiling countless lives every day. The federal government's mismanagement of the crisis has contributed to this problem, with President Trump repeatedly lying and misleading the public about the severity of the outbreak. These lies not only exhaust the public's critical thinking but also destroy their confidence in objective truth. Disinformation muddies public discussions on public safety, perpetuating fallacies that hinder efforts to combat the pandemic. In a time where every day counts, it is crucial for individuals to hold themselves responsible and not abet the proliferation of disinformation, whether from social media or places of authority.
