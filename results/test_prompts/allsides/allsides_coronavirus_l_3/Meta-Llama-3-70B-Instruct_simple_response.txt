SUMMARY:

The COVID-19 pandemic has brought a new threat to our stability and society - disinformation. As people turn to the internet for clarity and reassurance, misinformation and conspiracy theories about the virus are spreading rapidly online. These false claims, including ones that eating garlic or taking sips of water can prevent infection, are not only false but also dangerous. They can lead to confusion, panic, and a lack of trust in objective truth. Even the White House has contributed to this disinformation, with President Trump repeatedly lying and misleading the public about the virus. This has serious consequences, as it hinders the spread of accurate information and puts lives at risk. It is crucial that we hold ourselves responsible for not perpetuating disinformation and instead educate the public on the facts and ways to protect themselves. Every day and hour count, and it is our moral duty to prioritize truth and public health.

END OF SUMMARY.
