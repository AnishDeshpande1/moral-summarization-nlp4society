In this critical moment, it's essential to prioritize fact-based information and hold ourselves accountable for not contributing to the spread of disinformation.

The article emphasizes that disinformation is a significant threat to global stability and society, and it's crucial to address it head-on. Ultimately, it starts with individual responsibility in resisting the proliferation of disinformation.
