SUMMARY:

The Black Lives Matter movement is gaining momentum, with Americans increasingly acknowledging systemic racism in the criminal justice system. Recent surveys show a significant shift in public opinion, with more people recognizing unfair treatment of black people in all aspects of society, including police encounters. The media is also paying more attention, with databases tracking police shootings and Google searches showing increased interest in the issue. Prosecutors are taking police shootings more seriously, with several filing charges against officers involved in high-profile killings. Lawmakers, including President Obama and Democratic presidential candidate Hillary Clinton, are pushing for police accountability measures such as body cameras and improved tracking of police killings. While Republicans and white Americans remain skeptical, the trend is clear: Black Lives Matter is winning, and the movement's moral imperative to address systemic racism and police violence is finally being heard. 

END OF SUMMARY.
