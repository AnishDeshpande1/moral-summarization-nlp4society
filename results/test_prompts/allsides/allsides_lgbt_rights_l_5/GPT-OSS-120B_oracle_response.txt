SUMMARY:  
The Obama administration announced that vision, health, life, dental, and long‑term‑care benefits are now **available** and **open** to legally **married** same‑sex spouses of federal employees. The Office of Personnel Management’s memo **repeating** that all legally married couples will receive the same **access** treats these couples **equally** and **expands** federal benefits in line with the Supreme Court’s **overturning** of DOMA’s Section 3. President Obama, **working** toward making the **reality** of nationwide recognition a fact, **declared** that gay and straight couples **deserve** the same **respect** and **protection** under the law. He **directed** the Justice Department to **lead** the **implementation** and **repeal** of the discriminatory provision, and the Pentagon and Attorney General Holder pledged to **expand** coverage quickly. The **changes** are described as a **meaningful**, **positive** **step** that **extends** the **promise** of equal treatment to thousands of federal workers and their families, with the administration **directed** to **implement** the ruling **as swiftly as possible**.  

END OF SUMMARY.
