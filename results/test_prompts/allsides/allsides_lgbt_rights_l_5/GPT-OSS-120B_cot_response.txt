**SUMMARY:**  
The Obama administration announced that same‑sex spouses of federal employees can now apply for the same suite of benefits—health, vision, dental, life, and long‑term‑care insurance—as opposite‑sex spouses. Acting OPM director Elaine Kaplan’s guidance emphasizes that all legally married couples will receive equal access, regardless of state law, reinforcing the administration’s commitment to justice and equal protection. President Obama called the move a critical, landmark step toward real‑izing the Supreme Court’s decision that all married couples deserve the same respect and protection under federal law. Defense Secretary Chuck Hagel and Attorney General Eric Holder echoed the sentiment, noting the positive, important impact on thousands of gay and lesbian federal workers and their families. The Justice Department will coordinate with other agencies to implement the changes swiftly and smoothly.
