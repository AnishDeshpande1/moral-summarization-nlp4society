SUMMARY:
The Obama administration has announced that same-sex spouses of federal employees can begin applying for benefits, including health insurance and retirement accounts, immediately. This move is a critical first step towards implementing the landmark Supreme Court decision declaring that all married couples, gay and straight, should be treated equally under federal law. The administration believes that couples married in one state should be treated as married in all 50 states, and is working on the legalities of making that reality. This expansion of access to federal benefits will make a meaningful, positive difference in the lives of many, ensuring that same-sex spouses are entitled to equal protection and equal treatment under the law. The Justice Department will continue to coordinate with other federal agencies to implement this ruling as swiftly and smoothly as possible. END OF SUMMARY.
