Her actions are a blatant disregard for the authority of the Constitution and the rule of law, and an affront to the principles of fairness and equality. This support for Davis' subversion of the law is a disturbing example of the willingness of some politicians to prioritize their own moral beliefs over the rights and dignity of others. By cheering on Davis' defiance, these candidates are promoting a harmful and discriminatory agenda that seeks to undermine the principles of justice and equality.
