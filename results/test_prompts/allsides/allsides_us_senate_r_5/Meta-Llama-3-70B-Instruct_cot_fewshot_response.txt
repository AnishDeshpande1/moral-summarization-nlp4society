Republican Sen. Lindsey Graham is demanding more information from the White House about the night of the Benghazi terror attacks before confirming President Obama's nominees to run the CIA and the Defense Department. Graham is holding the administration accountable for its response to the attacks, which killed four Americans. He claims the White House has delayed, denied, deceived, and stonewalled in providing details about the president's actions during the crisis. Graham wants to know if the president pressed the Libyan government to let a response team reach the U.S. outpost under attack. Democrats, including Sen.
