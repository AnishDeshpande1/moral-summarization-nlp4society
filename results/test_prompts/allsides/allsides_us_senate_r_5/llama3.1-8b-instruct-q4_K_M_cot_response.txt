**SUMMARY:** 
Republican Sen. Lindsey Graham has threatened to block the confirmation of President Obama's nominees for Defense Secretary and CIA Director unless the White House provides more information about its response to the Benghazi terror attacks. Graham, who has been critical of the White House's handling of the incident, said that he wants to know if the president had made any efforts to intervene on behalf of the Americans under attack. He accused the White House of being "delayed, denied, deceived and stonewalled" in their responses to his questions. Graham stated that he would not allow Hagel's confirmation as Defense Secretary without an "accounting" from the White House. Democratic Sen. Jack Reed criticized Graham's actions as "unprecedented" and accused him of using the tragedy to block nominations.
