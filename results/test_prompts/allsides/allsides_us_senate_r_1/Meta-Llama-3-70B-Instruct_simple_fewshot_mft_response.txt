SUMMARY:

The Senate is set to consider the Born-Alive Abortion Survivors Protection Act, a bill that exposes the disturbing logic of unlimited legal abortion. The legislation requires doctors to provide ordinary medical care to infants born alive after an attempted abortion, a basic human right. However, Democrats have consistently opposed this bill, revealing their commitment to the subversion of authority and the harm of innocent human life. They claim that "born-alive" infants don't exist, but evidence proves otherwise. They also argue that laws already exist to prevent infanticide, which is false. By opposing this bill, Democrats are essentially saying that the right to abortion includes the right to deny medical care to a newborn infant, a blatant disregard for human life and fairness. This legislation will succeed in exposing the dehumanizing rationale at the heart of the argument for abortion, and lawmakers who oppose it will be forced to confront the arbitrary nature of denying rights to human beings. The bill is a crucial step towards protecting the most vulnerable among us and upholding the values of care, fairness, and respect for human life.

END OF SUMMARY.
