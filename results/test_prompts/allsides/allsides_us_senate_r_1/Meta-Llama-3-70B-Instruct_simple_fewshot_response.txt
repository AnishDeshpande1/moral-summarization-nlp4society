SUMMARY:

The Senate is set to consider the Born-Alive Abortion Survivors Protection Act, a bill that exposes the Democrats' commitment to unlimited legal abortion. The legislation requires doctors to provide medical care to infants born alive after an attempted abortion. Democrats have opposed the bill, claiming that "born-alive" infants don't exist and that laws already prevent infanticide. However, these claims are false, and the bill simply requires doctors to treat newborns who survive abortions with the same care as any other newborn infant. The Democrats' opposition to the bill reveals their dehumanizing rationale, which prioritizes the right to abortion over the right to life of a newborn infant. If lawmakers support this bill, they risk exposing the arbitrary nature of denying rights to a human being inside the womb and granting them rights after birth. The bill will succeed in exposing the cruel logic of the pro-abortion argument.
