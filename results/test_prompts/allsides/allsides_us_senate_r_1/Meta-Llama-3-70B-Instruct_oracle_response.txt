SUMMARY:

The Senate is set to consider the Born-Alive Abortion Survivors Protection Act, which exposes Democrats' commitment to unlimited legal abortion. The bill requires doctors to provide ordinary medical care to infants born alive after an attempted abortion. Despite Democrats' claims that "born-alive" infants don't exist, evidence suggests that infants do survive late-term abortion procedures. Democrats also argue that laws already exist to prevent infanticide, but this is false, as only 33 states have such laws in place. By opposing this bill, Democrats are essentially supporting the right to deny medical care to a newborn infant who was targeted for abortion, which is a dehumanizing rationale. The bill doesn't regulate abortion, but rather requires doctors to treat newborns with the same care as any other infant. Democrats' opposition to this bill reveals their arbitrary stance on denying rights to human beings inside the womb and granting them rights after birth. END OF SUMMARY.
