SUMMARY:
The Senate is set to consider the Born-Alive Abortion Survivors Protection Act, a bill that exposes Democrats' commitment to the logic of unlimited legal abortion. The legislation would require doctors to provide ordinary medical care to infants who are born alive after an attempted abortion. Democrats have presented flimsy rationales for their opposition, claiming that "born-alive" infants don't exist and that laws already exist to prevent infanticide. However, these claims are false, and the bill merely requires doctors to treat newborns who survive abortions with the same degree of care as any other newborn infant. The bill's opponents maintain that the right to abortion includes the right to let an infant die of neglect if they were planned to have been killed a second before. This dehumanizing rationale is at the heart of the argument for abortion. END OF SUMMARY.
