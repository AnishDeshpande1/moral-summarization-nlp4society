SUMMARY:

New York City Mayor Michael R. Bloomberg's efforts to institute gun bans have been unsuccessful in stopping criminals from obtaining guns. Instead, he has resorted to policing tactics like stop and frisk to take illegal guns away from bad guys. However, his one area of success is no longer allowed, and he's looking for someone to blame. The City Council overrode his veto of two bills that will create a New York Police Department inspector general to oversee tactics like stop and frisk and allow more categories of people to sue the city for racial profiling. Bloomberg claims this will put minorities at higher risk, as they have benefited most from crime reduction. The mayor's stop and frisk program has resulted in 8,000 illegal guns being taken off the streets and the murder rate being cut in half. Bloomberg is wrong to link the NRA to the plaintiffs in the federal lawsuits, and his agenda against guns and the NRA is misguided. The NRA advocates for tougher sentencing for possession of illegal guns, which is a proven method to make cities safer. The federal appeals court should overturn the absurd ruling that taking illegal guns off bad guys is racist.

END OF SUMMARY.
