SUMMARY:

An attempt to fabricate sexual misconduct allegations against Special Counsel Robert Mueller has been exposed and referred to the FBI for investigation. The scheme, which appears to have involved conservative activists Jacob Wohl and Jack Burkman, aimed to pay women to make false claims against Mueller. Wohl, a 20-year-old conservative, and Burkman, a Republican lobbyist, were involved in the plot, which was revealed on Twitter and sparked an investigation by journalists and online researchers. The supposed private intelligence company, Surefire Intelligence, was found to be essentially a one-man operation run by Wohl. The far-right website Gateway Pundit posted a heavily redacted document making a rape allegation against Mueller, but later took it down and said it was "looking into" the allegations against Wohl. Mueller's office referred the matter to the FBI, and the incident has sparked concerns about the tactics used to discredit the special counsel.

END OF SUMMARY.
