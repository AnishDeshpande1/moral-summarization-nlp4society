SUMMARY:

Conservative activists Jacob Wohl and Jack Burkman have been accused of attempting to drum up false sexual misconduct allegations against special counsel Robert Mueller. Wohl, a 20-year-old pro-Trump Twitter personality, may have been involved in a plot to smear Mueller, while Burkman, a Republican lobbyist, claimed that his company Surefire Intelligence was investigating the allegations. However, it appears that Surefire is essentially a one-man operation run by Wohl himself.

The FBI has taken the matter seriously and launched an investigation after Mueller's office referred the allegations to them. The whole incident has sparked controversy and raised questions about the motivations of Wohl and Burkman. While their scheme may have been intended to smear Mueller, it ultimately ended up drawing attention away from other important issues in the days leading up to the midterm elections.

The incident highlights the importance of fact-checking and verifying information before reporting on sensitive topics. It also raises concerns about the tactics used by some conservative activists to manipulate public opinion and discredit their opponents.
