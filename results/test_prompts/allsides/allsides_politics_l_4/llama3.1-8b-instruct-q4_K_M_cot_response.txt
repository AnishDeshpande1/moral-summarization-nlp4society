**SUMMARY:**

A phony attempt to drum up false sexual misconduct allegations against special counsel Robert Mueller has collapsed. The scheme, which may have been a scam by conservative activists Jacob Wohl and Jack Burkman, aimed to smear Mueller and his investigation. However, the plan was embarrassingly thin and exposed Wohl's shady past as a hedge fund operator with dubious claims. The far-right website Gateway Pundit initially posted a heavily redacted document making a rape allegation against Mueller, but later took it down. Mueller's office referred the matter to the FBI for investigation, and online sleuths uncovered that Surefire Intelligence, supposedly investigating these allegations, was likely just Wohl in disguise. If this was indeed a smear attempt, it failed miserably, but if it was meant to troll journalists and change the subject, it may have succeeded.
