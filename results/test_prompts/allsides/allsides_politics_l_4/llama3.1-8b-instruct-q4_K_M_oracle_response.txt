SUMMARY:

Conservative activists Jacob Wohl and Jack Burkman may have been involved in an attempt to drum up phony allegations of sexual misconduct against special counsel Robert Mueller, but the operation appears to have collapsed spectacularly. The plot was allegedly part of a scam to smear Mueller and his investigation, with Wohl and Burkman trying to convince others to make false claims about Mueller's behavior. However, their scheme was quickly uncovered by journalists, who began to investigate Surefire Intelligence, the supposed private intelligence company behind the allegations. It turned out that Surefire was essentially just a front for Wohl, and the whole operation may have been an attempt to troll the media and distract from other issues.

The investigation into this matter has now been referred to the FBI, with Mueller's spokesperson saying they immediately made the referral when they learned about the allegations. The incident highlights the lengths to which some individuals will go to smear and discredit others, but ultimately their efforts may be seen as a failed attempt to make false claims stick.
