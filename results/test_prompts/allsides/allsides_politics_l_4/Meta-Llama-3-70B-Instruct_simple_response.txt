SUMMARY:

A dubious scheme to fabricate sexual misconduct allegations against Special Counsel Robert Mueller has been exposed, and it may lead to trouble for some conservative activists with the FBI. The plot, which appears to involve Jacob Wohl, a 20-year-old conservative, and Jack Burkman, a Republican lobbyist, aimed to discredit Mueller and his investigation. The plan was to offer women money to make false claims against Mueller, but it backfired when journalists and online sleuths uncovered the scam. The whole ordeal has been labeled as an "embarrassingly thin" attempt to smear Mueller. Despite being debunked, the story has managed to steal the spotlight and divert journalistic resources, achieving the trolls' goal of changing the subject just days before the midterm elections. The FBI is now investigating the matter.

END OF SUMMARY.
