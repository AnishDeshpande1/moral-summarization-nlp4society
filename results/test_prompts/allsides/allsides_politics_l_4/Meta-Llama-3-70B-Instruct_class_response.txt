A suspected plot to smear Special Counsel Robert Mueller with false sexual misconduct allegations has been exposed, and the FBI is now investigating. The scheme, which appears to have been orchestrated by conservative activists Jacob Wohl and Jack Burkman, involved offering women money to make false claims against Mueller. Wohl, a 20-year-old conservative, has a history of making implausible and factually dubious claims, while Burkman is a Republican lobbyist and conspiracy theorist known for making false accusations. The pair's attempt to drum up phony allegations was embarrassingly thin and quickly unraveled, with internet sleuths uncovering evidence that Wohl's supposed private intelligence company, Surefire Intelligence, was essentially a one-man operation. The FBI is now investigating the matter, and Wohl may face consequences for his actions.
