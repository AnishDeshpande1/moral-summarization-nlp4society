SUMMARY:
U.S. Democrats are pushing for the impeachment of President Donald Trump, citing his pressuring of Ukraine to investigate political rival Joe Biden as a threat to free and fair elections and national security. The House Judiciary Committee heard testimony on Monday, with top Democrat Jerrold Nadler stating that there is extensive proof of Trump's wrongdoing. Republicans have fired back, disputing the evidence and claiming the inquiry is unfair. The heart of the impeachment battle is whether Trump abused his power by pressuring Ukraine to investigate Biden and then obstructed Congress' investigation. Trump denies any wrongdoing and has called the probe a hoax.

END OF SUMMARY.
