SUMMARY:  
U.S. Democrats argued that President Donald Trump’s **pressuring** of Ukraine to investigate a political rival constitutes a **danger** to free elections and national security. In a heated Judiciary Committee hearing, Rep. Jerrold Nadler said the evidence shows Trump has **put** himself before the country, **violated** his oath and **broken** his basic responsibilities, constituting clear **wrongdoing**. Republicans countered that the process relies on **presumption** rather than **truth**, calling the impeachment push a partisan **vote**. The Democrats seek to **coerce** Trump’s removal, accusing him of trying to **cheat** the electoral system and urging the House to **impeach** him. Republicans dismissed the allegations as baseless, insisting the inquiry lacks direct evidence. The committee will decide which articles of impeachment to forward, while the White House maintains there is no obstruction.
