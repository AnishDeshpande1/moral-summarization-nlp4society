SUMMARY:

President Obama's administration deliberately deceived the public about the Benghazi attack, claiming it was a spontaneous protest when in fact it was a terrorist assault. Emails and diplomatic cables reveal that the White House knew within hours of the attack that Ansar al-Sharia had claimed responsibility, but instead blamed an anti-Muslim video to cover up their failure. The author argues that Obama's actions were driven by cynical politics, as he feared that the truth about his Libyan intervention would come out, which was indeed making Libya a safe haven for radical Islam. The administration's willful blindness to the Islamist reality in Benghazi led to the murder of four American diplomats, and the author accuses Obama of having "American blood on his hands." The article concludes that Obama's actions constitute a grave betrayal of his duties as President, and he is unfit to lead.

END OF SUMMARY.
