SUMMARY: The article is an opinion piece accusing former President Barack Obama of deliberately misleading the public about the September 11, 2012, Benghazi attack. It claims that internal emails and diplomatic cables showed the Obama administration knew the assault was carried out by the terrorist group Ansar al‑Sharia, but publicly framed it as a spontaneous protest triggered by an anti‑Muslim video. The author argues this deception was intended to protect Obama’s political narrative of defeating al‑Qaeda and to hide the perceived failures of his 2011 military intervention in Libya, which the piece says created a haven for jihadists. It alleges that prior security requests were ignored, that U.S. military assets were nearby but not deployed, and that the president’s refusal to act amounted to abandoning American personnel. The article concludes that these actions render Obama unfit for office and calls for his defeat in the upcoming election. END OF SUMMARY.
