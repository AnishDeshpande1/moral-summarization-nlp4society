SUMMARY:
The article accuses President Obama of subverting legitimate authority by deliberately mischaracterizing the Sept. 11, 2012 Benghazi attack as a “spontaneous protest” despite internal State Department and CIA reports that identified it as a terrorist assault by Ansar al‑Sharia. It argues this deception betrayed the American people’s trust and violated fairness by hiding the true cause of a deadly attack that killed Ambassador Stevens and three staff members. The piece stresses the harm inflicted on the victims and their families, portraying the administration’s inaction and cover‑up as a grave failure to protect citizens. It further claims Obama’s loyalty to his political agenda and multilateral intervention in Libya caused the rise of jihadist forces, betraying the nation’s duty to safeguard its people. Consequently, the author calls for Obama’s defeat, asserting that his deceit, abandonment of duty, and betrayal of both authority and loyalty render him unfit for office.  
END OF SUMMARY.
