SUMMARY:
The article accuses President Obama of a deliberate deception surrounding the September 11, 2012 Benghazi attack, portraying it as a “spontaneous protest” while internal State Department emails and CIA reports identified the assault as a terrorist strike by al‑Qaeda‑linked Ansar al‑Sharia. It argues Obama knowingly misled the public to protect his political narrative of having defeated al Qaeda, concealing the reality that his 2011 Libyan intervention created a failed Islamist state where jihadists flourished. The piece claims the administration ignored repeated security warnings, denied requests for protection, and failed to launch a rapid military rescue, sacrificing four American diplomats—including Ambassador J. Christopher Stevens—on “the altar” of multilateral interventionism and electoral ambition. By hiding the truth and refusing a timely response, the author frames Obama’s actions as a grave moral failure, an abuse of power, and a betrayal of American lives, concluding that he is unfit for re‑election. END OF SUMMARY.
