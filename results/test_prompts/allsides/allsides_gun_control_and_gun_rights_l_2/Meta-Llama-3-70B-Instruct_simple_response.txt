SUMMARY:

In a bold move, Democratic lawmakers are set to introduce legislation to impose background checks on all gun sales, a crucial step towards curbing gun violence in America. The push comes after years of inaction from the White House and Congress, despite devastating mass shootings that have left countless families bereaved. The morally repugnant conspiracy theories that have harassed victims' families, such as Lenny Pozner, whose six-year-old son was killed in the Sandy Hook massacre, only add to the urgency of this issue. The Democratic majority in the House is determined to make progress on gun control, and their efforts are backed by prominent Democrats, shooting survivors, and advocacy groups. While the GOP-controlled Senate may pose a hurdle, Democrats are hopeful of winning bipartisan support and setting a strong agenda for the 2020 presidential election. It's time for America to take a moral stand against gun violence and ensure that such tragedies never happen again.

END OF SUMMARY.
