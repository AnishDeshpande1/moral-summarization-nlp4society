SUMMARY:

Democrats are set to introduce legislation to impose background checks on all gun sales, a top priority for the new Congress. Senators Chris Murphy and Richard Blumenthal, who represent Connecticut, will lead the push for stronger gun control, joined by House Democrats and anti-gun violence advocacy groups. The effort comes as the nation mourns the victims of recent mass shootings, including the Sandy Hook elementary school tragedy. Despite past pledges from President Trump, progress on gun control has stalled in Washington. Democrats, now empowered with a majority in the House, aim to introduce sweeping legislation to strengthen background checks, hoping to win Republican support and set the stage for the 2020 presidential election. The moral imperative to act on gun violence is clear, and Democrats are determined to make progress on this bitterly divisive issue.

END OF SUMMARY.
