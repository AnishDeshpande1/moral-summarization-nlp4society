SUMMARY:

A group of 14 Wisconsin parents has filed a lawsuit against the Madison Metropolitan School District (MMSD) for its policy allowing teachers to address their children by their preferred "gender identity" without notifying them first. The parents argue that the policy is unconstitutional, violating their fundamental right to direct the upbringing of their children. By keeping parents in the dark about their children's gender identity, the district is usurping parental authority and undermining their ability to guide their children through life-altering decisions. The policy also interferes with parents' religious freedom, as they believe God intended humans to be male and female. The lawsuit is a pushback against the district's attempt to subvert parental authority and impose its own ideology on students. By standing up for their rights, the parents are fighting to ensure that they, not the state, have the final say in their children's education and upbringing.

END OF SUMMARY.
