**SUMMARY:**  
A coalition of 14 Wisconsin parents, represented by the conservative law firm Wisconsin Institute for Law and Liberty (WILL), has filed a lawsuit against Madison Metropolitan School District (MMSD) alleging that the district’s transgender policy **violates** parents’ **fundamental** **constitutional** **rights**. The policy permits teachers to address students by their self‑identified gender **without** prior **parental** **consent** or notification, effectively **subverting** parental authority over a child’s upbringing and health decisions. Plaintiffs argue the rule breaches the state constitution’s **due‑process** clause and **religious** **freedom** guarantees, asserting that God intended humans to be male and female and that the district’s practice **conceals** children’s gender struggles from parents. WILL’s counsel contends the school’s actions **intervene** in a matter that should be **decided** by parents, not by school officials, and that there is no compelling government interest to **keep** such information **secret**. MMSD has not yet responded to the filing but maintains its guidance for transgender and nonbinary students.
