**SUMMARY:**  
Fourteen Wisconsin parents, represented by the conservative firm Wisconsin Institute for Law and Liberty, have sued the Madison Metropolitan School District, claiming its policy that lets teachers use a child’s self‑identified name and pronouns without parental notice violates parents’ **fundamental**, **constitutional** **rights**. The lawsuit argues the policy **violates** due‑process protections, **religious** **freedom**, and the parents’ **right** to guide their children’s **health**, **future**, and **life‑altering** decisions in line with their **beliefs** that **God** created humans as **male** and **female**. While the district says parental consent is required only for legal name changes, its 2018 policy permits any student to be called by a chosen name and pronoun, aiming to **affirm** each student’s self‑designated gender and **disrupt** the gender **binary**. District officials have not yet responded to the suit.
