SUMMARY:
Toronto trans rights activist Julie Berman was murdered on December 22, 2019, in a heartbreaking and alarming incident. Just two years prior, she had delivered a speech at a trans day of remembrance event, vocal about the transphobia and violence faced by her community. Her friend, Davina Hader, described her as a lovely person committed to the political issues facing her community. Berman's murder is a stark reminder of the high rates of violence the trans community experiences, with a study showing that 20% of Ontario's trans people have been physically or sexually assaulted for being trans. The incident is particularly unfortunate, given that Berman was struggling to survive, like many in the trans community.
