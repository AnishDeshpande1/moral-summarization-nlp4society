A tragic shooting incident occurred at Noblesville West Middle School in Indiana, leaving a student and a teacher critically injured. Authorities took a male student into custody after the incident, which was reported as an active shooter situation. The victims were rushed to the hospital, and their families were notified. The suspect and victims have not been identified. Authorities believe there is no further threat to the school, but a threat was directed towards Noblesville High School, where many middle school students were evacuated. An investigation has been opened, and multiple agencies, including the FBI, are involved. The school's resource officer was present during the incident, and a science teacher reportedly tackled the student, saving many lives.
