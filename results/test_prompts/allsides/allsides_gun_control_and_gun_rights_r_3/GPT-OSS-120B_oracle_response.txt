Andre Carson, Sen. Todd Young, Gov. Eric Holcomb, the Indianapolis Colts, and the Indiana State Teachers Association, issued **thoughts**, **prayers**, and **efforts** of **bravery**, calling the event a **tragedy** and a **difficult** **crisis** that has **landed** in their **hearts**.
