SUMMARY:

A tragic shooting incident occurred at Noblesville West Middle School in Indiana, leaving a student and a teacher critically injured on Friday. A male student was taken into custody after the threat was reported to authorities. The suspect excused himself from the classroom and returned with two handguns, firing shots that were heard by students in the auditorium. Fortunately, a science teacher tackled the suspect, preventing more harm. The teacher, Jason Seaman, was shot multiple times but is currently undergoing surgery. The school's resource officer was present at the time of the incident, but there are no metal detectors on campus. Authorities are investigating the incident and have opened an investigation into the shooting. Politicians, including Vice President Mike Pence and Senator Todd Young, expressed their thoughts and prayers for the victims and the Noblesville community. The incident comes a week after an attack at a high school in Texas that killed eight students and two teachers, highlighting the crisis of violence in schools.

END OF SUMMARY.
