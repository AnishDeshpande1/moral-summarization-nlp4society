SUMMARY:

A male student was taken into custody after a shooting at Noblesville West Middle School in Indiana, leaving a student and a teacher critically injured. The suspect allegedly excused himself from class, returned with two handguns, and opened fire. A science teacher, Jason Seaman, reportedly tackled the student, saving lives. The teacher was shot multiple times and underwent surgery. Authorities praised the swift response of law enforcement and first responders. Politicians, including Vice President Mike Pence, and local officials expressed condolences and support for the victims and the community. The incident has sparked concerns about school safety and gun violence, with the Indiana State Teachers Association calling for action to keep kids safe from violence. The community is in shock, with parents rushing to reunite with their children.
