SUMMARY:

A shooting occurred at Noblesville West Middle School in Indiana on Friday, leaving a student and a teacher critically injured. A male student was taken into custody after the incident, which happened around 9:06 a.m. ET. The suspect excused himself from the classroom and returned with two handguns before being tackled by a science teacher, who is one of the victims. Authorities said there was no further threat to the school but did find a threat directed at Noblesville High School. Multiple agencies, including the FBI, are investigating the incident. The teacher who tackled the suspect has been identified as Jason Seaman and underwent surgery after being shot multiple times. Politicians, including Vice President Mike Pence, have tweeted their thoughts and prayers for the victims and the community. The incident comes a week after a shooting at a high school in Texas that killed eight students and two teachers.

END OF SUMMARY.
