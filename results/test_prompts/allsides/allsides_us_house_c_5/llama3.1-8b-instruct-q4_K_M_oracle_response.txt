SUMMARY:

House Intelligence Committee Chairman Adam Schiff (D-Calif.) is facing calls for his resignation from President Trump and other Republicans, who claim he has damaged the panel's integrity by promoting a demonstrably false narrative about Trump's links to Russia. However, Schiff fiercely defended himself, stating that it is not OK for the president's son to be offered dirt as part of an effort to help Trump. He also accused Trump and his campaign of lying and leaking information during the investigation into Russian interference in the 2016 election.

Schiff has been advancing the committee's investigation into Trump's links to Russia, financial and otherwise, and allegations that the president could be compromised by the Kremlin or another foreign power. The panel is investigating potential meddling by Putin and other foreign powers, which Republicans claim has caused divisions within the committee. Schiff has been accused of misrepresenting information and abusing his power as chair, but Democrats have come to his defense, calling the GOP's attack an ambush and a cheap shot.

END OF SUMMARY.
