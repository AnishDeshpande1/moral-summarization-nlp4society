SUMMARY:

Former National Security Council official Fiona Hill excoriates Republicans for promoting unsubstantiated conspiracy theories about Ukrainian interference in the 2016 presidential election, which she calls a "fictional narrative" perpetuated by Russian security services. Hill emphasizes that the US intelligence community's consensus conclusion is that Russia interfered on Trump's behalf, and that any attempt to legitimize an alternative narrative undermines national security. She warns against promoting "politically driven falsehoods" that advance Russian interests, stating that impeding or subverting national security for domestic political or personal interests is a serious concern. Hill also highlights the ongoing impact of Russian election interference, including the undermining of US support for Ukraine and the erosion of trust in institutions. Her statement underscores the moral imperative of prioritizing truth over partisan interests and protecting national security from those who would exploit it for personal gain.

END OF SUMMARY.
