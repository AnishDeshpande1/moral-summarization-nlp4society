SUMMARY:

Fiona Hill, a former National Security Council official, testified before impeachment investigators, condemning Republicans for perpetuating unsubstantiated conspiracy theories about Ukrainian interference in the 2016 presidential election. Hill emphasized that these theories are a "fictional narrative" propagated by Russian security services, and that the US intelligence community has consistently concluded that Russia interfered in the election on Trump's behalf. She warned against promoting "politically driven falsehoods" that advance Russian interests and undermine US national security. Hill's testimony highlighted the absurdity of Trump's claims and the ongoing threat of Russian election interference, which has torn the nation apart and undermined trust in institutions. Her statement underscored the importance of upholding the truth and defending the integrity of the electoral process, rather than indulging in partisan fantasies that subvert authority and fairness.

END OF SUMMARY.
