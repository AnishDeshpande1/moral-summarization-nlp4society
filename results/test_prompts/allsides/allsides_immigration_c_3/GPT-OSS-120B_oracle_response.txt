SUMMARY:
The House passed a $4.5 billion emergency aid package to **prevent** a humanitarian disaster at the U.S.–Mexico border. After Speaker Nancy Pelosi **quelled** a **mini‑revolt** by progressive and Hispanic lawmakers, the bill was approved along party lines, **ensuring** that migrant families and unaccompanied children **have** food, clothing, sanitary items, **shelter**, and medical **care**. It **provides** legal **assistance**, **protecting** families so “families belong together.” More than $1 billion will **feed** and **shelter** detained migrants, and almost $3 billion will **mandate** higher **standards** for HHS “influx shelters.” The legislation **ensures** funds cannot be **used** for a border wall, immigration **raids**, or additional ICE detention beds, **blocking** Trump’s **policies** that **tear** families apart and **terrorize** communities. Republicans warn a **veto** would be a **failure** that **ignores** the plight of children **housed** in overcrowded stations, where some were **kept** in dire conditions and **died**. Lawmakers stress the need to **establish** better **assistance**, replace inadequate contractors, and **provide** proper **treatment** amid agencies being **overwhelmed** and **depleting** resources. The Senate now faces a showdown to pass a companion bill before the July 4 recess.  

END OF SUMMARY.
