SUMMARY: President Trump called the Las Vegas attack an "act of pure evil" and offered his prayers through this "very dark period". He announced he will visit Las Vegas to meet with law enforcement, first responders, and families of victims. The president ordered the flag be flown at half-staff to honor those who died. He emphasized that our unity cannot be shattered by evil and violence, and that we can take solace in knowing that even the darkest space can be illuminated by a single ray of hope. Trump prayed for the entire nation and expressed his condolences to the victims and families. The suspect was identified as 64-year-old Stephen Paddock, who killed himself, and authorities are still searching for motive. The attack left 58 people dead and 515 hospitalized. Officials described it as well-planned and a type of attack the U.S. has been fearing would come to our shores.

END OF SUMMARY.
