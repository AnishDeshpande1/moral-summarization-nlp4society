**SUMMARY:**

The development of an effective vaccine is crucial to ending the coronavirus pandemic, with over 100 potential vaccines in various stages of development. However, experts express skepticism about the idea that a game-changing vaccine will be available by the end of the year or even next summer, which would shatter the record for the fastest vaccine ever developed. Despite promising progress, there are concerns about investing too much in what may be a best-case scenario and waiting until enough people have been infected. Some argue that preparing for years-long development of vaccines is a better plan, developing effective systems of testing, tracing, and therapeutic medicines to last as long as they're needed. Others worry that rushing development may backfire by increasing the risk of an unsafe vaccine making it to market, which could undermine herd immunity.
