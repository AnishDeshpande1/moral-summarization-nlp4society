SUMMARY:
A white Fort Worth police officer **killed** 28‑year‑old Atatiana Jefferson inside her home after responding to a neighbor’s call about an **open** front door. The officer **opened** fire without **announcing** his presence, and the **shooting** was captured on body‑cam. Attorney Lee Merritt called the act “**murder**,” saying the officer “didn’t have time to perceive a threat.” Jefferson’s sister said the police are supposed to **protect**, yet the incident shows they failed to **protect** her. The family expressed **concern** over the **loss**, describing themselves as **shaken**, **upset**, and demanding a thorough **investigation** and **transparency**. Police Lt. Brandon O’Neil placed the officer on administrative leave and noted the officer **fired** a single **shot** without warning. Community leaders, including Sen. Bernie Sanders, demanded an end to the **killings** of unarmed Black Americans and called for justice. The department released limited footage, citing state law, and promised a more detailed news conference. Merritt, who also represented the family of Amber Guyger’s victim, said the family expects a swift and comprehensive **investigation**.  

END OF SUMMARY.
