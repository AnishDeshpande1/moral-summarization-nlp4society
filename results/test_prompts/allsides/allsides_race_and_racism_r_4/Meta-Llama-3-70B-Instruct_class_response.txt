SUMMARY:

A white police officer in Fort Worth, Texas, "didn't have time to perceive a threat" before killing a 28-year-old black woman, Atatiana Jefferson, inside her home while responding to a neighbor's call about an open front door. The officer's bodycam video shows him firing a shot through a window without announcing himself as police, which is part of the department's investigation. Jefferson's family calls it "murder," and her sister says, "It's another one of those situations where the people that are supposed to protect us are actually not here to protect us." The family is seeking "justice" and an "investigation" into the fatal shooting. The officer is on administrative leave pending the outcome of the investigation. The incident has sparked outrage, with Democratic presidential candidate Sen. Bernie Sanders calling for a Justice Department investigation, saying "The killings of unarmed Black Americans have got to end." END OF SUMMARY.
