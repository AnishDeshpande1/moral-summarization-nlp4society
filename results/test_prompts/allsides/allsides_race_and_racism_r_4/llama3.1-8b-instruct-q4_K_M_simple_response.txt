SUMMARY:

A white police officer in Fort Worth, Texas shot and killed Atatiana Jefferson, a 28-year-old black woman, while responding to a call about an open front door. According to the family's attorney, Lee Merritt, the officer did not have time to perceive a threat before opening fire through a window. The incident has sparked outrage and grief among Jefferson's family and community. "It's another one of those situations where the people that are supposed to protect us are actually not here to protect us," said Jefferson's sister, Amber Carr.

The police department has released bodycam footage showing two officers searching the home from outside before one shouts "Put your hands up" and fires a shot through the window. The officer who fired the fatal shot is on administrative leave pending an investigation. Democratic presidential candidate Bernie Sanders has called for the Justice Department to investigate the shooting, which is part of a larger pattern of police violence against unarmed black Americans.

END OF SUMMARY.
