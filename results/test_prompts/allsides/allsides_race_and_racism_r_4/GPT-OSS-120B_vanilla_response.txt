SUMMARY: A white Fort Worth police officer shot and killed 28‑year‑old Black woman Atatiana Jefferson inside her home after a neighbor reported an open front door. Body‑cam footage shows officers outside the house, one shouting “Put your hands up,” then firing through a window without announcing police presence. Jefferson was watching her 8‑year‑old nephew at the time. The officer, on duty since 2018, is on administrative leave while an investigation proceeds; his name has not been released. Jefferson’s family, represented by attorney Lee Merritt, says the officer had no time to perceive a threat and calls the killing murder. Police say they saw someone near a window and later found a gun in the home, but it is unclear if Jefferson possessed it. The incident has sparked calls for a swift, thorough investigation, including a demand from Sen. Bernie Sanders for a Justice Department probe. The shooting follows the recent conviction of former Dallas officer Amber Guyger for the murder of her Black neighbor. END OF SUMMARY.
