SUMMARY:

A white police officer in Fort Worth, Texas, shot and killed a 28-year-old black woman, Atatiana Jefferson, inside her home while responding to a neighbor's call about an open front door. The officer, who has been on the force since 2018, is on administrative leave pending an investigation. The bodycam footage shows the officer firing a shot through a window without announcing himself as police. The family's attorney, Lee Merritt, said the officer "didn't have time to perceive a threat" and that it's "murder." Jefferson was watching her 8-year-old nephew at the time of the shooting. The police department has released a statement saying that officers saw someone near a window and perceived a threat, but the family is seeking answers and justice. The incident has sparked outrage, with Democratic presidential candidate Sen. Bernie Sanders calling for a Justice Department investigation.

END OF SUMMARY.
