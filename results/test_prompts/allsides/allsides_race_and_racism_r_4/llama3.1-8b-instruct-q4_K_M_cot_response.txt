**SUMMARY:**
A white police officer shot and killed Atatiana Jefferson, a 28-year-old black woman, inside her Texas home while responding to a neighbor's call about an open front door. The officer did not announce he was police before firing the fatal shot, which is part of the department's investigation. The shooting has been deemed "murder" by attorney Lee Merritt, who represents Jefferson's family. They expect a thorough and expedient investigation into her death. The Fort Worth Police Department released bodycam footage showing two officers searching the home from the outside before one shouts "Put your hands up, show me your hands" and fires through a window. The killing is the latest in a series of incidents where police have used excessive force against unarmed black Americans. Democratic presidential candidate Bernie Sanders called on the Justice Department to investigate, saying "The killings of unarmed Black Americans have got to end."
