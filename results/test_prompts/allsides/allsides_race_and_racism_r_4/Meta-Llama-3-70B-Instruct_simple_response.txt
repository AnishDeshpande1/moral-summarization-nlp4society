SUMMARY:

A white police officer in Fort Worth, Texas, shot and killed a 28-year-old black woman, Atatiana Jefferson, inside her home while responding to a neighbor's call about an open front door. The officer did not announce himself as police before firing the fatal shot, and the family's attorney, Lee Merritt, called it "murder" because the officer didn't have time to perceive a threat. The video shows the officer shouting "Put your hands up, show me your hands" before firing through a window. Jefferson was watching her 8-year-old nephew at the time. The officer is on administrative leave pending an investigation. The family is demanding justice, but acknowledges that it won't bring Jefferson back. The incident has sparked outrage, with Democratic presidential candidate Sen. Bernie Sanders calling for a Justice Department investigation, saying "The killings of unarmed Black Americans have got to end."

END OF SUMMARY.
