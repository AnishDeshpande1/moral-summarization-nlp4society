SUMMARY:
A white police officer in Fort Worth, Texas shot and killed Atatiana Jefferson, a 28-year-old black woman, inside her home while responding to a neighbor's call about an open front door. Attorney Lee Merritt said the officer "didn't have time to perceive a threat" before opening fire, calling it "murder." The police department stated that officers saw someone near a window and one of them fired after perceiving a threat. However, the bodycam video shows no warning was given before the shot was fired.

Jefferson's family is seeking justice, with her sister saying they want to see change but it won't bring back Jefferson. The officer involved has been placed on administrative leave pending an investigation. The police department confirmed that the officer did not announce himself as police before firing and that this is part of their investigation. Democratic presidential candidate Bernie Sanders called for a Justice Department investigation into the killing.

END OF SUMMARY.
