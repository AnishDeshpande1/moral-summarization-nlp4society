SUMMARY: A white police officer's shooting of Atatiana Jefferson, a 28-year-old black woman, has sparked outrage in Fort Worth, Texas. The officer, who was responding to a neighbor's call about an open front door, fired through a window without announcing himself or perceiving a threat. Jefferson was watching her 8-year-old nephew when she was killed. Her family and community are demanding justice for this unspeakable loss. The police department has released bodycam footage showing the shooting, but critics argue that it lacks transparency. Senator Bernie Sanders has called on the Justice Department to investigate the killing of another unarmed black American. The incident is part of a larger pattern of police violence against black communities, which must come to an end. As one neighbor who called the police expressed, "If I had never dialed the police department, she'd still be alive." The community is seeking answers and accountability for this murder.

END OF SUMMARY.
