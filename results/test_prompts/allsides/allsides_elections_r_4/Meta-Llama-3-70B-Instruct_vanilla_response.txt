SUMMARY:

At the final presidential debate, GOP nominee Donald Trump refused to commit to accepting the election results, saying he would "keep you in suspense." This is not surprising, given his consistent disregard for democratic norms and legitimacy throughout his campaign. Trump has encouraged violence against protesters, claimed the election is rigged, and promised to violate constitutional rules. He has also praised authoritarian leaders like Vladimir Putin and expressed admiration for their strength. Trump's lack of respect for the Constitution and his desire to implement authoritarian policies are evident in his statements and actions. His vast personal vanity and belief that he is an eternal winner also contribute to his authoritarian outlook. It appears that Trump is not running to be the president of a democratic republic, but rather to be an unchecked and unaccountable leader.

END OF SUMMARY.
