SUMMARY:

President Trump became the first president to speak in person at the anti-abortion March for Life rally, where he slammed Democrats for embracing "radical and extreme positions" on abortion. Trump has been a consistent champion for life, appointing anti-abortion judges and federal workers, cutting taxpayer funding for abortions, and calling for an end to late-term abortions. A new Gallup poll shows that discontent over US abortion policy is at an all-time high (58%), with many wanting less restrictive laws. The poll also found that mounting support for easing abortion policy is fueled by a rise in Democrats and independents expressing such sentiments. Meanwhile, a separate poll by the Kaiser Family Foundation found that 59% of Americans believe abortion should be legal in most or all cases, while nearly seven in 10 respondents don't want the landmark Supreme Court ruling Roe v. Wade to be overturned.

END OF SUMMARY.
