SUMMARY: President Trump spoke at the anti-abortion March for Life rally, calling unborn children "wonderfully made" and stating that Democrats have radical positions on abortion. He criticized them for supporting taxpayer-funded abortions up to birth, a claim critics dispute. The event's mission is to promote the dignity of every human life by working to end abortion. Trump's administration has been consistent in its support for life, appointing anti-abortion judges and cutting funding for abortions. A new Gallup poll shows record-high dissatisfaction with US abortion laws (58%), with 32% satisfied. Some Americans want stricter laws, while others favor less restrictive ones. The Supreme Court will hear cases on abortion restrictions this year. Many Americans believe abortion should be legal in most or all cases, and don't want the landmark Roe v. Wade ruling overturned. Trump's stance has contributed to growing discontent with abortion policy, but his administration remains a champion for life. END OF SUMMARY.
