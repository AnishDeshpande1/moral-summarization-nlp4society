SUMMARY:

President-elect Donald Trump has a unique opportunity to restore transparency and accountability in government by revealing the secret memos and documents underlying the Obama administration's controversial policies. By doing so, Trump can demonstrate his commitment to the values of democracy and expose the Obama administration's abuses of power. The Obama administration's excessive secrecy and stonewalling of Freedom of Information Act requests have led to widespread distrust of the government. Trump can change this narrative by releasing documents related to the drone assassination program, the Syrian Civil War, and the bombing of Libya, among others. This would not only vindicate Trump's warnings about the dangers of intervention but also provide a much-needed check on the executive branch's power. By promoting transparency and accountability, Trump can help restore trust in government and prevent similar abuses of power in the future.

END OF SUMMARY.
