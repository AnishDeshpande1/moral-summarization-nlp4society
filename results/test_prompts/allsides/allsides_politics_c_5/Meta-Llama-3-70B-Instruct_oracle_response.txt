SUMMARY:

President-elect Donald Trump should prioritize transparency by exposing the Obama administration's secretive dealings. By doing so, he can dispel doubts about his legitimacy and showcase his commitment to civil liberties. Trump should follow Obama's precedent of releasing secret memos, starting with those underlying the "targeted killing" drone assassination program. This would vindicate Trump's warnings about the Syrian Civil War, where the US has armed and bankrolled Al Qaeda-linked groups despite federal law prohibiting material support to terrorist groups. The paper trail preceding the US bombing of Libya in 2011 should also be revealed, exposing the bureaucratic fiasco that has led to chaos. Additionally, Trump should compel disclosures on the Freedom of Information Act, which has become a mirage under Obama's administration. By ending excessive secrecy, Trump can boost democracy and restore trust in the federal government. END OF SUMMARY.
