SUMMARY:

The article suggests that President-elect Donald Trump should release secret memos and documents from the Obama administration to prove his legitimacy and expose the previous administration's actions. This would include revealing the legal memos behind the "targeted killing" drone program, the paper trail on US intervention in the Syrian Civil War, and the records preceding and following the US bombing of Libya in 2011. The article argues that the Obama administration was secretive and denied many Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests, and that Trump should compel disclosures to promote transparency and prevent similar follies in the future. By opening the books, Trump can create good will and help restore trust in the federal government, which has been eroded by excessive secrecy.

END OF SUMMARY.
