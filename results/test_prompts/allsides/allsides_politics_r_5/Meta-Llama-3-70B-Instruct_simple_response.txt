SUMMARY:

In a significant development in the Russian interference investigation, former Trump campaign adviser George Papadopoulos has been revealed to be a "proactive cooperator" with federal investigators. This term suggests that Papadopoulos may have worn a wire to help prosecutors gather evidence, potentially recording conversations with Trump associates. Papadopoulos pleaded guilty to lying to the FBI about his contacts with Russians and has been cooperating with the government since his arrest in July. His cooperation has been kept secret until now, and it is unclear what information he may have provided. However, experts believe that his recordings could be a "very, very big deal" in the investigation. The White House has downplayed Papadopoulos' role in the campaign, but this development raises new questions about the extent of Russian interference and potential collusion. As the investigation continues to unfold, Papadopoulos' cooperation could prove to be a crucial piece of evidence.

END OF SUMMARY.
