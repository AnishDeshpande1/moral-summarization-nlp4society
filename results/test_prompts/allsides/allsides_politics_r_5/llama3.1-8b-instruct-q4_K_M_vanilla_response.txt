SUMMARY:

Former Trump campaign adviser George Papadopoulos has been referred to as a "proactive cooperator" in court documents, sparking speculation that he may have worn a wire to help prosecutors in the Office of Special Counsel Robert Mueller's investigation into Russian interference in the presidential campaign. Papadopoulos accepted a plea deal for lying to the FBI about his contacts with Russians and is expected to cooperate fully with investigators. Court filings suggest that Papadopoulos' cooperation includes wearing a wire or recording conversations with Trump associates, which could be used as evidence in the investigation. This revelation has raised questions about the extent of Papadopoulos' involvement in the investigation and its potential implications for the Trump campaign.

END OF SUMMARY.
