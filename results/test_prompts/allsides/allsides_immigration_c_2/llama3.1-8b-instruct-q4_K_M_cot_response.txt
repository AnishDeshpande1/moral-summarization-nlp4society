**SUMMARY:**

In his Christmas Day address, Pope Francis called for kindness and hope to be shown to migrants around the world who are forced to emigrate due to injustices. He prayed that God would comfort those suffering from war and conflicts in various countries, including the Middle East, Ukraine, Africa, and Latin America. The pontiff urged an end to these ongoing humanitarian crises, which he described as causing immense unspeakable sufferings. Francis also called on governments and leaders to find solutions to allow people to live together in peace and security. He encouraged people to recognize the suffering around them and act with good will to help those in need. The pope concluded his message by praying that God would soften hardened hearts and bring tenderness to all, brightening the darkness of the world.
