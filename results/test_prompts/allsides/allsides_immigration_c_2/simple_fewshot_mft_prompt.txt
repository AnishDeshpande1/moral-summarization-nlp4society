On Wednesday, Trump appeared to shift stance somewhat by avoiding saying it would be ended by executive order, while acknowledging the matter “will be settled by the United States Supreme Court.”

"This is just more of the same of what he's been saying," she said, referring to Trump's remarks on immigration. 
"What he was saying today has no relationship to what his authority is, but that's of course not unusual."
