SUMMARY:

In his Christmas Day address, Pope Francis highlighted the suffering of migrants and refugees, emphasizing the injustices that force them to flee their homes in search of a secure life. He lamented that these injustices turn deserts and seas into cemeteries and walls of indifference into barriers to a dignified life. The Pope prayed for comfort for those suffering and called on people to show goodwill and find solutions to ongoing humanitarian crises. He specifically urged an end to conflicts in the Middle East, Latin America, Ukraine, and Africa, and prayed for those persecuted on the basis of religion. The Pope concluded by calling on people to recognize the suffering around them and act to help those in need, softening their hearts and making them channels of love.

END OF SUMMARY.
