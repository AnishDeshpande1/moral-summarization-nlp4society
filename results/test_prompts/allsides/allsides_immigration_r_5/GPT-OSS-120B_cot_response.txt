**SUMMARY:**  
Former President Obama condemned President Trump’s decision to dismantle DACA, calling the move “cruel” and a betrayal of “basic decency,” “spirit,” and “common sense.” Obama argued that DACA recipients pose no “threat,” have done nothing “wrong,” and that targeting them is “self‑defeating.” He framed the rollback as a “moral” issue, warning that it would “threaten” the future of young people who contribute as scientists, soldiers, and community helpers. Obama emphasized that removing Dreamers would not improve unemployment, taxes, or wages, and urged compassion for those who arrived through no fault of their own. **END OF SUMMARY.**
