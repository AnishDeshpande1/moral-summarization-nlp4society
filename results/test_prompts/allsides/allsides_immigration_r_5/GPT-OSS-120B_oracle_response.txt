SUMMARY: Former President Obama blasted President Trump for ending DACA, calling the move “cruel,” “lacking basic decency,” and “self‑defeating.” Obama argued that targeting Dreamers is a moral decision, not a legal necessity, and that it threatens young people who have done nothing wrong. He reminded readers that DACA recipients serve their communities, help in labs, start businesses, and even wear the uniform of a country that gave them a chance to love and contribute. Obama asked, “What if our kid’s science teacher or friendly neighbor turns out to be a Dreamer? Where are we supposed to send her?” He emphasized that kicking them out will not lower unemployment, raise wages, or ease taxes, but only punish innocent individuals who pose no threat. The former president framed the rollback as a failure of compassion and common sense, urging Americans to consider the human cost of this political action. END OF SUMMARY.
