SUMMARY:

Rep. Justin Amash has launched an exploratory committee to seek the Libertarian Party's presidential nomination, a move that could bring a fresh perspective to the White House. Amash's decision is driven by his commitment to "practical approaches based in humility and trust of the people" and a desire to "restore respect for our Constitution and bring people together." The former Republican congressman has been a vocal critic of President Trump, accusing the GOP of "excusing his misbehavior" and turning a blind eye to "assault" on democratic values. Amash's libertarian values, which emphasize less government intervention and sharp cuts in federal spending, have earned him a national profile and made him an attractive third-party option for those disillusioned with the current political landscape. As he takes the first steps towards serving Americans of every background as president, Amash's candidacy could be a game-changer in the 2020 election.

END OF SUMMARY.
