SUMMARY:
Rep. Justin Amash announced the formation of an exploratory committee to seek the Libertarian Party’s presidential nomination, signaling a direct challenge to the entrenched two‑party authority. By positioning himself as a principled alternative, Amash frames his bid as a fight for fairness—restoring respect for the Constitution and offering voters a genuine choice beyond the perceived cheating of both major parties. He criticizes Republican leadership for abandoning fiscal conservatism and for tolerating President Trump’s “misbehavior,” portraying the GOP’s current rule‑making as a subversive betrayal of public trust. Libertarians and “Never‑Trump” Republicans view Amash as a potential game‑changer who could disrupt the established power structure and deliver a more equitable political landscape. While his candidacy may split the anti‑Trump vote, supporters argue that his independent run embodies a fairer, more accountable system that holds the dominant parties to higher standards.  

END OF SUMMARY.
