SUMMARY: A man was shot and killed after police and Kentucky National Guard opened fire on a crowd in a parking lot near Dino’s Food Mart in Louisville at about 12:15 a.m. The confrontation followed destructive protests over the police killings of Breonna Taylor and George Floyd. According to Metro Police Chief Steve Conrad, someone in the crowd fired at officers and soldiers, prompting a return of fire that killed the unidentified man. Governor Andy Beshear ordered an independent Kentucky State Police investigation. Authorities are interviewing witnesses and reviewing video, which will be released publicly. The incident, occurring about two miles from the main downtown protests demanding justice for Taylor—a 26‑year‑old Black EMT killed on March 13 when police shot her boyfriend after breaching her apartment—could heighten tensions in Louisville. Conrad emphasized the need to rebuild trust between police and the community. END OF SUMMARY.
