SUMMARY:

A fatal shooting occurred in Louisville when police and the National Guard opened fire on a crowd in a parking lot, killing one man. The incident followed a night of destructive protests over the police shootings of Breonna Taylor and George Floyd. According to authorities, someone in the crowd fired at officers and soldiers, prompting them to return fire. The victim has not been identified. The police chief acknowledged that many people do not trust the police, which is an issue that needs to be addressed. The incident is likely to further inflame tensions in Louisville, where protesters have been demanding justice for Taylor, an unarmed African American EMT who was killed by police in her apartment on March 13. The police chief pledged to release video footage of the shooting to the public soon.

END OF SUMMARY.
