SUMMARY:

One man was killed in Louisville when police and National Guard opened fire on a crowd gathered in a parking lot. The incident occurred after a night of protests over the deaths of Breonna Taylor and George Floyd. According to authorities, someone in the crowd fired at officers and soldiers, prompting them to return fire. The man killed has not been identified. An investigation into the fatal shooting has been authorized by Gov. Andy Beshear. Police are reviewing video footage and interviewing witnesses. The incident is likely to escalate tensions in Louisville, where protesters have been demanding justice for Taylor, an unarmed African American EMT who was killed by police in her apartment on March 13.

END OF SUMMARY.
