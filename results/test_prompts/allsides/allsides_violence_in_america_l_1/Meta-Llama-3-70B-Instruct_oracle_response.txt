SUMMARY:

A fatal shooting occurred in Louisville when police and the National Guard opened fire on a crowd gathered in a parking lot, killing one man. The incident followed a night of destructive protests over the fatal police shootings of Breonna Taylor and George Floyd. According to authorities, someone in the crowd fired at officers and soldiers, prompting them to return fire. The killing is likely to further inflame tensions in Louisville, where protesters have been demanding justice for Taylor, an unarmed African American EMT who died when police broke down her apartment door and returned fire. Metro Police Chief Steve Conrad acknowledged that many people do not trust the police, which is an issue that needs to be addressed. The incident is under investigation, with police collecting video footage and interviewing witnesses. The fatal shooting has sparked concerns about keeping people safe and protecting property, while also highlighting the need for justice and accountability.

END OF SUMMARY.
