SUMMARY:

A fatal shooting occurred in Louisville, Kentucky, when police and the National Guard opened fire on a crowd gathered in a parking lot. Authorities said someone in the crowd fired at officers, prompting both law enforcement agencies to return fire, resulting in one man's death. The incident is likely to further inflame tensions in Louisville, where protesters have been demanding justice for Breonna Taylor, an unarmed 26-year-old African American EMT who was killed by police on March 13.

Metro Police Chief Steve Conrad said that officers are working hard to keep people safe and protect property, but acknowledged that many people do not trust the police. He pledged to release video footage of the shooting soon. The Kentucky State Police have been authorized to independently investigate the fatal shooting.

Protests over Taylor's death and George Floyd's killing in Minnesota have been ongoing in Louisville, with demands for justice and accountability from law enforcement.

END OF SUMMARY.
