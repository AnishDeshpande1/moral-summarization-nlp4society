SUMMARY:  
One man was **shot** and **killed** after police and the Kentucky National Guard opened fire in a parking‑lot **confrontation** in Louisville. Authorities said a crowd gathered at Dino’s Food Mart around 12:15 a.m. after night‑long **protests** over the fatal police **shooting** of Breonna Taylor and the death of George Floyd. As officers tried to **keep** the area safe, someone in the crowd **fired** at them; the National Guard and Louisville police **returned fire**, resulting in the **death** of the unnamed man. Metro Police Chief Steve Conrad called the incident an **assault** on law‑enforcement and warned that it could **inflame** tensions while the city continues to demand **justice** for Taylor, whose home was **broke** into and who **died** when police **fired** back. Gov. Andy Beshear ordered an independent Kentucky State Police investigation, and officials said video evidence will be released soon.  

END OF SUMMARY.
