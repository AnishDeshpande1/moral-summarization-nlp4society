The US Supreme Court has agreed to decide whether a California law requiring private facilities that counsel pregnant women against abortion to post signs telling clients how to get state-funded abortions and contraceptives violates free speech rights. The law, backed by Democrats, aims to ensure that women are fully informed about the nature of different facilities and have access to information they need when making personal healthcare decisions.
