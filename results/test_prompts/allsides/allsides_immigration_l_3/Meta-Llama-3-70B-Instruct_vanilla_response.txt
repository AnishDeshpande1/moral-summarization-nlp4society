SUMMARY:

US Homeland Security Secretary Kirstjen Nielsen stated that American soldiers have no intention of shooting migrants trying to cross the US-Mexico border, but they will be apprehended. The Trump administration is planning to dispatch at least 800 active-duty troops to the southern border to bolster national guard forces already there. The move comes as a caravan of thousands of migrants, mostly Hondurans fleeing poverty and violence, makes its way through Mexico. President Trump has been using the caravan to stoke fears about immigration and make it a top issue heading into the midterm elections. He has claimed, without evidence, that "Middle Easterners" are among the group and has tweeted that Democrats are pro-illegal immigration. The migrants, who are largely disconnected from news reports about them, plan to continue heading north, seeking a better future for their children.

END OF SUMMARY.
