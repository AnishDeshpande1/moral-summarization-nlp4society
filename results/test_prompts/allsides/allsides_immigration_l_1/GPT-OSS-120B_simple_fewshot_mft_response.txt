SUMMARY:
The article argues that immigrants are the foundation of America’s greatness, countering anti‑immigrant rhetoric that seeks to subvert the nation’s core values. By highlighting the economic contributions of immigrant‑founded companies and the billions of dollars they generate, it frames fairness as a matter of recognizing and rewarding the honest labor of newcomers rather than accusing them of cheating the system. The piece also stresses the care owed to all residents, noting that undocumented immigrants actually contribute more to Medicare than they receive, while native‑born citizens are far more likely to commit crimes, thereby challenging claims that immigrants harm public safety. Loyalty is invoked to call for a respectful, inclusive attitude toward those who seek freedom and opportunity, urging lawmakers to uphold the nation’s historic promise rather than betray it with impractical mass deportations or wasteful wall projects. The author concludes that a fair, compassionate immigration process—free from bureaucratic subversion—will preserve America’s strength and honor its founding ideals.

END OF SUMMARY.
