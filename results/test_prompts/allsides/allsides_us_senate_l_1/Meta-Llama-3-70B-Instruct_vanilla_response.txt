SUMMARY:

Senator Ron Johnson is leading investigations into corruption allegations involving Hunter Biden, son of presumptive Democratic presidential nominee Joe Biden, and the Obama administration's handling of the 2016 election. Johnson claims he is a "nonpartisan guy" seeking the truth, but Democrats accuse him of abusing his power to boost Trump's political prospects and operating a Russian disinformation front. Republicans are privately worried that the efforts could backfire, especially as the country faces the coronavirus pandemic, high unemployment, and unrest over police killings. Johnson plans to release reports on his investigations in the summer and fall, which could thrust the issues back into the spotlight as Election Day nears. Democrats argue that Johnson's investigations are politically motivated and a misuse of his position.

END OF SUMMARY.
