SUMMARY:

Senator Ron Johnson is investigating corruption allegations involving Hunter Biden and the Ukrainian gas company Burisma, as well as the 2016 Russia investigation. Democrats accuse Johnson of abusing his power to boost Trump's political prospects and operating a Russian disinformation front that jeopardizes U.S. election security. Johnson claims he is a nonpartisan guy who is concerned about the democracy and the presidential transition process. He intends to release reports on his investigations in the summer and fall, which Democrats believe will be used to influence the election. Johnson's actions have been criticized by Democrats, who see them as a misuse of his position and an attempt to distract from the country's current crises, including the coronavirus pandemic and police brutality. Johnson, on the other hand, believes Democrats are afraid of the truth and are trying to cover up corruption.

END OF SUMMARY.
