SUMMARY:

Senator Ron Johnson is investigating corruption allegations involving Hunter Biden and the Ukrainian gas company Burisma, as well as claims of Russian disinformation. Democrats accuse Johnson of abusing his power to boost Trump's political prospects, but Johnson insists he is nonpartisan and concerned about the integrity of democracy. Republicans are worried that the investigations may backfire, especially during the coronavirus pandemic and unrest over police killings of unarmed African Americans. Johnson's probe has uncovered information that he claims shows the Trump administration was "sabotaged" by the Obama team, and he plans to release reports in the summer and fall. Democrats argue that Johnson's investigations are politically motivated and a misuse of his position. Johnson, however, believes that Democrats are "afraid of the truth" and that his investigations are necessary to ensure a peaceful transition of power.

END OF SUMMARY.
