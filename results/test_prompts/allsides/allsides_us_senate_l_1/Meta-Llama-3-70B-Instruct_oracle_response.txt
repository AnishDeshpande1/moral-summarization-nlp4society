SUMMARY:

Senator Ron Johnson is investigating corruption allegations involving Hunter Biden, son of the presumptive Democratic presidential nominee, and claims that the former vice president sought to shield his son from a Ukrainian-led investigation. Democrats accuse Johnson of abusing his power, misusing Senate resources to boost Trump's political prospects, and operating a Russian disinformation front that jeopardizes U.S. election security. Johnson insists he's a "nonpartisan guy" concerned about the democracy and the presidential transition process. However, Democrats contend that Johnson's investigation is a misuse of his position, aimed at relitigating the Russia investigation and helping Trump's re-election bid. As the country faces the coronavirus pandemic, high unemployment, and unrest over police killings of unarmed African Americans, Johnson's priorities are being questioned. Johnson's investigation has uncovered information that shows the incoming Trump administration was "sabotaged" by the outgoing Obama team, and he plans to release reports in the summer and fall, which could impact the election.

END OF SUMMARY.
