He prioritizes appearances over realities, using emotional manipulation to garner sympathy from the media and public. This approach has led to catastrophic consequences in various regions, including Egypt, Iran, Ukraine, Nigeria, Syria, and now Iraq. The number of dead in Syria approaches 200,000, while Obama's promises to take action have been repeatedly broken. Critics argue that Obama's lack of commitment to taking lasting action is due to his unwillingness to lead rather than follow, and his failure to genuinely care about the suffering of innocents.
