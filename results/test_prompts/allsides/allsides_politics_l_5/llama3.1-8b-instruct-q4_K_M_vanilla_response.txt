SUMMARY:

Alabama Senate candidate Roy Moore is planning to sue the Washington Post over its report alleging he pursued relationships with teenagers, including a 14-year-old girl, when he was in his 30s. Moore denies the allegations, calling them "completely false" and "fake news." The Washington Post's report was based on interviews with over 30 people, including three other women who claimed Moore pursued them when they were between 16 and 18 years old.

Moore has been under fire since the article was published, with several Republicans withdrawing their endorsements. The National Republican Senatorial Committee severed a joint fundraising agreement that benefits Moore's campaign. Moore claims there are ongoing "investigations" into the motivations behind the Post's article and promises "revelations" about the story in the coming days.

The Washington Post declined to comment on Moore's plans to sue, but has stood by its reporting. The allegations against Moore come as he campaigns for a Senate seat in Alabama, where the age of consent is 16.

END OF SUMMARY.
