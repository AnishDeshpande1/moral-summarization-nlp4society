SUMMARY: Alabama Republican Senate candidate Roy Moore has threatened to sue the Washington Post over its report alleging he pursued sexual relationships with teenagers, including a 14-year-old, when he was in his 30s. Moore claims the attacks on his character and reputation are "false and untrue" and part of a desperate attempt to stop his political campaign. The Washington Post report, based on interviews with over 30 people, details allegations of Moore's sexual misconduct, including touching and undressing a 14-year-old girl, which could have been sexual offenses due to her age. Three other women also alleged that Moore pursued them when they were between 16 and 18 years old. Moore has denied the allegations, calling them "completely false" and "fake news." He claims to have the "highest regard for the protection of young children" and has never been guilty of sexual misconduct. Despite his denials, several Republicans have withdrawn their endorsements of Moore following the report. END OF SUMMARY.
