SUMMARY:

Christian leaders are responding to the Supreme Court's decision to legalize same-sex marriage nationwide with a mix of love, concern, and warnings of civil disobedience. Many leaders believe the ruling will restrict the role of people of faith in American society and put religious liberty at risk. Some, like Mike Huckabee, have strongly criticized the decision, calling it "unconstitutional judicial tyranny." Others, like Rev. Albert Mohler, are urging Christians to speak the truth in love and love in truth. The Alliance Defending Freedom and the Southern Baptist Convention have partnered to provide guidance for churches and faith-based institutions on protecting their ministries. Some leaders, like Mat Staver, are already calling for civil disobedience in response to the ruling. Overall, Christian leaders are concerned about the implications of the decision on religious freedom and are considering their next steps.

END OF SUMMARY.
