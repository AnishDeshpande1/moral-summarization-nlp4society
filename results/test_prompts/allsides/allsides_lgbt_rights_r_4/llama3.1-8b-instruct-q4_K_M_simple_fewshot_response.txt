SUMMARY:

Christian leaders have responded with concern, warnings of civil disobedience, and calls for love and truth in the aftermath of the Supreme Court's decision on marriage. They believe that the ruling recognizing gay marriage will restrict religious liberty and put people of faith at risk. Many leaders are warning that the court has stripped Americans of their freedom to debate and decide marriage policy through the democratic process. The Southern Baptist Convention, Alliance Defending Freedom, and others have partnered to provide guidance for churches and faith-based institutions on how to respond. Leaders such as Rev. Samuel Rodriguez and Liberty Counsel's Mat Staver have called for civil disobedience in response to the ruling. Despite these warnings, Rev. Albert Mohler urged Christians to speak the truth in love and speak love in truth, emphasizing that love of neighbor means speaking the truth about marriage.

END OF SUMMARY.
