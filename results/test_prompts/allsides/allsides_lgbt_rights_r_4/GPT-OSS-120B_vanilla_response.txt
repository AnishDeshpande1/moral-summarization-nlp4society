SUMMARY: Christian leaders reacted to the Supreme Court’s historic ruling that same‑sex couples have a constitutional right to marry nationwide. While some, like former governor Mike Huckabee and activist Bob Vander Plaats, denounced the decision as judicial overreach, others emphasized a balanced response, urging believers to speak “truth in love.” Representatives from the Alliance Defending Freedom warned that the ruling undermines democratic debate on marriage and threatens religious liberty. The Southern Baptist Convention, in partnership with the Alliance, issued a “Protecting Your Ministry” guide for faith‑based entities, and leaders such as Rev. Albert Mohler and Rev. Samuel Rodriguez cautioned that the decision could marginalize those holding a biblical worldview. Liberty Counsel’s Mat Staver and other activists have called for civil disobedience, while many religious groups are evaluating how best to respond. END OF SUMMARY.
