SUMMARY:

Christian leaders are responding to the Supreme Court's decision to legalize same-sex marriage nationwide with concern, warnings of civil disobedience, and a call to stand up for what is right. Many believe the ruling will lead to a restricted role for people of faith in American society and put religious liberty at risk. Some, like Mike Huckabee, have denounced the decision as "unconstitutional judicial tyranny," while others, like Rev. Albert Mohler, urge Christians to speak the truth in love and love in truth. The ruling is seen as a threat to democracy, with Jim Campbell of the Alliance Defending Freedom stating that it strips Americans of their freedom to debate and decide marriage policy. Faith leaders are now considering their response, with some, like Mat Staver, already calling for civil disobedience. The moral framing of this story highlights the importance of standing up for what is right, even if it means resisting the law.

END OF SUMMARY.
