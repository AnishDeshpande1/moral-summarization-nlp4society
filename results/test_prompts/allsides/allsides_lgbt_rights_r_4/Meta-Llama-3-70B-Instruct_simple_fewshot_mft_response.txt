SUMMARY:

Christian leaders are responding with concern and warnings of civil disobedience to the Supreme Court's decision to legalize same-sex marriage nationwide. They argue that the ruling undermines religious liberty and the democratic process, stripping Americans of their freedom to debate and decide marriage policy. Many leaders believe the decision will lead to the marginalization of those who hold a biblical worldview and are calling for civil disobedience in response. Others are studying the ruling to determine the best ways to respond. The ruling is seen as an attack on traditional values and an overreach of judicial authority, with some calling it "unconstitutional judicial tyranny." Christian leaders are urging followers to speak the truth in love and to stand up for what is right, even if it means going against the law. The decision is seen as a threat to the freedom to democratically address social issues and a catalyst for the marginalization of people of faith.

END OF SUMMARY.
