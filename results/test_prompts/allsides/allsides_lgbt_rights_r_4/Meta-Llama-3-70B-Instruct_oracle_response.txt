SUMMARY:

Christian leaders are responding with love and concern to the Supreme Court's decision to legalize same-sex marriage nationwide, warning of civil disobedience and the marginalization of Americans who hold a biblical worldview. The ruling is seen as an "out-of-control act of unconstitutional judicial tyranny" that has "stripped" Americans of their freedom to debate and decide marriage policy through the democratic process. Many believe the court has "succumbed" to demands to "invent" a new constitutional right, and that religious liberty will be "put" at risk. Rev. Albert Mohler admonished followers to "speak the truth in love and to speak love in truth," emphasizing the importance of loving one's neighbor without lying about marriage. Others, like Mat Staver, have called for civil disobedience in response to the ruling, which "serves as a defacto and legal catalyst" for the marginalization of people of faith. END OF SUMMARY.
