**SUMMARY:**  
Christian leaders reacted to the Supreme Court’s historic decision granting a constitutional right to same‑sex marriage with a mix of love, concern and warnings. Figures such as Rev. Albert Mohler urged believers to speak truth in love, while others—Mike Huckabee and Bob Vander Plaats— condemned the ruling as an act of unconstitutional judicial tyranny. Legal experts from the Alliance Defending Freedom warned that the decision strips Americans of the freedom to debate marriage through democratic processes, threatening religious liberty. The Southern Baptist Convention and the Alliance released a “Protecting Your Ministry” guide, and Dr. Russell Moore and Rev. Samuel Rodriguez described the ruling as a catalyst for the marginalization of those holding a biblical worldview. Some, like Liberty Counsel’s Mat Staver, are already calling for civil disobedience. The discourse highlights a clash between the court’s expansion of liberty for LGBTQ + couples and the perceived erosion of religious freedom.
