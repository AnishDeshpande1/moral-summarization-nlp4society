SUMMARY:

The Supreme Court's decision on marriage, allowing same-sex couples to marry nationwide, has sparked reactions from Christian leaders. Many are expressing concern and warning of potential civil disobedience. Former Governor Mike Huckabee called the ruling "unconstitutional judicial tyranny," while Bob Vander Plaats described it as a court succumbing to demands for a new constitutional right not envisioned by the Founders.

Christian leaders believe the ruling will restrict their role in American society and put religious liberty at risk. They argue that the freedom to debate and decide marriage policy through the democratic process has been stripped away. The Southern Baptist Convention partnered with the Alliance Defending Freedom to provide guidance for churches on how to protect themselves from potential marginalization.

Leaders such as Liberty Counsel's Mat Staver have already called for civil disobedience in response to the ruling, while others are studying it to determine the best course of action. Christian leaders emphasize the importance of speaking truth and love in the face of this decision.

END OF SUMMARY
