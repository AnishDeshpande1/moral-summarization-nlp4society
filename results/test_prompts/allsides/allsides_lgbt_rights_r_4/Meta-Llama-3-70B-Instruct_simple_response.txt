SUMMARY:

Christian leaders are responding to the Supreme Court's decision to legalize same-sex marriage nationwide with concern, warnings of civil disobedience, and a call to speak the truth in love. Many believe the ruling will restrict religious liberty and marginalize Americans who hold a biblical worldview. The decision is seen as an overreach of judicial power, stripping Americans of their freedom to debate and decide marriage policy through the democratic process. Leaders such as Mike Huckabee, Bob Vander Plaats, and Rev. Albert Mohler are speaking out against the ruling, with some calling for civil disobedience. The Alliance Defending Freedom and the Southern Baptist Convention have provided guidance for churches and faith-based institutions to protect their ministries. The ruling is seen as a threat to religious liberty and a catalyst for the marginalization of those who hold a biblical worldview.

END OF SUMMARY.
