**SUMMARY:**

The Supreme Court's historic decision on marriage has sparked a mix of reactions from Christian leaders, ranging from warnings of civil disobedience to calls for speaking the truth in love. Many believe that the ruling will lead to a restricted role for people of faith in American society and pose a threat to their freedom and liberty. Rev. Albert Mohler urged followers to speak the truth in love, emphasizing that "love of neighbor means we cannot lie about marriage." Others, like Liberty Counsel's Mat Staver, have called for civil disobedience in response to the ruling. The decision is seen as a catalyst for the marginalization of Americans who hold a biblical worldview. Christian leaders are warning of an out-of-control act of judicial tyranny and the potential erosion of traditional marriage definitions.
