**SUMMARY:**  
Reddit announced the removal of more than 2,000 subreddits that repeatedly violated its content policy by hosting hate speech and other rule‑breaking material. The purge targets communities such as the pro‑Trump “The_Donald,” the TERF‑aligned “Gender Critical,” and the radical left “CapoTrapHouse,” all cited for antagonizing other users and promoting extremist, hateful rhetoric—including white‑genocide conspiracies and trans‑phobic discourse. Reddit says the action is part of a broader effort to protect civil‑rights‑focused protests for gender, racial, and LGBTQ+ equality and to prevent the platform from being weaponized against vulnerable groups. The company stresses that it will continue to craft stricter rules to mitigate hate and uphold a safer, more inclusive environment.
