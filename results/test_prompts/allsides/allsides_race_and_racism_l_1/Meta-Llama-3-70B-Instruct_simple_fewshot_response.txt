SUMMARY:

A recent spate of anti-Semitic incidents involving celebrities like Ice Cube, DeSean Jackson, and Chelsea Handler has sparked concern about the growing apathy towards social justice. These incidents, which included tweets and posts perpetuating harmful stereotypes about Jews, were met with a muted response, perpetuating racism and contributing to an "Apatholypse." The lack of outrage is troubling, especially from those who claim to advocate for social justice. The incidents highlight the illogic of discrimination, where one group is singled out and blamed for societal ills. The apologies offered by some of the perpetrators were half-hearted and failed to acknowledge the harm caused. The article argues that until we learn to condemn injustice against all groups, we will never achieve true social justice. As Martin Luther King Jr. said, "Injustice anywhere is a threat to justice everywhere." We must recognize that no one is free until everyone is free.

END OF SUMMARY.
