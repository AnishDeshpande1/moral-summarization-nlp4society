SUMMARY:

The recent surge in anti-Semitic tweets and posts from sports and entertainment celebrities, including Ice Cube, DeSean Jackson, and Chelsea Handler, is a troubling sign of the "Apatholypse" - a growing apathy towards social justice. The muted response to these incidents perpetuates racism and contributes to a culture of discrimination. The author argues that the lack of outrage is particularly disturbing given the New Woke-fulness in Hollywood and the sports world, where one would expect more passionate public condemnation. The article highlights the illogical and harmful nature of scapegoating certain groups, citing the Nazis, KKK, and other oppressive groups that have used similar logic to justify violence and discrimination. The author concludes that true social justice requires outrage and action against all forms of discrimination, not just those that affect one's own group.

END OF SUMMARY.
