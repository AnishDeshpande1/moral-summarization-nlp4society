SUMMARY:

A recent surge in anti-Semitic tweets and posts from sports and entertainment celebrities, including Ice Cube, DeSean Jackson, and Chelsea Handler, has sparked concern about the perpetuation of racism. Despite the lack of widespread outrage, these incidents have contributed to an "Apatholypse" - a growing apathy towards social justice. The celebrities' statements, which implied that Jews are responsible for the oppression of blacks, were met with a muted response, with some even defending their views. The article argues that this lack of pushback perpetuates racism and discrimination against other groups. It also notes that some of the celebrities have apologized, but their responses were often arrogant and irrational, confirming people's worst opinions. The article concludes that in order to truly fight for social justice, we must be outraged by injustice against anyone, regardless of their race, religion, or identity.
