SUMMARY:

President Trump has sparked outrage after telling four minority Democratic congresswomen to "go back" to their countries of origin, despite three of them being born in the United States. Trump denied that his comments were racist, claiming that if someone hates America, they can leave. House Speaker Nancy Pelosi called on lawmakers to pass a resolution condemning Trump's remarks, which she described as "xenophobic." The targeted congresswomen, known as "the Squad," responded with outrage, with one member calling Trump's comments "what racism looks like." The tweets have drawn international criticism, with British Prime Minister Theresa May calling the language "completely unacceptable." Some Republicans have also spoken out against Trump's attacks, with one congressman calling the tweets "racist and xenophobic." The incident has highlighted Trump's divisive rhetoric and sparked concerns about racism and xenophobia.
