SUMMARY:

In his resignation speech, Senator Al Franken failed to take responsibility for his actions and instead offered a masterclass in how not to apologize for sexual misconduct. Despite multiple women accusing him of groping or sexual harassment, Franken did not acknowledge the harm he caused, admit his role in it, or express remorse. Instead, he focused on his own struggles, denied some allegations, and seemed to take back his previous apologies. This lack of accountability is a stark reminder of the importance of holding public figures accountable for their actions, especially when it comes to the exploitation and abuse of women. Franken's actions are a betrayal of the women who came forward and a disservice to the cause of protecting women from men's abuses of power. His failure to take responsibility will only serve to harm the very cause he claims to care about.
