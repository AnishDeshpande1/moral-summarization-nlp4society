SUMMARY: Attorney General Eric Holder announced that he has **assigned** two U.S. attorneys—Ronald C. Machen Jr. and Rod J. Rosenstein—to **lead criminal investigations** into recent **leaks** of classified national‑security information, including a secret terrorist **kill list** and details of an intelligence operation against al‑Qaida. Holder emphasized that any **unauthorized disclosures** will be **prosecuted**, stating that **leaking** such material **compromises** the security of the United States and will not be **tolerated**. President Obama rejected accusations that the White House **released** classified data for political **gain**, calling the notion **offensive** and **wrong**. In Congress, Republicans such as Sen. John McCain and Sen. Saxby Chambliss demanded a **special counsel** and swift **inquiries**, while Sen. Dianne Feinstein considered **legislation** to expand **authority** for inspectors‑general. Holder’s **authorized** prosecutors are directed to **investigate** all leads across the Executive and Legislative branches and to **prosecute** any **violations** uncovered, aiming to prevent further **breaches** of national security. END OF SUMMARY.
