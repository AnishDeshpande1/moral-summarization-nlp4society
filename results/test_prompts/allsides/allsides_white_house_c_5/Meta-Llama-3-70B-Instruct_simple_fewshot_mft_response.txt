SUMMARY:

Attorney General Eric Holder has assigned two prosecutors to investigate possible leaks of classified information, following calls for an inquiry into how sensitive national security details were leaked to the media. The investigation aims to uncover unauthorized disclosures of classified information, which can compromise national security and put Americans at risk. President Obama has denied allegations that the White House leaked classified material for political gain, calling the notion "offensive" and "wrong." The Congressional Intelligence Committee has also expressed concern over the leaks, with some members calling for a special counsel to investigate. The investigation is crucial to uphold the authority of the government and prevent harm to national security. It is also essential to ensure fairness and accountability in the handling of classified information, and to prevent cheating or exploitation of sensitive data for political purposes.

END OF SUMMARY.
