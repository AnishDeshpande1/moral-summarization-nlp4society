SUMMARY:

A 31-year-old woman, Kate Steinle, was fatally shot on San Francisco's pier by Juan Francisco Lopez-Sanchez, a Mexican immigrant who had been deported five times and was in the country without authorization. Lopez-Sanchez has seven felony convictions, including four for drug offenses. Despite this incident, research shows that immigration does not lead to increased crime rates, and most undocumented immigrants come to the US to work and provide for their families. The article argues that deporting all undocumented immigrants is not the solution and that our country needs smarter and better immigration enforcement. Immigration authorities have been criticized for wasting time and resources chasing after productive members of society while felons like Lopez-Sanchez slip through the cracks. The Department of Homeland Security has announced a new plan to focus on recent arrivals, serious criminals, and terrorism threats.

END OF SUMMARY.
