SUMMARY:
Planned Parenthood and the ACLU have sued Alabama over its near-total abortion ban, which is deemed unconstitutionally severe. The law has no exceptions for rape or incest and could lead to abortion providers spending up to 99 years in prison. The ban is seen as a blatant attempt to restrict abortion care and exploit people's health and lives for political gain. The lawsuit argues that the law will disproportionately harm black and low-income people, who make up a significant portion of those seeking abortions in the state. The ACLU and Planned Parenthood are fighting against this restrictive and medically unnecessary law, which they believe is a clear attempt to overturn Roe v. Wade. The lawsuit is the first step in a long legal battle over abortion rights.
