SUMMARY:
Planned Parenthood and the ACLU sued Alabama on Friday over the state’s near‑total abortion **ban**, a law that Republican lawmakers **engineered** to **push** an **unconstitutional** agenda and **use** the case to try to **overturn** Roe v. Wade. The statute permits abortion only for a “serious **health risk**” to the mother, with **no exceptions** for **rape** or **incest**, and threatens providers with up to 99 years in prison. ACLU attorney Alexa Kolbi‑Molinas called the measure **politically‑motivated**, **unnecessary**, and a **tailored** exception so narrow it is **impossible** to meet, arguing the **ban** will **harm** black and low‑income women who already bear the brunt of Alabama’s **restrictions**. Planned Parenthood’s Leana Wen said the organization has **no choice** but to **fight** in courts, legislatures and ballot boxes, warning that anti‑abortion activists are **emboldened** and **exploit** women’s health for political **gain**. The lawsuit challenges the law’s **restrictive** scope and seeks to halt its enforcement while similar **restrictions** sweep across other states.
