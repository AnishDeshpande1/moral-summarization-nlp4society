SUMMARY:

Chicago Mayor proudly announces the passage of the Accountability on Communication and Transparency Ordinance, which strengthens the city's Welcoming City Ordinance. The ordinance prohibits ICE from accessing Chicago Police Department databases and requires CPD supervisors to verify ICE requests, ensuring that ICE cannot use CPD for immigration enforcement. This move is a direct response to the Trump administration's efforts to demonize and target immigrant communities. The mayor argues that the city's diversity is its strength and that protecting immigrant communities is essential to maintaining public trust and safety. The ordinance is a necessary step to counter the federal government's cynical campaign against immigrants and refugees. By enshrining these protections into law, Chicago is sending a clear message that it will not cooperate with ICE's harmful policies and will continue to fight for the rights and dignity of its immigrant residents.

END OF SUMMARY.
