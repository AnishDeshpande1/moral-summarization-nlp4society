SUMMARY:

As part of our commitment to protecting Chicago's immigrant and refugee communities, we have passed the Accountability on Communication and Transparency Ordinance, which bolsters our Welcoming City Ordinance by prohibiting ICE from accessing CPD databases and turning to CPD for assistance with immigration enforcement operations. We will also establish reporting requirements to ensure transparency and provide legal guidance to public facilities on how to protect immigrant patrons in case of targeted raids. Our actions are designed to safeguard the rights of undocumented immigrants, who make up a vital part of our city's fabric. By standing firm against the current administration's politized campaign to demonize immigrants, we aim to create a safe and welcoming environment for all residents. We believe that immigration policy should be based on inclusive principles, such as providing clear pathways to citizenship, rather than fear and arrests. Our measures will help ensure that our communities remain whole and protected from division and demonization.

END OF SUMMARY.
