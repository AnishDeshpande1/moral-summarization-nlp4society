SUMMARY:
Jim Obergefell, the plaintiff in the landmark Obergefell v. Hodges case, anxiously awaits a new Supreme Court ruling on employment discrimination against LGBTQ+ individuals. Despite the freedom to marry nationwide, discrimination remains a threat, and Obergefell has heard countless stories of LGBTQ+ people facing unfair treatment in the workplace. A win in these cases would be a significant step towards justice, upholding the view that nearly 70% of Americans hold: LGBTQ+ individuals should be legally protected from discrimination. However, even with a landmark victory, the fight for equal rights is far from over. Comprehensive protections are needed in every area of life, including housing, health facilities, and public spaces, to ensure that everyone is treated with dignity and respect. Obergefell urges lawmakers to pass clear and comprehensive protections, and for individuals to continue pushing forward for full equality and dignity for all. END OF SUMMARY.
