SUMMARY:  
The author accuses the Obama White House of suffering from the “’60s disease,” a cultural rot that has left its senior staff spoiled, greedy and contemptuous of America’s institutions. He claims the boomers who run the administration learned to avoid responsibility, to peddle lies and evasions, and to treat the nation’s uniformed forces with contempt. Hillary Clinton is portrayed as having treated her security detail like servants and, after “dodging” the draft, showed a non‑chalance that borders on betrayal. Obama’s past associations with Bill Ayers and Marxist mentors are used to argue that the president has been crafting hoaxes, disinformation and fakery to cover up the Benghazi tragedy. The author says the administration’s changing story is full of holes, plugged with flimflam and honeyfuggle, and that the “misleading” tale of a video‑provoked riot was a prevarication designed for political gain through media collusion and indifference. He condemns the sin of indifference to the fighting and sacrifice of the rescue teams, calling the officials’ willingness to listen to the pleas of soldiers a desperate failure, and demands accountability for the betrayal and sins of the White House.  
END OF SUMMARY.
