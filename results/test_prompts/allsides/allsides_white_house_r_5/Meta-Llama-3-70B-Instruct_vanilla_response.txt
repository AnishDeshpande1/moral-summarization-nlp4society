SUMMARY:

The article criticizes the Obama White House, particularly the president and his top aides, including Hillary Clinton, for their handling of the Benghazi tragedy. The author argues that the administration's lies, evasions, and prevarications about the event are a result of their "60s disease," a mentality shaped by the decade's cultural rot. The article claims that Obama and Clinton have a contempt for the military and institutions, which led to their betrayal of trust in Benghazi. It reveals that rescue teams were available but not sent, and that the administration's changing versions of the event are full of holes. The article concludes that the administration's lies will continue to unravel and that Hillary Clinton must answer for her cold indifference to the plight of those fighting for their lives in Benghazi.
