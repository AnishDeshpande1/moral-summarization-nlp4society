SUMMARY:
The San Francisco Board of Supervisors unanimously adopted a resolution branding the National Rifle Association a “domestic terrorist organization,” condemning the group’s propaganda that obscures the deadly harm of gun violence. By labeling the NRA as “absolutely disgraceful” and linking it to an “epidemic of gun violence” that kills roughly 100 Americans each day, the board asserts moral authority over an organization it views as subverting public safety and cheating the nation out of lives. The resolution calls for a thorough review of city vendors and contractors to sever any ties with the NRA, urging the city—and potentially other jurisdictions—to refuse business with the group and its affiliates. Supporters argue this action restores fairness by holding the NRA accountable for its opposition to gun‑control measures that they say blocks lifesaving policies. The NRA denounced the measure as a “worthless and disgusting sound‑bite remedy” and an assault on law‑abiding citizens’ freedoms. END OF SUMMARY.
