SUMMARY:

Donald Trump rallied his supporters in Pennsylvania on Monday night, ignoring the controversy surrounding his leaked audio tape and Republican defections. He repeated his threat to appoint a special prosecutor to investigate Hillary Clinton's alleged crimes, prompting chants of "Lock her up!" from the crowd. However, Trump also made several mistakes, including praising WikiLeaks and incorrectly citing an email from Sidney Blumenthal that was actually quoted in a Newsweek article. This botched attack on Clinton could backfire for Trump, given his already-tarnished reputation on Russian ties.

Meanwhile, Republican defections continue to mount, with Speaker of the House Paul Ryan saying he will neither defend nor campaign with Trump. New polls show Clinton leading Trump by 11 points nationally and solidifying her lead in key swing states. The controversy surrounding Trump's leaked audio tape has left his campaign in trouble, despite his attempts to fire up his base with familiar campaign promises and braggadocio.
