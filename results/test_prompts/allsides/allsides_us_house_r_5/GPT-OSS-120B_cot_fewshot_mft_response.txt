Republicans confronted House Intelligence Committee Chairman Adam Schiff, accusing him of abusing his authority and promoting a false, conspiratorial narrative that they say undermines faith in government institutions. In a sharply worded letter, GOP members declared they have no faith in Schiff’s ability to discharge his duties, labeling his continued claims of collusion as immoral, unethical, unpatriotic and corrupt.
