Republicans confronted House Intelligence Committee Chairman Rep. Adam Schiff, demanding his resignation and accusing him of **abusing** his position, **misrepresenting** intelligence, and promoting a **demonstrably false narrative** about Russian‑Trump collusion. Schiff defended his record, citing contacts between Trump associates and foreign actors, and called the interactions “**immoral**, **unethical**, **unpatriotic**, and **corrupt**.” He warned the President and his defenders that they may think such behavior is acceptable, but he does not. House Speaker Nancy Pelosi backed Schiff, dismissing the GOP critics as **“scaredy‑cats.”** Republicans, invoking the Mueller report’s conclusion that no conspiracy was proven, labeled Schiff’s continued claims as **false** and **alarming**, likening his tactics to **McCarthyism**. The dispute centers on whether any Trump‑related activity was **compromised** by hostile foreign powers.
