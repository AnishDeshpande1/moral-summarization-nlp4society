SUMMARY:

Republicans confronted House Intelligence Committee Chairman Adam Schiff, urging him to resign due to his "incompatible" actions and "unethical" behavior. They claimed he "abused" his position and "damaged" the committee's integrity by promoting a "false narrative" of Trump-Russia collusion. Schiff defended himself, listing Trump associates' "immoral" and "unpatriotic" interactions with Russians, but failed to provide evidence of coordination in Russian election interference. Republicans argued that Schiff's continued investigation is a "corrupt" attempt to undermine faith in US government institutions. They likened Schiff to Sen. Joseph McCarthy, accusing him of "misrepresented" information and "chasing" after Russian collusion. Schiff tweeted that he finds Trump's actions "immoral," "unethical," and "corrupt." House Speaker Nancy Pelosi defended Schiff, calling Republicans "scaredy-cats." The dispute highlights the deepening partisan divide over the Russia probe.

END OF SUMMARY.
