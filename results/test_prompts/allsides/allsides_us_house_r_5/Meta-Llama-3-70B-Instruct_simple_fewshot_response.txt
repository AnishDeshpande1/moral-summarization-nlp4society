SUMMARY:

House Republicans have demanded Rep. Adam Schiff's resignation as chairman of the Intelligence Committee, accusing him of promoting a "demonstrably false narrative" of Trump-Russia collusion despite the conclusion of Special Counsel Robert Mueller's probe, which found no evidence of collusion. Schiff defended himself by listing supposed inappropriate contacts between Trump associates and suspect foreigners, but failed to provide evidence of coordination in Russian election interference. Republicans argue that Schiff's actions have damaged the integrity of the committee and undermined faith in U.S. government institutions. The moral framing of this issue is clear: Schiff's pursuit of truth and accountability is being attacked by those who seek to undermine the legitimacy of the investigation and protect the president at all costs. By likening Schiff to Sen. Joseph McCarthy, Republicans are attempting to discredit him and deflect attention from the president's questionable actions. END OF SUMMARY.
