SUMMARY:

Project Veritas released an undercover video depicting campaign organizer Kyle Jurek expressing support for ideological reeducation of Trump supporters, comparison to Nazis, praise for gulags, prediction of police riots during the 2020 Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee, and encouragement of violent protests against Trump's re-election.

Jurek allegedly works for Sanders' campaign and was previously employed by the Democratic Senatorial Campaign Committee. His comments and actions were criticized by both Republicans and Democrats as potentially inciting violence and promoting extreme ideologies.

END OF SUMMARY.
