SUMMARY: A hidden‑camera video released by Project Veritas shows Kyle Jurek, a paid field organizer for Senator Bernie Sanders, bragging that “cities burn” if President Trump wins reelection and predicting that police will be **beaten** in **riots** at the 2020 Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee. Jurek likens Trump supporters to **Nazis**, calls for their **re‑education** in Soviet‑style **gulags**, and says the violence will make 1968 look like a “Girl Scout cookout.” He boasts that “the cops are going to be the ones that are getting **beaten**,” urges the National Guard to intervene, and threatens to **drag** MSNBC staff “by their hair, and **light** them on fire.” The video also shows him praising **Antifa**, comparing Trump voters to **Nazified** Germans, and claiming that free tuition is a tool to prevent future **Nazis**. Project Veritas presented FEC records showing Jurek earned about $11,000 from the Sanders campaign and previously worked for the Democratic Senatorial Campaign Committee. Critics accuse Project Veritas of deceptive **editing**, a charge its founder James O’Keefe rejects. The Sanders campaign has not responded.  

END OF SUMMARY.
