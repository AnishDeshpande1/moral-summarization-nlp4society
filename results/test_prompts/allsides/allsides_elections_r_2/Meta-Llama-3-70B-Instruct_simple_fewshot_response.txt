SUMMARY:

A Project Veritas video shows a Sanders campaign organizer, Kyle Jurek, predicting violence and chaos if President Trump is reelected. Jurek says "cities burn" and that police will be "beaten" at the Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee. He also expresses support for reeducating Trump voters, comparing them to Nazis, and praises Soviet-style gulags. Jurek aligns himself with antifa, saying that Trump supporters are "scared senseless of anti-fascists" and that "the only thing that fascists understand is violence." The video raises questions about the true nature of the Sanders campaign and whether Jurek's views are representative of the campaign's ideology. The Sanders campaign has not commented on the video. Jurek's comments have sparked outrage and concern about the potential for violence and unrest in the 2020 election.

END OF SUMMARY.
