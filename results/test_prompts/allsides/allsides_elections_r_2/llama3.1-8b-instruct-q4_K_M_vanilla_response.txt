SUMMARY:

A Project Veritas video released on Tuesday shows Kyle Jurek, identified as a campaign organizer for Sen. Bernie Sanders, making inflammatory comments about the potential consequences of President Trump's re-election. Jurek predicts "cities burn" and claims that police will be beaten in riots at the 2020 Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee. He also expresses support for ideological reeducation of Trump voters, comparing them to Nazis, and praises Soviet-style gulags as a means of breaking down privilege.

Jurek's comments suggest a deep-seated hostility towards Trump supporters and law enforcement, and he aligns himself with antifa. The video also shows him taking shots at less radical liberals and media outlets, including MSNBC. Project Veritas President James O'Keefe released the video as part of his #Expose2020 series, which has been accused by critics of deceptively editing undercover videos.

END OF SUMMARY.
