SUMMARY:
A hidden‑camera video released by Project Veritas shows a self‑described Bernie Sanders field organizer, Kyle Jurek, openly calling for “cities burn” if President Trump wins re‑election and bragging that police would be “beaten” at the 2020 Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee. Jurek likens Trump supporters to Nazis, praises Soviet‑style gulags as a tool for “re‑education,” and urges violent actions against media and anti‑fascist opponents. The footage, accompanied by FEC data confirming Jurek’s six‑month, $11,000 stint on the Sanders campaign and a brief 2018 role with the Democratic Senatorial Campaign Committee, raises questions about whether he represents a broader extremist current within the campaign or is an isolated “lone wolf.” The Sanders campaign has not responded. Project Veritas, despite accusations of deceptive editing, presents the video as evidence of radical, violent rhetoric being cultivated by a major Democratic presidential hopeful’s operation.  

END OF SUMMARY.
