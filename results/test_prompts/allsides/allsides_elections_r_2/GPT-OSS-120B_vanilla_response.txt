SUMMARY: A Project Veritas undercover video shows Kyle Jurek, identified as a field organizer for Senator Bernie Sanders’ campaign, saying “cities burn” if President Trump is re‑elected and predicting violent riots against police at the 2020 Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee. Jurek also calls for “re‑education” of Trump supporters, likening them to Nazis, praises Soviet‑style gulags, and makes extremist statements about attacking media and antifa. The video includes FEC data indicating Jurek was paid about $11,000 for six months of work on the Sanders campaign and briefly worked for the Democratic Senatorial Campaign Committee. Project Veritas questions whether Jurek represents the broader campaign, but the Sanders campaign did not respond. The organization, known for disputed editing practices, released the clip as part of its #Expose2020 series.

END OF SUMMARY.
