SUMMARY:

A Project Veritas video exposes a Sanders campaign organizer, Kyle Jurek, who predicts violent riots and police beatings if Trump wins reelection. Jurek's remarks subvert authority, promoting harm and chaos, and disregard for the rule of law. He expresses support for reeducating Trump voters, comparing them to Nazis, and praises Soviet-style gulags. His words incite violence, encouraging riots and harm to police officers. The video raises concerns about the Sanders campaign's true values and whether Jurek's views are representative of the campaign's ideology. The campaign's silence on the matter is troubling, and the video's release sparks questions about the campaign's commitment to fairness and non-violence. Jurek's rhetoric is alarming, and his presence in the campaign raises concerns about the potential for harm and subversion of democratic values.

END OF SUMMARY.
