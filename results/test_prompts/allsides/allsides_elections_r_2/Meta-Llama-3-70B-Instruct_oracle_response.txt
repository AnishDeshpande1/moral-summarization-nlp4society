SUMMARY:

A Project Veritas video has exposed a campaign organizer for Democratic presidential hopeful Sen. Bernard Sanders, Kyle Jurek, predicting "cities burn" and violence against police if President Trump wins reelection. Jurek expressed support for ideological reeducation of Trump voters, comparing them to Nazis, and praised Soviet-style gulags. He predicted police would be "beaten" in riots at the 2020 Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee, saying "We're going to make 1978 look like a f–ing Girl Scout f–ing cookout." Jurek also aligned himself with antifa, saying Trump supporters are "scared f–ing senseless of anti-fascists" and that "the only thing that fascists understand is violence." He even suggested dragging liberal media personnel out by their hair and setting them on fire in the streets if they "take Bernie from us." The video raises questions about the true nature of the Sanders campaign and whether Jurek's views are representative of the campaign's ideology.

END OF SUMMARY.
