SUMMARY:

A Project Veritas video released Tuesday showed a campaign organizer for Democratic presidential hopeful Sen. Bernie Sanders, Kyle Jurek, making disturbing statements about violence and reeducation. Jurek predicted that "cities burn" if President Trump wins reelection and expressed support for ideological reeducation of Trump voters, comparing them to Nazis. He also praised Soviet-style gulags as a means of breaking down privilege and superiority. The video showed Jurek calling for police to be beaten at the 2020 Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee and saying that such violence would spread to other cities. Jurek's statements were endorsed by Sanders' campaign, which has employed him as a top-tier organizer earning over $11,000. The Sanders campaign has been criticized for its handling of Jurek's comments, with Project Veritas President James O'Keefe asking if Jurek is the "true but hidden face" of the Sanders campaign.
