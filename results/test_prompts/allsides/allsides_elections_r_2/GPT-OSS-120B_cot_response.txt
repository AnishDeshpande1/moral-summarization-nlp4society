**SUMMARY:**  
Project Veritas released undercover footage of Kyle Jurek, a field organizer for Senator Bernie Sanders’ campaign, in which he boasts that “cities burn” if President Trump is re‑elected and predicts violent riots against police at the 2020 Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee. Jurek calls Trump supporters “Nazis,” urges their “re‑education” in Soviet‑style gulags, and praises antifa‑style militancy, claiming the police will be “beaten” and that “the cops are going to be the ones that are getting f‑ing beaten.” He links Sanders’ free‑education platform to a “re‑education” program to prevent a resurgence of fascist or extremist ideology. The video also shows Jurek threatening media and liberal opponents with violent attacks. Project Veritas presents the clip as evidence of extremist rhetoric within the Sanders campaign, while the campaign has not responded. Critics note the organization’s history of disputed editing.
