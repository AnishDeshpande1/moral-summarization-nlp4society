SUMMARY:

A Project Veritas video shows a Sanders campaign organizer, Kyle Jurek, making inflammatory statements about what would happen if President Trump wins reelection. Jurek predicts "cities burn" and violence against police at the 2020 Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee. He also expresses support for reeducating Trump voters, comparing them to Nazis, and praises Soviet-style gulags. Jurek aligns himself with antifa, saying that Trump supporters are scared of anti-fascists and that violence is the only thing they understand. He also takes shots at less radical liberals and media outlets, saying they should be dragged out and lit on fire. Jurek has worked for the Sanders campaign for six months, earning $11,000, and has been identified as a "top tier organizer" by the campaign's Iowa field director. The Sanders campaign has not responded to requests for comment.

END OF SUMMARY.
