**SUMMARY:**  
A Project Veritas video shows Kyle Jurek, identified as a Sanders campaign field organizer, openly calling for “cities burn” if President Trump wins reelection and predicting that police will be “beaten” in riots at the 2020 Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee. Jurek likens Trump supporters to Nazis and argues they must be “re‑educated,” praising Soviet‑style gulags as a tool for such re‑education. He describes the anticipated violence as a way to “break rocks” for the “working‑class” and to dismantle the “privilege” and “superior” attitudes of the wealthy. Aligning with anti‑fascists, Jurek says fascists only understand violence, and he threatens to “drag … out by their hair, and light … on fire” media critics. The video raises questions about whether Jurek is a “lone wolf” or reflects a broader extremist current within the Sanders campaign. The Sanders campaign has not responded.
