Summary:

Project Veritas released an undercover video depicting Democratic presidential hopeful Senator Bernie Sanders' campaign organizer Kyle Jurek advocating for violent protests, re-education, and comparison to Nazi practices. Jurek claimed that if Trump wins, cities will burn, police will be beaten, and the 2020 Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee will also be targeted. Jurek's comments align with Sanders' calls for free tuition and suggest a parallel between the current situation and Nazi Germany's past experiences with forced labor camps. The video highlights Sanders' campaign's reliance on volunteers and reflects broader concerns within the Democratic movement regarding the safety and rights of protesters.

END OF SUMMARY.
