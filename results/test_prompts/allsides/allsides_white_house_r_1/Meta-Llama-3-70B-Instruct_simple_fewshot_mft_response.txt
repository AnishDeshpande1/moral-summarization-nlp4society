The Trump administration's handling of leaks and classified information has sparked controversy, with concerns over the president's anger and actions to investigate and punish those responsible. The article also touches on the issue of White House staff leaking Oval Office gossip to the press, which raises concerns about accountability and transparency in government. The president's oath to preserve and protect the Constitution, including the First Amendment, is at odds with his anger towards the press. The article frames the issue as a struggle between accountability and transparency in government, and the need for a free press to hold those in power accountable.
