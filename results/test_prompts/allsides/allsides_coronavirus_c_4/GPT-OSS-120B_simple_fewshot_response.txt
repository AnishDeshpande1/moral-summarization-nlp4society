States are re‑imposing restrictions on bars and travel, while the EU has removed the U.S. from its “safe list.” The moral of the story is clear: leadership that ignores science and public‑health guidance endangers the whole country, and only a unified, science‑based response can protect lives and begin to heal the nation’s economic wounds. END OF SUMMARY.
