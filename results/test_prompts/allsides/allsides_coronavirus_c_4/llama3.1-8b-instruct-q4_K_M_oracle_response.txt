President Trump is facing renewed criticism for his handling of the pandemic, with his rival Joe Biden accusing him of historic mismanagement that has cost lives and inflicted more damage than necessary to the economy. The European Union has excluded Americans from its "safe list" due to the fresh rise in cases and hospitalizations. As hospitals in Texas and Arizona are filling up with COVID-19 patients, Dr. Fauci warned about the risks of Independence Day celebrations this weekend.
