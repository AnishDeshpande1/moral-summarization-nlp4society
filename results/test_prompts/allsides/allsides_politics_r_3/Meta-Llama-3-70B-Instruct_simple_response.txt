SUMMARY:

Harvard Law Professor Alan Dershowitz, a self-described liberal Democrat, strongly criticized congressional Democrats for their impeachment efforts against President Trump. He likened their actions to those of the Russian secret police under Stalin, accusing them of "making up crimes" to justify impeachment. Dershowitz argued that Democrats have fabricated crimes, such as "collusion" and "obstruction of Congress,” which are not actual crimes. He warned that this approach poses a danger to all Americans, as it undermines the rule of law and the Constitution. Dershowitz also criticized left-leaning media outlets, such as CNN and MSNBC, for their biased coverage and for silencing opposing views. He claimed that CNN has banned him from their airwaves because he tells the truth about the Constitution. Dershowitz's criticism highlights the importance of upholding the Constitution and the rule of law, rather than succumbing to partisan politics.

END OF SUMMARY.
