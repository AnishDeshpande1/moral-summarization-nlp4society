SUMMARY:

Harvard Law School professor Alan Dershowitz, a self-described liberal Democrat, criticized congressional Democrats for their efforts to impeach President Trump, likening them to the Russian secret police under Stalin's regime. Dershowitz argued that Democrats are "making up crimes" and "creating crimes out of nothing," citing the example of the whistleblower's identity being potentially leaked. He claimed that Democrats previously made up crimes such as "collusion" and "obstruction of Congress," which are not actual crimes under federal law. Dershowitz warned that this behavior poses a danger to all Americans, as it undermines the rule of law and the Constitution. He also criticized CNN and MSNBC for their biased coverage, claiming that they have banned him from their airwaves for presenting a liberal Democrat's perspective on the Constitution.
