SUMMARY:

Harvard Law School professor Alan Dershowitz, a self-described liberal Democrat, has criticized congressional Democrats for their efforts to impeach President Trump, comparing them to the Russian secret police under Stalin's regime. Dershowitz argues that Democrats are "making up crimes" and "creating crimes out of nothing" in their desperate attempt to impeach Trump. He specifically points out that there is no crime of collusion or obstruction of Congress, and that the Democrats are misusing the Constitution's system of checks and balances. Dershowitz warns that this approach is dangerous and undermines the rule of law, putting all Americans at risk. He also criticizes the liberal media, particularly CNN, for their biased treatment and banning him from their airwaves for speaking out against the impeachment efforts. Dershowitz's critique is rooted in concerns about authority, care, and fairness, highlighting the subversion of democratic norms and the harm caused by partisan manipulation of the law.

END OF SUMMARY.
