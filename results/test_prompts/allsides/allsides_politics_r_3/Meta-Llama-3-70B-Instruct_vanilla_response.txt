SUMMARY:

Harvard Law School professor Alan Dershowitz, a self-described liberal Democrat, criticized congressional Democrats for their efforts to impeach President Trump, comparing them to the Russian secret police under Stalin. Dershowitz argued that Democrats are "making up crimes" and creating a dangerous precedent. He cited examples of alleged crimes, such as collusion and obstruction of Congress, which he claims are not actual crimes. Dershowitz also criticized CNN and MSNBC for biased reporting, saying they are misleading viewers and refusing to allow him to appear on their networks because he presents a contrarian view. He warned that if Democrats are allowed to "create crimes out of nothing," it poses a threat to all Americans. Dershowitz, who has been critical of impeachment efforts, has been shunned by left-leaning media outlets and has become a regular guest on Fox News.

END OF SUMMARY.
