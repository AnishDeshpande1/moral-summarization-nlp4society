SUMMARY: Harvard Law professor Alan Dershowitz, a self‑identified liberal Democrat, sharply criticized congressional Democrats on John Catsimatidis’ radio show, likening their impeachment push against President Trump to the tactics of Soviet secret police under Stalin. He argued that Democrats are inventing crimes—first “collusion,” which he says isn’t a statutory offense, then “obstruction of Congress,” which he contends is a constitutional check, not a crime. Citing a Soviet anecdote about fabricating charges, Dershowitz warned that such behavior endangers all Americans. He also accused CNN and MSNBC of bias, noting that CNN has barred him from appearing, and lamented the ACLU’s silence. Despite his liberal background, Dershowitz has faced backlash from Democrats and reduced invitations from left‑leaning media, while appearing more frequently on Fox News. END OF SUMMARY.
