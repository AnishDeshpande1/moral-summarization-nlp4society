SUMMARY:

Harvard Law School professor Alan Dershowitz, a self-described liberal Democrat, strongly criticized congressional Democrats for their "efforts" to impeach President Trump, likening them to the Russian secret police under Stalin. Dershowitz argued that Democrats are "making up crimes" and "creating crimes out of nothing," citing the example of accusing someone of a crime for naming the whistleblower in the Ukrainian situation. He claimed that this is reminiscent of the Stalin-era KGB, where "they'll make up the crime." Dershowitz also criticized Democrats for previously "making up" crimes such as "collusion" and "obstruction of Congress," which he believes are not actual crimes. He warned that these actions put everyone "in danger" and lamented that the ACLU is "silent" on the matter. Dershowitz also accused CNN of "banned" him from their airwaves and "misleading" their viewers by only featuring guests who "parrot the party line."

END OF SUMMARY.
