**SUMMARY:**
More than 180 CEOs signed a full‑page “Don’t Ban Equality” ad in The New York Times to oppose restrictive abortion laws that they say conflict with their values and are bad for business. They argue that banning reproductive health care undermines women’s autonomy, empowerment, and human‑right dignity, and threatens a diverse, inclusive workforce and the well‑being of employees who keep companies thriving. Executives from firms such as The Body Shop, H&M, Glossier, Yelp, and Square stress that access to reproductive care is essential, pivotal, and a critical component of equality and economic success. The letter, organized with the ACLU, Planned Parenthood, NARAL and the Center for Reproductive Rights, calls on business leaders to respect, protect and fulfill women’s human‑rights and to use their influence for good social change.
