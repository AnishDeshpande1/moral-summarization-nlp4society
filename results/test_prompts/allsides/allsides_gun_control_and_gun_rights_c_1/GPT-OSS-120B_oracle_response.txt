SUMMARY: A roving gunman fired randomly from his vehicle through southwest Springfield before crashing into a Kum & Go convenience store, where he opened fire on customers and employees. Police Chief Paul Williams reported that the shooter killed five people—including a Springfield police officer, Officer Christopher Walsh, who died while rushing to rescue victims—and then died from a self‑inflicted shot. Two other officers were injured, and three civilians were killed: store employee Troy D. Rapp, waste‑company worker Shannon R. Perkins, and customer Matthew J. Hicks‑Morris. Officer Josiah Overton survived non‑life‑threatening injuries. Witnesses described the chaotic shots and the shock and sorrow felt by the community. The investigation continues as authorities grieve the loss of one of their own and honor the brave actions of the first responders. END OF SUMMARY.
