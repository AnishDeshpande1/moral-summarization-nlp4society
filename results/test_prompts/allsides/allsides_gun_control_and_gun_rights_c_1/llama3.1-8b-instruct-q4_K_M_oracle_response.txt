SUMMARY: A man fired randomly from his vehicle, killing five people, including a police officer and himself, in a southwest Missouri city on Sunday night. The gunman's motives remain unclear. Police received reports of "multiple shooting calls" throughout the city before he crashed into a convenience store and began shooting customers and employees. Two officers were shot as they arrived at the scene, but others pulled them to safety and then entered the store, finding three people dead. Officer Christopher Walsh, 32, was killed while trying to rescue a victim, described by his chief as "a hero" who died rushing in without regard for his own safety to protect members of his community. The police chief expressed shock and sorrow at the loss of life and praised the bravery of his officers. The investigation is ongoing, with multiple crime scenes still being investigated.

END OF SUMMARY.
