SUMMARY:

The American Civil Liberties Union (ACLU) has filed a lawsuit against seven East Texas towns that have declared themselves "sanctuary cities" for unborn children. The ACLU argues that these ordinances, which aim to protect unborn life, are unconstitutional and restrict the rights of pro-abortion groups to free expression and association. According to the lawsuit, the ordinances mislead residents about the legality of abortion services and label abortion providers as "criminal organizations," preventing them from operating in these cities. The ACLU claims that this violates the First Amendment and argues that cities cannot punish pro-abortion organizations for carrying out their work. Mark Dickson, director of Right to Life East Texas, has vowed to defend the ordinances, calling the lawsuit a "meritless" attempt to intimidate cities into reversing their decisions.
