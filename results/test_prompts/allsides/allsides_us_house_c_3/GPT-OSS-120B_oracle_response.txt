SUMMARY:  
The U.S. House Judiciary Committee subpoenaed former White House staff secretary Rob Porter to testify about President Donald Trump’s alleged **efforts** to **obstruct** the Mueller Russia **investigation**.  Porter is accused of **directing** and **ordering** White House Counsel Don McGahn to **fire** the special counsel and then **ask** him to **deny** it, even threatening to **get** rid of him if he refused.  The Mueller report says Trump called McGahn a “**lying** bastard” and **asked** Porter to stay in touch with former Associate Attorney General Rachel Brand to **find** a way to **end** the probe or replace Attorney General Jeff Sessions.  Democrats say “no one is above the law” and plan to use Porter’s testimony, along with that of former advisers Corey Lewandowski and Rick Dearborn, to decide whether to recommend impeachment.  Porter, who left the White House after **abuse** allegations, has not commented.  

END OF SUMMARY.
