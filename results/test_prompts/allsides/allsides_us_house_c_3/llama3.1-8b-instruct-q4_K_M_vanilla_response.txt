SUMMARY:
The US House Judiciary Committee has subpoenaed three former Trump advisers, including Rob Porter, to testify about alleged efforts by President Donald Trump to impede the federal probe into Russian interference in the 2016 presidential election. Porter is a former White House staff secretary who witnessed several episodes laid out in Robert Mueller's Russia investigation report, including an incident involving then-White House Counsel Don McGahn. Democrats want Porter and others to testify about Trump's alleged efforts to pressure Attorney General Jeff Sessions to direct Mueller's investigation away from the 2016 Trump campaign. The committee is seeking testimony as part of its investigation into whether to recommend Trump's impeachment. Lewandowski, a Trump confidant, has agreed to testify, while it is unclear whether the White House will seek to block the testimony of Porter and Dearborn.

END OF SUMMARY.
