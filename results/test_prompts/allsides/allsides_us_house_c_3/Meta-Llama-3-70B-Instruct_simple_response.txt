SUMMARY:

The US House Judiciary Committee has subpoenaed former White House aide Rob Porter to testify about President Trump's alleged efforts to impede the federal probe into Russian interference in the 2016 election. This is the third subpoena issued to a former Trump adviser in two weeks, with Corey Lewandowski and Rick Dearborn also set to testify. The committee aims to determine whether to recommend Trump's impeachment to the full House. Porter, a key witness in the Mueller report, is expected to testify about Trump's attempts to obstruct justice, including directing Don McGahn to fire the special counsel and then ordering him to lie about it. Democrats believe that any other American would have been prosecuted based on the evidence uncovered in the Mueller report. The committee's actions send a clear message: no one is above the law.

END OF SUMMARY.
