SUMMARY: NFL quarterback Drew Brees faced criticism from LGBTQ advocates after appearing in a video promoting “Bring Your Bible to School Day,” encouraging children to share their Christian faith. In the video, Brees cited a favorite scripture and urged kids to live out their faith. Critics, including Robert Littal of Black Sports Online and Deadspin, labeled the program and Brees as supporting conversion therapy and hateful agendas. Brees responded on Instagram, denying any affiliation with discriminatory groups, reiterating his personal credo of “love all, respect all, and accept all,” and stating he only promoted a non‑political, faith‑based initiative. He called the backlash a smear and asked that the matter be set aside. END OF SUMMARY.
