SUMMARY: NFL quarterback Drew Brees was attacked by LGBTQ activists after he promoted a “Bring Your Bible to School” program, urging children to publicly practice their Christian faith. In a promotional video, Brees cited a favorite Scripture and encouraged kids to share God’s love, emphasizing his personal creed to “love all, respect all, and accept all.” Critics on social media, including Robert Littal of Black Sports Online and Deadspin, labeled the program and Brees as part of a “gay‑conversion‑therapy” agenda, calling him a bigot and accusing him of supporting a hateful, discriminatory group. Brees responded in an Instagram video, insisting he was falsely smeared by a misleading article, denying any affiliation with groups that promote inequality, and reaffirming his support for religious expression and universal respect. He urged the controversy to end, stating his intention was simply to encourage kids to bring their Bibles to school, not to endorse discrimination. END OF SUMMARY.
