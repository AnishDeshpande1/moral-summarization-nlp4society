SUMMARY:

NFL quarterback Drew Brees faced backlash from the LGBT community for supporting a program that encourages children to bring their Bibles to school and live out their Christian faith publicly. In a video promotion, Brees shared his favorite Bible scripture and encouraged kids to share God's love with friends. However, allies of the LGBTQ agenda attacked Brees on social media, calling the program a "racist discriminatory religious cult" and accusing Brees of supporting "gay conversion therapy." Brees responded with a video on Instagram, clarifying that he was falsely smeared and reiterating his credo to "love all, respect all, and accept all." He denied supporting any groups that discriminate or promote inequality, emphasizing that his intention was to encourage kids to live out their faith. Despite the controversy, Brees remains committed to his Christian values and promoting a message of love and acceptance.

END OF SUMMARY.
