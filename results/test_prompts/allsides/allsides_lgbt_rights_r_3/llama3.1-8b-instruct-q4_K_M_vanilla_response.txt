SUMMARY:

NFL quarterback Drew Brees faced backlash from the LGBT lobby over his support of a program encouraging children to bring their Bibles to school on Bring Your Bible to School Day. In a video promotion, Brees spoke about one of his favorite Bible scriptures and encouraged kids to share God's love with friends. Allies of the LGBTQ agenda criticized Brees on social media, calling him a "bigot" and accusing him of supporting conversion therapy and discriminatory laws.

Brees responded to the online furor in an Instagram video, saying he was falsely smeared by a deceptive article and that he lives by the credo "love all, respect all, and accept all." He clarified that he does not support any groups that discriminate or promote inequality. Brees stated that his intention was simply to encourage kids to bring their Bibles to school on Bring Your Bible to School Day.

The controversy highlights the ongoing tensions between religious freedom and LGBTQ rights.

END OF SUMMARY.
