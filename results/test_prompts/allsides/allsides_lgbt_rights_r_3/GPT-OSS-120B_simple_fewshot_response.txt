SUMMARY:
NFL star quarterback Drew Brees was attacked online by LGBTQ‑rights activists after he appeared in a promotional video for “Bring Your Bible to School Day,” encouraging children to share their Christian faith. In the video Brees cited a favorite scripture and urged kids to “live out your faith” and “share God’s love.” Critics on social media and outlets such as Deadspin labeled the program a “gay‑conversion therapy” scheme and called Brees a bigot. Brees responded on Instagram, insisting he was mischaracterized, reaffirming his personal credo to “love all, respect all, and accept all,” and stating he does not support any group that discriminates or promotes inequality. He asked that the misunderstanding be cleared so the controversy can end. END OF SUMMARY.
