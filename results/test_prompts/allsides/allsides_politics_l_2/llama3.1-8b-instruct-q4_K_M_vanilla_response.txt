SUMMARY:
The Trump White House has released a list of Obama officials who may have received access to NSA reports that identified Michael Flynn's name in electronic surveillance conversations with Russian Ambassador Sergey Kislyak. The list includes 39 officials, including FBI Director James Comey, Treasury Secretary Jacob Lew, and Vice President Joe Biden. However, there is no evidence that Biden personally ordered Flynn's "unmasking." The note explains that each individual was an authorized recipient of the original report, but it's unclear who actually saw the unmasked information.

The list has been seized on by promoters of Trump's "Obamagate" claim, which alleges that Obama was involved in unfairly smearing Flynn. However, experts say that it would not be unusual for officials to request access to reports referencing an American involved in clandestine high-level contacts with foreign governments. Flynn had potentially compromising ties to foreign states, including Turkey and Russia, which may explain why the Obama officials given access to information by NSA on Flynn included US Ambassadors to Turkey and to Russia.

END OF SUMMARY.
