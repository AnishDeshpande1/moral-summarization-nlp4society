SUMMARY:

The Trump White House has released a list of Obama administration officials who may have had access to NSA reports identifying Michael Flynn, Trump's national security adviser, in secret conversations with Russian Ambassador Sergey Kislyak. The list, declassified at the request of acting Director of National Intelligence Richard Grenell, includes 39 officials, including FBI Director James Comey, Treasury Secretary Jacob Lew, and Vice President Joe Biden. However, the list has been misinterpreted by Trump supporters to bolster the false "Obamagate" claim that Obama unfairly targeted Flynn. In reality, the officials on the list were authorized recipients of the information and the unmasking was approved through normal protocol. The attempt to spin this as a scandal is a clear example of subverting authority and undermining fairness. It is a desperate attempt to deflect attention from Flynn's guilty plea and the Justice Department's efforts to drop the case, and to smear Obama administration officials instead. This kind of dishonesty and blame-shifting is a dangerous erosion of accountability and the rule of law.

END OF SUMMARY.
