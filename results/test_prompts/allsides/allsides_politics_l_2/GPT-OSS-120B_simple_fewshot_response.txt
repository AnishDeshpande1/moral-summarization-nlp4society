SUMMARY:
A declassified NSA list released by the Trump White House shows 39 Obama‑era officials—including James Comey, Jacob Lew, James Clapper, John Brennan, Denis McDonough and Vice President Joe Biden—who may have seen an “unmasked” report that identified Michael Flynn by name in intercepted communications with Russian ambassador Sergey Kislyak. The memo clarifies that all listed individuals were ordinary, authorized recipients of classified intelligence and that the unmasking followed standard protocol; it does **not** prove that any of them specifically requested the disclosure. Despite this, Trump allies have seized on the list to revive the debunked “Obamagate” narrative, falsely claiming Obama officials deliberately targeted Flynn to smear him. The claim ignores that Flynn pleaded guilty to lying to the FBI about his contacts and that the Justice Department, under Attorney General William Barr, is now seeking to drop the case. While Biden’s name appears on the authorized list, there is no evidence he ordered the unmasking. The moral framing highlights how political opponents weaponize routine intelligence procedures to spread misinformation and undermine legitimate investigations, betraying a duty to truth and responsible discourse.  
END OF SUMMARY.
