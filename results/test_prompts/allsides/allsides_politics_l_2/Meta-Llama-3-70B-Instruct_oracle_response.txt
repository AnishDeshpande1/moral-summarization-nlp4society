SUMMARY:

The Trump White House has released a list of Obama officials who may have received access to NSA reports identifying Michael Flynn, who attempted to influence Russia's reaction to sanctions. The list includes FBI Director James Comey, Treasury Secretary Jacob Lew, and Vice President Joe Biden, among others. However, promoters of Trump's "Obamagate" claim are misrepresenting the information to bolster their false narrative that Obama unfairly targeted Flynn. In reality, officials with regular access to classified information were authorized to hear about Flynn's suspect activities, which included lying to FBI agents about his conversations with Russian Ambassador Sergey Kislyak. Flynn's involvement in clandestine high-level contacts with foreign governments, including Russia and Turkey, raised red flags, and Obama officials were justified in reviewing reports referencing him. The release of this list is an attempt to seize on a false claim and smear Obama officials, but it only highlights Flynn's compromised position. END OF SUMMARY.
