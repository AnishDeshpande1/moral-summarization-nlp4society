SUMMARY:

Former Defense Secretary Jim Mattis has broken his silence to condemn President Trump's divisive leadership, citing the president's response to the George Floyd protests as the catalyst for his public criticism. In a statement, Mattis argues that Trump's actions have deliberately divided the American people, and that the country's unity is being threatened. Mattis, who resigned from the Trump administration in 2018, had previously refrained from criticizing the president, citing the need to maintain the apolitical nature of the military. However, he now believes that the American experiment is directly threatened by Trump's actions. Mattis specifically criticizes the president's decision to use troops to violate the Constitutional rights of citizens, and implicitly condemns the current secretary of defense and other senior officials for their role in militarizing the response to protests. He emphasizes the importance of unity and the need for civilian leaders to maintain public order, rather than relying on the military.
