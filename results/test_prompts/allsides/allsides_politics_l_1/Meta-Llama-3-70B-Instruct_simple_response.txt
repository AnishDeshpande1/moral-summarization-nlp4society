SUMMARY:

Former Defense Secretary James Mattis has broken his silence to condemn President Donald Trump's divisive leadership, which he believes is threatening the American experiment. Mattis argues that Trump's actions, particularly his response to the George Floyd protests, have deliberately divided the nation and undermined the Constitution. He criticizes the militarization of the response, which erodes the trust between the military and civilian society. Mattis calls for unity, drawing on the strengths of American civil society, and rejects the idea of using the military to dominate cities. He emphasizes that keeping public order is the responsibility of civilian leaders, not the military. Mattis's statement is a moral call to action, urging Americans to summon their unity and strength to overcome the current crisis.

END OF SUMMARY.
