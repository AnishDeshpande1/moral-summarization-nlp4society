SUMMARY:

A tragic shooting incident occurred at West Freeway Church of Christ in White Settlement, Texas, on Sunday, killing two people. A gunman opened fire, but two volunteer security team members drew their weapons and fatally shot him, saving an "untold number of lives." The Texas Department of Public Safety hailed their actions as "heroic." The shooting, which remained under investigation, occurred during a livestreamed service with over 240 parishioners in attendance. Licensed handgun owners can legally carry loaded weapons into Texas churches that do not have posted signs banning weapons. The incident highlights the importance of church security, which became a major issue in the state after a similar attack two years ago. Gov. Greg Abbott condemned the "evil act of violence" and praised the church members who acted quickly to prevent further loss of life.

END OF SUMMARY.
