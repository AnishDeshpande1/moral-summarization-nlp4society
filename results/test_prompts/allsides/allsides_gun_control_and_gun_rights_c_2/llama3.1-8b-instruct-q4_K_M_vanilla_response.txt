SUMMARY:

A shooting occurred at the West Freeway Church of Christ in White Settlement, Texas on Sunday, killing two people and injuring an unknown number. A gunman opened fire, but was quickly confronted by two volunteer security team members who drew their own guns and fatally shot him. The attack could have been much worse, as there were over 240 parishioners present at the time of the shooting. Authorities have not released the names of the victims or the gunman, who had roots in the area but was considered "relatively transient." Texas officials praised the actions of the security team members, calling them "heroic" and crediting them with saving untold number of lives. The incident has raised questions about church security in Texas, particularly after a similar attack in Sutherland Springs two years ago killed 26 people.

END OF SUMMARY.
