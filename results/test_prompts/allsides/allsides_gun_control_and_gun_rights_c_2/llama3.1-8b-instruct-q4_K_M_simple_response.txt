SUMMARY:

A shooting occurred at the West Freeway Church of Christ in North Texas on Sunday, resulting in the deaths of two people. However, thanks to the quick actions of two volunteer security team members who drew their guns and confronted the gunman, the attack was brought to an end within six seconds, saving "untold number" of lives. The shooter had pulled out a shotgun during the service, which was being livestreamed, but was fatally shot by the church members. Authorities have not released the names of the victims or the gunman, who is described as "relatively transient." The incident has been met with praise for the heroic actions of the security team and prayers for the community affected by this tragedy.

END OF SUMMARY.
