SUMMARY:

In a heroic display of bravery, two volunteer security team members at West Freeway Church of Christ in Texas drew their weapons and fatally shot a gunman who opened fire during a Sunday service, saving countless lives. The quick response, which took only six seconds, brought an end to the attack that could have been much worse. Two people were killed in the attack, and the gunman, who had roots in the area but was considered "relatively transient," was also killed. Authorities have not released information on a possible motive. The incident highlights the importance of responsible gun ownership and the effectiveness of Texas' gun laws. The governor and other officials have hailed the actions of the security team members as "heroic" and have called for prayers for the affected community. The attack is a tragic reminder of the need for vigilance and preparedness in places of worship.

END OF SUMMARY.
